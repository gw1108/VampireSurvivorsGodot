# Thorns {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

**Thorns** is one of the hidden [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It allows the player character to deal [retaliatory](Retaliate.md) damage to [enemies](Enemies.md) they come in contact with.

## Effects {#Effects}

Thorns starts with 0 points. For every 1 point of Thorns the player possesses, they will deal 10 damage to enemies they contact, much like the damage provided by  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md). Thorns is considered [retaliatory](Retaliate.md) damage.

- The player does not need a full point of Thorns to gain the effect; 0.5 points of Thorns will deal 5 damage to enemies upon contact.

Thorns is additive with other forms of contact damage, such as that from  [Stone Skull](Stone_Skull.md) .

## From Arcana {#From_Arcana}

-  [Edge of the Earth (VIII)](Edge_of_the_Earth_(VIII).md)} will grant the player +3 Thorns.
