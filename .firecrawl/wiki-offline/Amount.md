# Amount {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

</figure>

**Amount** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It determines how many extra projectiles most [weapons](Weapons.md) have.

The player has a base Amount of 0 before modifications. Bonuses to Amount are flat bonuses and thus stack additively.

The upper limit of Amount from [characters](Characters.md), [PowerUps](PowerUps.md) and [passive item](Passive_items.md) or weapon bonuses is 10.

For certain weapons, Amount may work differently. It affects:

- [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md) of  [Four Seasons](Four_Seasons.md) ,  [Godai Shuffle](Godai_Shuffle.md) ,  [Flash Arrow](Flash_Arrow.md) ,  [Millionaire](Millionaire.md) ,  [Endo Gears](Endo_Gears.md) ,  [Peri Pendulum](Peri_Pendulum.md) ,  [Myo Lift](Myo_Lift.md) , and  [Epi Head](Epi_Head.md) 
- [Duration](Duration.md) of  [Globus](Globus.md)  and  [Nitesco](Nitesco.md) 
- [Pierce](Pierce.md) of  [Just Vent](Just_Vent.md) ,  [Mace](Mace.md) , and  [Stamazza](Stamazza.md) 
- [Healing](Recovery.md) effect of  [Lifesign Scan](Lifesign_Scan.md)  and  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md) 
- Number of bounces of  [Carréllo](Carréllo.md)

## From items {#From_items}

Amount\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is the  [Duplicator](Duplicator.md), which provides +1 Amount per level, up to a maximum of +2 Amount at level 2.

-  [Academy Badge](Academy_Badge.md)  has 6 levels and provides +1 Amount every even level, up to a maximum of +3 Amount.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting an Amount stat bonus of +0.2 upon activation.
-  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Amount by +0.1 for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Beginning (X)](Beginning_(X).md) provides +3 Amount for the character\'s starting weapon and its [evolution](Evolution.md), and +1 Amount for  [Bone](Bone.md),  [Cherry Bomb](Cherry_Bomb.md),  [Carréllo](Carréllo.md),  [Celestial Dusting](Celestial_Dusting.md), and  [La Robba](La_Robba.md).

## From characters {#From_characters}

### Base Game {#Base_Game}

-  [Gennaro](Gennaro_Belpaese.md) and  [Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md) start with +1 Amount.
-  [Mortaccio](Mortaccio.md),  [Cavallo](Yatta_Cavallo.md),  [Ramba](Bianca_Ramba.md), and  [O\'Sole](O'Sole_Meeo.md) gain +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60.
  - Mortaccio, Cavallo, Ramba, and O\'Sole gain +1 additional Amount when they morph at level 80 following the collection of their respective Chaos [relic](Relics.md).
-  [Ambrojoe](Sir_Ambrojoe.md) starts with +10 Amount, every level decreases by -2 until it returns to normal by level 6. He gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60.
-  [Bats](Bats_Bats_Bats.md) gains +1 amount per revival.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell}

-  [Gav\'Et-Oni](Gav'Et-Oni.md) gains+1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

### Tides of the Foscari {#Tides_of_the_Foscari}

-  [Rottin\'Ghoul](Rottin'Ghoul.md) gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Crewmate](Crewmate_Dino.md) has a chance to gain a random amount of Amount every time a weapon or passive item is upgraded to its maximum level, with no cap.
-  [Horse](Horse.md) gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

### Operation Guns {#Operation_Guns}

-  [Lucia](Lucia_Zero.md) gains +0.2 Amount after [reviving](Revival.md), with no cap.
-  [Simondo](Simondo_Belmont.md) gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Carrie](Carrie_Fernandez.md) and  [Cornell](Cornell.md) start with +1 Amount.
-  [Blue Crescent Moon Cornell](Blue_Crescent_Moon_Cornell.md) starts with +3 Amount.
-  [Trevor](Trevor_Belmont.md) gains +1 Amount every 10 levels, up to +3 at level 30.
-  [Familiar](Familiar.md) and  [Innocent Devil](Innocent_Devil.md) gain +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60.
-  [Cave Troll](Cave_Troll.md) gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.
-  [Hammer](Hammer.md) starts with +1 Amount and gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60. His Amount is also doubled while invulerable.
-  [Leon](Leon_Belmont.md) gains +1 Amount upon obtaining the  [Vampire Killer](Vampire_Killer.md).
