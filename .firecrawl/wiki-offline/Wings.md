**Wings** are a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. They are unlocked by reaching Level 5 as any character.

Wings increase [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md) by 10% per level.

Wings are required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Shadow Pinion](Shadow_Pinion.md), [Valkyrie Turner](Valkyrie_Turner.md), and for the evolution of [Glass Fandango](Glass_Fandango.md), [Celestial Voulge](Celestial_Voulge.md).

Wings can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Whiteout](Whiteout.md), and [Tiny Bridge](Tiny_Bridge.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Wings have 5 levels. At max level, Wings provide +50% [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md).

| Level 2 | Movement speed increases by 10% |
| Level 3 | Movement speed increases by 10% |
| Level 4 | Movement speed increases by 10% |
| Level 5 | Movement speed increases by 10% |
