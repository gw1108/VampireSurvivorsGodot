**Skull O\'Maniac** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by surviving 30 minutes with [Lama](Lama_Ladonna.md).

Skull O\'Maniac increases [Curse](Curse.md) by 10% per level.

Skull O\'Maniac is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Song of Mana](Song_of_Mana.md), [Mannajja](Mannajja.md). It is also required for the evolution of [Shadow Servant](Shadow_Servant.md), [Ophion](Ophion.md), in which Skull O\'Maniac must also be fully leveled.

Skull O\'Maniac can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md), and [Abyss Foscari](Abyss_Foscari.md).
