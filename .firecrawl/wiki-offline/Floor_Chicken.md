The **Floor Chicken** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md). It restores 30 Health to the player upon being picked up, and its drop rate depends on the [Luck](Luck.md) stat.

Collecting five Floor Chickens will unlock the  [Garlic](Garlic.md).

Collecting 500 Floor Chickens will unlock [The Coop](The_Coop.md) [stage](Stages.md).

Floor Chicken can also be gained upon [level up](Level_up.md) in the late game. It is offered as a choice when your inventory is full and all items are maxed out.
