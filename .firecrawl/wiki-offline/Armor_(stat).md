# Armor (stat) {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Armor** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. Each point of Armor the player has will reduce all incoming damage by 1 and increases the damage from [retaliatory effects](Retaliatory_Damage.md) by 10%.

The player has 0 base Armor before modifications, such as the characters bonuses or [PowerUps](PowerUp.md). Armor is additional and the retaliatory damage bonus stack additively with itself and multiplicatively with  [Might](Might.md).

There is no upper limit to how much Armor the player can have, but the retaliatory bonus damage is capped at +500% (50 Armor). Also, regardless the amount of Armor the player has, the damage taken from an enemy will always be at least 1.

Armor also affects Base Damage of  [Iron Ball](Iron_Ball.md)  and  [Wrecking Ball](Wrecking_Ball.md) .

 [Probotector](Probotector.md)  gains 10% Might per +1 Armor.

The Armor [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases Armor by 1 per rank, with a maximum rank of 3. At rank 3, it provides +3 Armor. It initially costs 600.

| 2        | +2        |
| 3        | +3        |

## From items {#From_items}

Armor\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is  [Armor (item)](Armor_(item).md), which increases Armor by 1 per level, up to a maximum of +5 Armor at level 5.

-  [Centralis Custos](Centralis_Custos.md)  provides +10 Armor when the player character takes damage from enemies.
-  [Trinum Custodem](Trinum_Custodem.md)  provides a passive +10 Armor and an additional +10 Armor when the player character takes damage from enemies.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting an Armor stat bonus of +0.2 upon activation.
-  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Armor by +0.1 for the character that collected them.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) provides +1 Armor per  [Revival](Revival.md) consumed.
