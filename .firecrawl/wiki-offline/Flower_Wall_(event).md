**Flower Wall** is a [circle](Circle.md)-type [map event](Enemy.md#Map_events) that occurs in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md).

The Flower Wall event summons a ring of durable  [Flower Wall](Flower_Wall.md) units around the player, which slowly creeps inward. The Flower Walls trap both the player and enemies inside until they are defeated or expire after their set duration.\
