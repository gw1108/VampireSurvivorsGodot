**Achievements**, displayed as **Unlocks** in-game, unlock new [items](Item.md), [characters](Character.md), [stages](Stage.md), [gameplay features](Relics.md), or [gold coins](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) when completed. When they are completed, the corresponding achievement will also be unlocked on the gaming platform if it supports an achievement system. For the Steam version in particular, the achievements from the base game, as well as the *[Legacy of the Moonspell](Legacy_of_the_Moonspell.md)* and *[Tides of the Foscari](Tides_of_the_Foscari.md)* DLC, are required to achieve 100% completion. This makes a total number of **243** required achievements.

While collab [DLCs](Expansions.md) have their own unlocks, due to licensing deals that can expire and stop the distribution of collaboration DLCs, the achievements may be unobtainable in the future for some people. This concern prevented the development team to implement achievement support for these DLCs on any platforms.

With the release of *[Ante Chamber](Ante_Chamber.md)*, the total number of achievements is **449**.
