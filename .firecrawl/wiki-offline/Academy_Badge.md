**Academy Badge** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is only obtainable as a stage item or by reaching Level 30 as [Eleanor](Eleanor.md), [Maruto](Maruto.md), or [Keitha](Keitha.md).

Academy Badge reduces [experience gain](Growth.md) by -3% per level, in exchange for +1 [Amount](Amount.md) or +1 [Revival](Revival.md) alternatively.

Academy Badge can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md) and [Abyss Foscari](Abyss_Foscari.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Academy Badge has 6 levels. At max level, Academy Badge provides -15% [Growth](Growth.md), +3 [Amount](Amount.md), and +2 [Revival](Revival.md).

|  |  |
|----|----|
| Level | Description |
| Level 1 | \"Will provide bonus Amount and Revivals in exchange for Growth.\" |
| Level 2 | Fires 1 more projectile. XP gain decreases by -3%. |
| Level 3 | Adds 1 Revival. XP gain decreases by -3%. |
| Level 4 | Fires 1 more projectile. XP gain decreases by -3%. |
| Level 5 | Adds 1 Revival. XP gain decreases by -3%. |
| Level 6 | Fires 1 more projectile. XP gain decreases by -3%. |
