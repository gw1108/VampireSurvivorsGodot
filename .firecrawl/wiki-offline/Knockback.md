# Knockback {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

**Knockback** is an effect applied to [enemies](Enemy.md) that briefly pushes them backwards whenever they are damaged by the player.

When an enemy is receiving knockback, its overall movement speed is reversed and multiplied by a knockback multiplier for the duration of the effect. The knockback multiplier is the product of the damaging [weapon](Weapon.md)\'s knockback dealt multiplier and knockback taken multiplier of the target enemy. The knockback effect lasts 120 milliseconds.^\[*Verify*\]^

[Garlic](Garlic.md) and its evolution [Soul Eater](Soul_Eater.md) have an additional effect that increases the knockback taken by enemies hit by it. If the enemy is not immune to [debuffs](Debuff.md), every time it is hit by either of these weapons, its knockback taken multiplier is increased by an additional 0.3 until its maximum knockback value is reached.
