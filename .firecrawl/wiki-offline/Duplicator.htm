Duplicator is a passive item in Vampire Survivors. It is unlocked by getting the Magic Wand to Level 7.

Duplicator increases Amount by +1 per level.

Duplicator is required for the evolution of Lightning Ring, Thunder Loop. It is also required for the evolution of Summon Night, Echo Night, in which Duplicator must also be fully leveled.

Duplicator can be found as a stage item in Cappella Magna, Moongolow, Boss Rash, and Mt.Moonspell.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Duplicator is a great passive item, as it boosts the effectiveness of most weapons by a large amount. Extra projectiles leads to extra damage and extra protection from enemies.
  • Amount bonuses work for some weapons better than others. Weapons such as the Axe and Fire Wand do great when paired with the Duplicator, but weapons such as the Magic Wand benefit less due to the already high amount of low damage projectiles.
