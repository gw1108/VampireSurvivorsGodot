The **Rerollo** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md).

 [Hail from the Future (X)](Hail_from_the_Future_(X).md) has a chance of dropping a Rerollo with each [Level Up](Level_up.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Picking up the Rerollo gives the player one  [Reroll](Reroll.md).

 [Chula-Reh](Chula-Reh.md) will reroll his  [Luck](Luck.md) once when picking up a Rerollo.
