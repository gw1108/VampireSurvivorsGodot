**Gilded Clover** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md).

## Gold Fever {#Gold_Fever}

Picking up Gilded Clover gathers all the  [gold](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) left on the ground to the character and starts a Gold Fever that lasts for 10 seconds. When an [enemy](Enemy.md) is killed, there is a 75% chance it drops a Gold Coin that has a value of 0.5, which then is multiplied by  [Greed](Greed.md) and [Stage Modifiers](Stage.md#Available_stages). Enemies from [Il Molise](Il_Molise.md) will divide the value by 5 to 0.1.

Its duration can be extended by collecting  [Gold Coins](Gold_Coin_(pickup).md),  [Coin Bags](Coin_Bag.md), and  [Rich Coin Bags](Rich_Coin_Bag.md) by 0.5, 5 and 10 seconds respectively. Coins generated from killing enemies during Gold Fever and  [Vicious Hunger](Vicious_Hunger.md) extend the duration by 0.01 and 0.05 seconds respectively. The timer is capped at 10 seconds.

 [Fever](Fever.md) increases Gold Fever duration, which can be obtained with  [Karoma\'s Mana](Karoma's_Mana.md) and some  [Suravorts](Survarocchi.md).

During an active  [Orologion](Orologion.md), the duration decay is halved to counteract enemies not moving in, turning them into [Starry Heavens](Starry_Heavens.md) with  [Celestial Voulge](Celestial_Voulge.md) make them actively helpful by extending the duration without slowing down the kill rate.

With  [Big Trouser](Big_Trouser.md), Gold Fever lasts for 15 seconds and all duration extensions from collecting coins are also multiplied by 1.5; With  [Sammy](Sammy.md)  and  [Master Librarian](Master_Librarian.md) , Gold Fever lasts for 12.5 seconds and duration extensions are multiplied by 1.25.

Sammy triggers a Gold Fever at level 30.

 [Power of Lire](Power_of_Lire.md)  triggers a Gold Fever every attack, which Master Librarian have a hidden weapon version.

With  [Disco of Gold (XV)](Disco_of_Gold_(XV).md), collecting both types of Coin Bags also starts a Gold Fever.
