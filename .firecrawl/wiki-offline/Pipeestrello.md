> The cultural association between bat and vampire stretches back as far as folklore itself. Some claim to have seen the deathly cold visage of a Vampire Lord striding within the swirling maelstrom of these nocturnal nasties, but those people just wanted attention. They're bats, and nothing more.
>
> **--- [Ars Gouda](Ars_Gouda.md), p. 1**

**Pipeestrello** (compare *pipistrello*, Italian for *bat*^[\[1\]](#cite_note-1)^) is an [enemy](Enemy.md) primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md) and [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), with additional appearances in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md), [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Whiteout](Whiteout.md), [Carlo Cart](Carlo_Cart.md), [Bat Country](Bat_Country.md), [Tiny Bridge](Tiny_Bridge.md), and [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md).

In Mad Forest, Boss Rash, and Bat Country, it appears both as a normal enemy and during the [**Bat Swarm**](Bat_Swarm.md) [event](Map_event.md). In Gallo Tower and Tiny Bridge, it only spawns as a normal enemy, while in Inlaid Library, it appears exclusively during the Bat Swarm. In Eudaimonia Machine, it is summoned by the [The Directer](The_Directer.md) as a special enemy during the initial phase of the stage.

There are two boss variants of Pipeestrello: *Pipeestrello 4* and *Pipeestrello 5*

- Pipeestrello 4, also referred to as \"Glowing Bat\", appears in Mad Forest, Inlaid Library, Bat Country, Gallo Tower, Dairy Plant, and Carlo Cart. In Mad Forest and Inlaid Library, spawns both as a regular boss and an Arcana-carrier boss when [Arcanas](Arcanas.md) are enabled. In Bat Country, only the regular boss variant appears. In Gallo Tower, Dairy Plant, and Carlo Cart, only the Arcana-carrier boss can appear.
- Pipeestrello 5, also referred to as \"Silver Bat\", appears only in Mad Forest, Inlaid Library, and Whiteout. It spawns as a regular boss in Mad Forest and Inlaid Library, and also as an Arcana-carrier boss in Whiteout when Arcanas are enabled.

Both boss variants reappear together as regular enemies during later waves in Mad Forest.

The boss variants\' [health scales with the player\'s level](HP_x_Level.md). The *Swarm* variant is resistant to [defang](Defang.md) and moves in a [fixed direction](Fixed_Direction.md).
