Critical Hit (sometimes referred to as "crit") is a special attack type in Vampire Survivors. It allows a weapon to roll a Critical Hit Chance on hit and if succeeded apply a Critical Hit Damage Multiplier to the damage that would be dealt. Not all weapons are able to perform Critical Hits.

Critical Hit Chance is affected by  Luck.

Formula for how Luck affects Critical Hit Chance: chanceWithLuck = positiveEffectChance ⋅ totalLuck

(Critical Hit Chance shares the same percentage with other chance based effects a.k.a positiveEffectChance (there are a few exceptions))

(totalLuck = luck percentage + 100, so 30% luck = 130 totalLuck, and 130 totalLuck ⋅ 10% critical chance = 13% critical chance)

Effects

[edit | edit source]

Different weapons have different Critical Hit Chances and Critical Hit Multipliers.

 Slash (XVI) doubles Critical Hit Multiplier.

 Claimh Solais passively increases Critical Hit Damage by up to 100% depending on the amount of enemies on screen. This effect is applied to all equiped weapons capable of Critical Hits.
