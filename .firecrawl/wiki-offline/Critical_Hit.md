**Critical Hit** (sometimes referred to as \"crit\") is a special attack type in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It allows a weapon to roll a Critical Hit Chance on hit and if succeeded apply a Critical Hit Damage Multiplier to the damage that would be dealt. Not all weapons are able to perform Critical Hits.

Critical Hit Chance is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md).

Formula for how Luck affects Critical Hit Chance: *chanceWithLuck* *= positiveEffectChance ⋅ totalLuck*

(Critical Hit Chance shares the same percentage with other chance based effects a.k.a *positiveEffectChance* (there are a few exceptions))

(totalLuck = luck percentage + 100, so 30% luck = 130 totalLuck, and 130 totalLuck **⋅** 10% critical chance = 13% critical chance)

## Effects {#Effects}

Different weapons have different Critical Hit Chances and Critical Hit Multipliers.

 [Slash (XVI)](Slash_(XVI).md) doubles Critical Hit Multiplier.

 [Claimh Solais](Claimh_Solais.md) passively increases Critical Hit Damage by up to 100% depending on the amount of enemies on screen. This effect is applied to all equiped weapons capable of Critical Hits.
