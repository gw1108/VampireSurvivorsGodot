Skip

Skip is one of the stats in Vampire Survivors. It affects the amount of times the player can skip the rewards when leveling up and gain experience instead.

There is no upper limit to how many Skips the player may have.

The Skip PowerUp increases Skip by 2 per rank, with a maximum rank of 5 after unlocking all Skip achievements. With one rank unlocked it initially costs 100.

Rank Skip
1 +2
2 +4
3 +6
4 +8
5 +10

Each rank of PowerUp is unlocked by these achievements:

  • Surviving 15 minutes in Il Molise.
  • Surviving 30 minutes in Green Acres.
  • Surviving 30 minutes in The Bone Zone.
  • Surviving 15 minutes in Moongolow.
  • Surviving 15 minutes in Boss Rash.

From items

[edit | edit source]

Skip does not have any associated passive item.

 Golden Egg permanently increases Skip by 0.2 for the character that collected it.

From arcana

[edit | edit source]

 Silent Old Sanctuary (XX) provides 3 Skip.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Use up all the  Reroll and  Banish before using Skip, as they provide 20% of the experience to the next level so using it later when each level requires more exp will be more efficient. Having more weapons and passive items early on is much better than using a level to reroll choices because it translates to more kills and experience when it take less to level up.
