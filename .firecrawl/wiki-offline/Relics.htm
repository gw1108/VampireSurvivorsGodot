Relics

Relics are permanent items that unlock new mechanics and features once collected. They are very similar to stage items as they are simply spawned on the map and are collected upon touching them. Once collected, they are permanently added to the collection menu and do not appear again. Until picked up, their locations are indicated by green arrows once the player enters a stage that contains them.
