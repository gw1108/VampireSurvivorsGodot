**Empty Tome** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by holding 6 different [weapons](Weapon.md) at once.

Empty Tome reduces the [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) of weapons by 8% per level.

Empty Tome is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Magic Wand](Magic_Wand.md), [Holy Wand](Holy_Wand.md).

Empty Tome can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), and [Abyss Foscari](Abyss_Foscari.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Empty Tome has 5 levels. At max level, Empty Tome provides -40% [Cooldown](Cooldown.md).

| Level 4 | Cooldown reduced by 8.0%            |
| Level 5 | Cooldown reduced by 8.0%            |
