The Golden Egg is an item pickup in Vampire Survivors that permanently increases a random stat of the player's current character by a small amount.

Obtaining

[edit | edit source]

Golden Eggs can be purchased repeatedly from the  Merchant or the  Eggman, who appears when the player has at least 5,000 Golden Eggs on their save file and is currently holding at least 1,000,000, for 10,000. The Eggman also has the option to buy max eggs, using all of the player's gold. Additionally, the following special enemies can drop a Golden Egg when killed:

  •  Moon Atlantean
  •  Sun Atlantean
  •  City Atlantean
  •  Volcano Atlantean
  •  Moongolow Atlanteans
  •  The Reaper will drop five Golden Eggs when killed if the player is holding the  Infinite Corridor or  Crimson Shroud.

The amount of Golden Eggs a single character can have is hard capped at 3.402823E+38, which is the 32-bit floating point maximum.

Unlike all other characters,  Queen Sigma is completely unaffected by Golden Eggs. When playing as her, Golden Eggs in the Merchant's shop are replaced with the  Candybox, and Golden Eggs dropped by Atlanteans or The Reaper will be thrown away when picked up.

Effects

[edit | edit source]

Each Golden Egg will provide a small permanent boost to one of the 19 stats available by the following amount:

  • +1  Max Health
  • +0.1  Recovery,  Armor,  Amount,  Revival,  Reroll
  • +1%  Move Speed,  Might,  Speed,  Duration,  Area,  Luck,  Growth,  Greed,  Curse
  • -0.5%  Cooldown
  • +0.3  Magnet
  • +0.2  Skip,  Banish

Golden Eggs boosts the character's starting stats. It does not however increase the character stats while in-game. As a result, any golden eggs obtained in-game will take effect for the next game session.

There is a hard cap on certain stats, even if visually the number displayed is higher. The following are stats with hard caps:

  • +50  Armor (with the exception of  Toastie)
  • +900%  Might
  • +400%  Speed
  • +400%  Duration
  • +900%  Area
  • -90%  Cooldown
  • +10  Amount

Removal

[edit | edit source]

The  Healer will appear in Moongolow when a character has at least 5,000 Golden Eggs. The Healer can remove 100 Golden Eggs at once, or, when that same character has at least 25,000 Golden Eggs, all eggs that grant Move Speed.
