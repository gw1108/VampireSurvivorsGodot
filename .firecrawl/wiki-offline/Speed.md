# Speed {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

This article is about projectile speed. For movement speed, see [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md).

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Speed** (also known as **Projectile Speed**) is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects how fast the projectiles of most [weapons](Weapons.md) move.

The player has 100% base Speed before modifications, such as the characters bonuses or [PowerUps](PowerUps.md). The Speed in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Speed, meaning that Speed stat of +30% equals to 130% total Speed, for example. Speed modifications from [characters](Characters.md), PowerUps, and items stack additively. Other modifiers, such as [Arcanas](Arcanas.md), stack multiplicatively with the collective bonuses from the aforementioned.

The upper limit of total Speed after character, PowerUp and item modifications is 500%. Bonuses from Arcanas are unaffected by the limit. If total Speed is negative, projectiles move backwards. At 0 Speed, they do not move at all.

Speed also affects:

- Base Damage of [SpellString](SpellString.md) ,  [SpellStrom](SpellStrom.md) ,  [Confodere](Confodere.md) ,  [Vol Confodere](Vol_Confodere.md) ,  [Sonic Dash](Sonic_Dash.md) ,  [Rapidus Fio](Rapidus_Fio.md) ,  [Hydro Storm](Hydro_Storm.md) 
- Base Damage and Cooldown of  [Melio Confodere](Melio_Confodere.md) 
- Singularity damage of  [SpellStrom](SpellStrom.md) 
- Critical Damage of  [Rune Sword](Rune_Sword.md) 
- Further increases the projectile speed of  [Thunderbolt Spear](Thunderbolt_Spear.md) \'s special attack.
-  [Jonathan Morris](Jonathan_Morris.md) \'s charged ability
-  [Newt Plissken](Newt_Plissken.md)  gains +1% Might per +1% Speed.

The Speed [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases Speed by 10% per rank, with a maximum rank of 2. At rank 2, it provides +20% Speed. It initially costs 300.

|------|-------|
| Rank | Speed |
| 1    | +10%  |
| 2    | +20%  |

## From items {#From_items}

Speed\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is the  [Bracer](Bracer.md), which provides +10% Speed per level, up to a maximum of +50% Speed at level 5.

-  [Torrona\'s Box](Torrona's_Box.md) provides +4%, then +3% per level, up to a maximum of +25% at level 8.
-  [Rapid Fire](Rapid_Fire.md)  provides +30% Speed for 10 seconds, this stacks with no cap.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting a Speed stat bonus of +4% upon activation.
-  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Speed by +1% for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) provides +5% Speed when chosen per  [Revival](Revival.md) consumed.
-  [Chaos in the Dark Night (V)](Chaos_in_the_Dark_Night_(V).md) provides a multiplier cycling between 0.5 to 1.5 every 10 seconds, and gives +1% Speed every level.

## From characters {#From_characters}

### Base Game {#Base_Game}

-  [Pasqualina](Pasqualina_Belpaese.md) starts with +10% Speed and gains +10% Speed every 5 levels, up to +30% at level 15. In total, she can have +40% Speed.
-  [Dommario](Dommario.md) starts with +40% Speed bonus.
-  [Giovanna](Giovanna_Grana.md) gains +1% Speed every level, with no cap.
-  [Zi\'Assunta](Zi'Assunta_Belpaese.md) gains +0.5% Speed every level, with no cap.
-  [Exdash](Exdash_Exiviiq.md) and  [Toastie](Toastie.md) start with -50% Speed.
-  [Smith](Smith_IV.md) starts with -50% Speed and gains +0.7% Speed every level, with no cap.
-  [Marrabbio](Boon_Marrabbio.md) starts with -110% Speed.
-  [Minnah\'s](Minnah_Mannarah.md) Speed cycles between multiplied by 2 and divided by 2 every minute.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell}

-  [Syuuto](Syuuto_Moonspell.md) starts with -30% Speed. He gains +30% Speed after he morphs when his  [starting weapon](Summon_Night.md) evolves.

### Tides of the Foscari {#Tides_of_the_Foscari}

-  [Keitha](Keitha_Muort.md) starts with +20% Speed.

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Scientist](Scientist_Mina.md) can select to gain +1% Speed per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Speed per level.

### Operation Guns {#Operation_Guns}

-  [Bill](Bill_Rizer.md) and  [Lance](Lance_Bean.md) start with +20% Speed and gain 2.5% Speed at specific kill thresholds up to 50%.
-  [Ariana](Ariana.md) starts with +20% Speed.
-  [Lucia](Lucia_Zero.md) gains +4% Speed after reviving, with no cap.
-  [Brad](Brad_Fang.md) gains up to +10% Speed based on missing percentage of  [Max Health](Max_Health.md) scaling linearly.

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Eric](Eric_Lecarde.md) and  [Isaac](Isaac.md) start with +20% Speed.
-  [Yoko](Yoko_Belnades.md) starts with +40% Speed.
-  [Joachim](Joachim_Armster.md) can select to gain 1% Speed per level.
-  [Blackmore](Blackmore.md) can select to gain +1% Speed per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Speed per level.
-  [Maxim](Maxim_Kischine.md) gains +1% Speed per 24 health of overhealing.
-  [Leon](Leon_Belmont.md) gains +10% Speed upon obtaining the  [Vampire Killer](Vampire_Killer.md).
