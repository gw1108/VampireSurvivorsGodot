# Seal {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Seal** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects the amount of times the player can remove an [item](Item.md) choice before the run in the [collection](Collection.md) menu.

Seal effectively allows the player to  [Banish](Banish.md) an item from [level up](Level_up.md) choices or a [pickup](Pickup.md) from [light sources](Light_source.md) before entering a stage. It cannot be used on [relics](Relics.md)^[\[1\]](#cite_note-1)^, [Arcanas](Arcanas.md), the  [Experience Gem](Experience_Gem.md),  [Gold Coin](Gold_Coin.md),  [Coin Bag](Coin_Bag.md),  [Rich Coin Bag](Rich_Coin_Bag.md),  [Treasure Chest](Treasure_Chest.md),  [Friendship Amulet](Friendship_Amulet.md),  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md), weapons acquired from  [Gemini (I)](Gemini_(I).md), passive items acquired from the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md), and  [Academy Badge](Academy_Badge.md).

After buying any of the Seal PowerUps, and having installed at least one DLC, the MEGA SEAL module is also unlocked in the collection menu, allowing the player to seal all [weapons](Weapons.md) in a specific version category at once, without taking up any normal Seal slots. However, it is not possible to \"un-seal\" a specific weapon sealed by the MEGA SEAL.

### Unlocking {#Unlocking}

Each PowerUp is unlocked by these achievements:

|          |                                            |
|----------|--------------------------------------------|
| PowerUp  | Unlocking                                  |
| Seal I   | Banish 10 or more weapons in a single run. |
| Seal II  | Banish 20 or more weapons in a single run. |
| Seal III | Banish 40 or more weapons in a single run. |
| Seal All | Banish 80 or more weapons in a single run. |

If no DLCs are owned the amount of weapons that can be banished through the level up choices will be lower than 80. To remedy this the player has to go to the collection and seal weapons that normally wouldn\'t show up in the level up screen like for example evolutions, around 20 seals will be needed to cover the missing weapons.
