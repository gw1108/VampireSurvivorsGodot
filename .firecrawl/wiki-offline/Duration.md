**Duration** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects how long the effects of certain [weapons](Weapons.md) last.

The player has 100% base Duration before modifications. The Duration in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Duration, meaning that Duration stat of +30% equals to 130% total Duration, for example. Duration modifications from [characters](Characters.md), [PowerUps](PowerUps.md), and items stack additively. Other modifiers, such as [Arcanas](Arcanas.md), stack multiplicatively with the collective bonus from the aforementioned.

The upper limit of total Duration after character, PowerUp and item modifications is 500%. Bonuses from Arcanas are unaffected by the limit.

Duration also affects:

- Base Damage of  [Four Seasons](Four_Seasons.md) ,  [Godai Shuffle](Godai_Shuffle.md)  and  [Hydro Storm](Hydro_Storm.md) 
- Critical Damage of  [Rune Sword](Rune_Sword.md) 
- [Amount](Amount.md) of  [Santa Javelin](Santa_Javelin.md)

The Duration [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases Duration by +15% per rank, with a maximum rank of 2. At rank 2, it provides +30% Duration. It initially costs 300.

|       |          |
|-------|----------|
| Level | Duration |
| 1     | +15%     |
| 2     | +30%     |

## From items {#From_items}

Duration\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is the  [Spellbinder](Spellbinder.md), which provides +10% Duration per level, up to a maximum of +50% Duration at level 5.

-  [Silver Ring](Silver_Ring.md) also provides +5% per level, up to a maximum of +40% at level 9.
-  [Torrona\'s Box](Torrona's_Box.md) provides +4%, then +3% per level, up to a maximum of +25% at level 8.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting a Duration stat bonus of +4% upon activation.
-  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Duration by +1% for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) provides +5% Duration when chosen per  [Revival](Revival.md) consumed.
-  [Lost & Found Painting (XVII)](Lost_&_Found_Painting_(XVII).md) provides a multiplier cycling between 0.5 to 1.5 every 10 seconds, and gives +1% Duration every level.

## From characters {#From_characters}

### Base Game {#Base_Game}

-  [Dommario](Dommario.md) starts with +40% Duration.
-  [Poppea](Poppea_Pecorina.md) gains +1% Duration every level, with no cap.
-  [Gallo](Iguana_Gallo_Valletto.md) starts with +15% Duration.
-  [Zi\'Assunta](Zi'Assunta_Belpaese.md) gains +0.5% Duration every level, with no cap.
-  [Exdash](Exdash_Exiviiq.md) and  [Toastie](Toastie.md) start with -10% Duration.
-  [Smith](Smith_IV.md) starts with -10% Duration and gains +0.7% Duration every level, with no cap.
-  [Minnah](Minnah_Mannarah.md)\'s Duration cycles between multiplied by 2 and divided by 2 every minute.

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Crewmate](Crewmate_Dino.md) has a chance to gain a random amount of Duration every time a weapon or passive item is upgraded to its maximum level, with no cap.
-  [Scientist](Scientist_Mina.md) can select to gain +1% Duration per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Duration per level.

### Operation Guns {#Operation_Guns}

-  [Lucia](Lucia_Zero.md) gains +4% Duration after [reviving](Revival.md), with no cap.

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Juste](Juste_Belmont.md),  [Charlotte](Charlotte_Aulin.md),  [Barlowe](Barlowe.md) and  [Olrox](Count_Olrox.md) start with +20% Duration.
-  [Sypha](Sypha_Belnades.md),  [Sara](Sara_Trantoul.md) and  [Charlotte & Jonathan](Charlotte_&_Jonathan.md) start with +40% Duration.
-  [Shaft](Shaft.md) starts with +50% Duration.
-  [Joachim](Joachim_Armster.md) can select to gain +1% Duration per level.
-  [Blackmore](Blackmore.md) starts with +40% Duration and can select to gain +1% Duration per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Duration per level.
