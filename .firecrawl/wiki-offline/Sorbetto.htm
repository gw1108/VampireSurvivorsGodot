Sorbetto is an item pickup in Vampire Survivors. It is also a hidden weapon wielded by  Frozenshade.

Sorbettos can be found from light sources while the player has  Out of Bounds (XII). They can also be found on the stage Whiteout, or dropped by  Hail from the Future (X).

Effects

[edit | edit source]

Similar to  Nduja Fritta Tanto, upon picking up the Sorbetto, the player's character begins to breathe a cone of icy wind in the direction they are facing, for 10 seconds. It also has the added effect of freezing the enemies it damaged. It is coded to be a temporary weapon, so it can be affected by different stat increases.

Collecting  Floor Chicken or  Wall Chicken while  Frozenshade is at max health has a chance of activating Sorbetto.

Sorbetto is affected by Gemini (I), causing it to emit frozen wind behind the character in addition to in front.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • Sorbetto is named after the sorbet, a frozen dessert.
  • The codename for the weapon "CONEOFCOLD" is likely a reference to the spell "Cone of Cold" in Dungeons and Dragons.
