**Gennaro Belpaese** is one of the playable [characters](Character.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. His starting weapon is the  [Knife](Knife.md).

## Unlocking {#Unlocking}

He is unlocked by default. He needs to be purchased for 500, which scales with other characters purchased.

Gennaro starts with +20  [Max Health](Max_Health.md) and +1  [Amount](Amount.md).
