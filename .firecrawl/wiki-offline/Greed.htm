Greed

Greed is one of the stats in Vampire Survivors. It affects the amount of  Gold Coins that are gained from pickups and  Treasure Chests.

The player has 100% base Greed before modifications. The Greed in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Greed, meaning that Greed stat of +30% equals to 130% total Greed, for example. Greed modifications from character bonuses, PowerUps and items stack additively. Stage bonuses to gold gained do not count as Greed and stack multiplicatively with the collective bonus from the aforementioned.

There is no upper limit to how much Greed the player may have.

Greed also affects Base Damage of  Night Sword  and  Muramasa .

 Colonel Bahamut  gains +1%  Might per +1% Greed.

The Greed PowerUp increases Greed by 10% per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, it provides +50% Greed. It initially costs 200.

From items

[edit | edit source]

Greed's associated passive item is the  Stone Mask, which provides +10% Greed per level, up to a maximum of +50% Greed at level 5.

 Golden Egg permanently increases Greed by 1% for the character that collected it.

From arcana

[edit | edit source]

 Wicked Season (XIII) doubles Greed in certain 10 second intervals, and gives +0.5% Greed every level.

From characters

[edit | edit source]

Base Game

[edit | edit source]
  •  Ambrojoe starts with +20% Greed.
  •  Gallo and  Divano reduce Greed by -50%.
  •  Leda and  Marrabbio reduce Greed by -80%.
  •  Trouser starts with +20% Greed and gains +1% Greed every level, with no cap.

Legacy of the Moonspell

[edit | edit source]
  •  Megalo Syuuto starts with +50% Greed.

Tides of the Foscari

[edit | edit source]
  •  Genevieve and  Je-Ne-Viv start with +50% Greed.

Emergency Meeting

[edit | edit source]
  •  Scientist can select to gain +1% Greed per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Greed per level.

Operation Guns

[edit | edit source]
  •  Lucia gains +1% Greed after reviving, with no cap.

Ode to Castlevania

[edit | edit source]
  •  Soma starts with +50% Greed.
  •  Dracula,  Elizabeth,  Stella & Loretta,  Loretta & Stella,  Stella,  Loretta,  Brauner,  Graham,  Walter,  Carmilla,  Olrox,  Succubus,  Galamoth,  Megalo Elizabeth,  Megalo Olrox,  Megalo Death and  Megalo Dracula start with +100% Greed.
  •  Master Librarian starts with +1000% Greed.
  •  Vincent starts with +20% Greed and gains Greed when overhealing, with diminishing returns. His Greed is also doubled while invulnerable.
  •  Joachim starts with +100% Greed and can select to gain +1% Greed per level.
  •  Blackmore starts with +100% Greed and can select to gain +1% Greed per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Greed per level.

From stages

[edit | edit source]

Base Game

[edit | edit source]
  • Dairy Plant multiply gold gain by 1.2.
  • Gallo Tower, Bat Country, and Tiny Bridge multiply gold gain by 1.3.
  • Cappella Magna multiply gold gain by 1.4.
  • The Bone Zone multiply gold gain by 1.5.

Legacy of the Moonspell

[edit | edit source]
  • Mt.Moonspell multiply gold gain by 1.5.

The hyper modes of all stages, except Holy Forbidden and Eudaimonia Machine, give a +50% gold gain bonus additively.

The inverse modes of all stages, except Holy Forbidden and Eudaimonia Machine, give a +200% gold gain bonus additively.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Greed is not vital for winning a run, but rather getting the most money out of it. If you find yourself unable to finish a run, it is advised to invest into other passives and PowerUps over taking the Stone Mask or ranking up the Greed PowerUp.
  • Bonuses to Greed apply additively. For example, 50% Greed makes  Rich Coin Bags give 150 coins instead of 100. Stage bonuses, however, do not count as Greed, but are an additional multiplier. The +50% Gold bonus from Mad Forest's hyper mode would increase the 150 coins from previous example to 225.
    • For the highest gold multiplier, you need the level 5 Stone Mask (+50%) and the Greed PowerUps (+50%) for a total of +100% Gold earning, and play The Bone Zone in hyper mode multiplying overall gold gain by 2x to total of 4x the original amount.
