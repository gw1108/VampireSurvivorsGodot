# Cooldown {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

**Cooldown** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects the length of the delay between attacks done by most [weapons](Weapons.md).

The player has 100% base Cooldown before modifications, such as the characters bonuses or [PowerUps](PowerUps.md). The Cooldown in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Cooldown, meaning that a Cooldown stat of -30% equals to 70% total Cooldown, for example. Cooldown modifications stack additively.

The lower limit of total Cooldown is 10%.

 [Browny](Browny.md)  gains +2%  [Area](Area.md) per -1% Cooldown.

The Cooldown [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) decreases Cooldown by 2.5% per rank, with a maximum rank of 2. At rank 2, it provides -5% Cooldown. It initially costs 900.

|------|----------|
| Rank | Cooldown |
| 1    | -2.5%    |
| 2    | -5%      |

## From items {#From_items}

Cooldown\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is the  [Empty Tome](Empty_Tome.md), which reduces Cooldown by -8% per level, down to a maximum of -40% Cooldown at level 5.

-  [Gold Finger](Gold_Finger.md) provides -100% Cooldown for 10 seconds ( [Hail from the Future (X)](Hail_from_the_Future_(X).md) helps with getting these often).
-  [Rapid Fire](Rapid_Fire.md)  provides -30% Cooldown for 10 seconds, stacking up to -100% total character Cooldown.
-  [Centralis Custos](Centralis_Custos.md)  provides -10% Cooldown when the player character takes damage from enemies.
-  [Trinum Custodem](Trinum_Custodem.md)  provides a passive -10% Cooldown and an additional -10% Cooldown when the player character takes damage from enemies.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting a Cooldown stat bonus of -3% upon activation.
-  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently reduce Cooldown by -0.5% for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Tragic Princess (III)](Tragic_Princess_(III).md) decreases Cooldown when moving. It is not limited by the -90% Cooldown cap.

<!-- -->

-  [Silent Old Sanctuary (XX)](Silent_Old_Sanctuary_(XX).md) provides -8% Cooldown when chosen per active weapon slot left empty, for a maximum of -40% Cooldown

## From characters {#From_characters}

### Base Game {#Base_Game}

-  [Arca](Arca_Ladonna.md) reduces Cooldown by -5% every 5 levels, up to -15% at level 15.
-  [Porta](Porta_Ladonna.md) starts with -90% Cooldown, every level increases by +30% until it returns to normal by level 4.
-  [Christine](Christine_Davain.md) and  [Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md) start with -25% Cooldown.
-  [Gallo](Iguana_Gallo_Valletto.md) starts with -15% Cooldown.
-  [Exdash](Exdash_Exiviiq.md) and  [Toastie](Toastie.md) both start with +10% Cooldown.
-  [Smith](Smith_IV.md) starts with +10% Cooldown and reduces Cooldown by -0.25% every level, with no cap.
-  [Avatar](Avatar_Infernas.md) reduces Cooldown by -0.25% every level, with no cap.
-  [Minnah](Minnah_Mannarah.md)\'s Cooldown cycles between multiplied by 2 and divided by 2 every minute.
-  [Leda](Leda.md) starts with -10% Cooldown.
-  [Santa](Santa_Ladonna.md) starts with -70 Cooldown and increases by 20% at levels 2 and 3 until she has -10% Cooldown.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell}

-  [Menya](Menya_Moonspell.md) starts with -10% Cooldown. She gains -20% Cooldown in her boosted state.
-  [Babi-Onna](Babi-Onna.md) is not affected by Cooldown. She attacks by posing and its rate is increased by  [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md).
-  [Megalo Menya](Megalo_Menya_Moonspell.md) starts with -20% Cooldown.

### Tides of the Foscari {#Tides_of_the_Foscari}

-  [Keitha](Keitha_Muort.md) starts with -10% Cooldown.
-  [Luminaire](Luminaire_Foscari.md) gains -100% Cooldown temporarily after [reviving](Revival.md).

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Crewmate](Crewmate_Dino.md) has a chance to lose a random amount of Cooldown every time a weapon or passive item is upgraded to its maximum level, with no cap.
-  [Impostor](Impostor_Rina.md) gains -20% Cooldown while in their boosted state.
-  [Megalo Impostor](Megalo_Impostor_Rina.md) starts with -10% Cooldown.

### Operation Guns {#Operation_Guns}

-  [Lance](Lance_Bean.md) gains -1% Cooldown at specific kill thresholds up to 25%.
-  [Lucia](Lucia_Zero.md) gains -3% Cooldown after [reviving](Revival.md), with no cap.
-  [Brad](Brad_Fang.md) gains up to -50% Cooldown based on missing percentage of  [Max Health](Max_Health.md) scaling linearly.
-  [Sheena](Sheena_Etranzi.md) gains -1% Cooldown per 2%  [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md).

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Mina](Mina_Hakuba.md),  [Eric](Eric_Lecarde.md) and  [Nathan](Nathan_Graves.md) start with -5% Cooldown.
-  [Richter](Richter_Belmont.md) and  [Succubus](Succubus.md) start with -10% Cooldown.
-  [Megalo Elizabeth](Megalo_Elizabeth_Bartley.md),  [Megalo Olrox](Megalo_Olrox.md),  [Megalo Death](Megalo_Death.md) and  [Megalo Dracula](Megalo_Dracula.md) start with -20% Cooldown.
-  [Saint Germain](Saint_Germain.md) reduces Cooldown by 0.2% every level, with no cap.
-  [Leon](Leon_Belmont.md) reduces Cooldown by 5% upon obtaining the  [Vampire Killer](Vampire_Killer.md).
-  [Sonia](Sonia_Belmont.md) reduces Cooldown by 30% for 10 seconds when overhealing by +8.
-  [Shaft](Shaft.md) reduces Cooldown by 20% during his HP-Critical ability.
