Thorns

From Vampire Survivors Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Thorns is one of the hidden player stats in Vampire Survivors. It allows the player character to deal retaliatory damage to enemies they come in contact with.

Effects

[edit | edit source]

Thorns starts with 0 points. For every 1 point of Thorns the player possesses, they will deal 10 damage to enemies they contact, much like the damage provided by  Divine Bloodline (IX). Thorns is considered retaliatory damage.

  • The player does not need a full point of Thorns to gain the effect; 0.5 points of Thorns will deal 5 damage to enemies upon contact.

Thorns is additive with other forms of contact damage, such as that from  Stone Skull .

From Arcana

[edit | edit source]
  •  Edge of the Earth (VIII)} will grant the player +3 Thorns.

From Survarot

[edit | edit source]
  •  GALA Skill:  Dommario's GALA Skill: Gains +0.5 Thorns after gaining a set amount of gold [1k, 2k, 3k].[1]
  •  FOIL Skill: Gains +0.5 Thorns after gaining a set amount of gold [1k, 2k, 3k].[1]

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • Thorns,  Charm,  Fever,  Defang,  Preserve, and Invulnerability Time are the only character stats in the game that are not displayed on the stat panel.
    • Thorns, Invulnerability Time, and Fever are the only stats without a respective PowerUp.
