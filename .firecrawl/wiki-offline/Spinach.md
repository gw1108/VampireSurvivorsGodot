**Spinach** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by default.

Spinach increases  [Might](Might.md) by 10% per level.

Spinach can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Mt.Moonspell](Mt.Moonspell.md), and [Emerald Diorama](Emerald_Diorama_(stage).md).

## Levels {#Levels}
