**Santa Water** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Suor Clerici](Suor_Clerici.md). It is unlocked by default.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [La Borra](La_Borra.md), which requires  [Attractorb](Attractorb.md).

As a vampire-killing tool, it can be picked from  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md) .

## Effects {#Effects}

Santa Water rains down bottles of holy water from the sky, which break upon impact with the ground, leaving puddles of water and blue flames that damage enemies.

Until Santa Water\'s total  [Amount](Amount.md) reaches four, the first bottle of each cast aims at the closest enemy. Otherwise, the bottles are dropped in a clockwise, roughly circular pattern.

Santa Water has a 0.5 seconds [Hitbox Delay](Weapon.md#Stats), meaning that the same enemy cannot be hit more often than every half a second by the same puddle. However, because all active delays are reset simultaneously as soon as any one elapses, some enemies may get hit more often.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Tragic Princess (III)](Tragic_Princess_(III).md) - Reduces Santa Water\'s  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) when the character is moving. It is affected by  [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md).
-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) - Allows Santa Water\'s puddles to deal extra flat damage based on  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md).
-  [Pale Diamond Incursion (V)](Pale_Diamond_Incursion_(V).md) - Aims at the nearest enemy instead of shooting randomly for every bottle even after four Amount, and gains  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) bonus based on  [Speed](Speed.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Santa Water has 8 levels. At max level, Santa Water has +30 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +80% [Area](Area.md), +3 [Amount](Amount.md), and +1 second of [Duration](Duration.md).

|  |  |
|----|----|
| Level | Description |
| Level 1 | Generates damaging zones. |
| Level 2 | Fires 1 more projectile. Base Area up by 20%. |
| Level 3 | Effect lasts 0.5 seconds longer. Base Damage up by 10. |
| Level 4 | Fires 1 more projectile. Base Area up by 20%. |
| Level 5 | Effect lasts 0.3 seconds longer.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-duration-1)^ Base Damage up by 10. |
| Level 6 | Fires 1 more projectile. Base Area up by 20%. |
| Level 7 | Effect lasts 0.3 seconds longer.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-duration-1)^ Base Damage up by 5. |
| Level 8 | Base Area up by 20%. Base Damage up by 5. |
