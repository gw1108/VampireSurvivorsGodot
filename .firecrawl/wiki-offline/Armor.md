**Armor** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by default.

Armor increases  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md) by 1 per level, reducing all damage dealt to the player by 1. It cannot reduce damage to 0. Armor also increases [Retaliatory Damage](Retaliatory_Damage.md) by 10% additively and it is an additional multiplier on top of  [Might](Might.md).

Armor can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md), and [Abyss Foscari](Abyss_Foscari.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Armor has 5 levels. At max level, Armor provides +5  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md) and +50% [Retaliatory Damage](Retaliatory_Damage.md).

|----|----|
| Level | Description |
| Level 1 | \"Reduces incoming damage by 1. Increases retaliatory damage by 10%.\" |
| Level 2 | Reduces incoming damage by 1. |
| Level 3 | Reduces incoming damage by 1. |
| Level 4 | Reduces incoming damage by 1. |
| Level 5 | Reduces incoming damage by 1. |
