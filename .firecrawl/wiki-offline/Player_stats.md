**Stats** (short for [*statistics*](Statistic_(role-playing_games).md)) are numerical attributes that a player has in *Vampire Survivors*, which affect the player\'s [character](Character.md) and how the environment reacts to them. They can be seen before the player starts the game in the *Character Selection* menu, in-game when paused, or when [leveling up](Leveling_up.md).

All stats can be modified by [PowerUps](PowerUp.md), and in addition most of them have their own [passive item](Passive_item.md) that provides a bonus. Also most characters have some stats with modifications to the respective base value (e.g. 100 [Max Health](Max_Health.md) and 30 [Magnet](Magnet.md)).

## Types {#Types}

There are 26 different stats in the game, all of which directly apply to the player\'s character, with the exception of [Seal](Seal.md).

## Notes {#Notes}

- Leveled up [weapons](Weapon.md) may have boosted properties identical to existing player stats, but they only affect that specific weapon\'s behavior. Player stats are usually multipliers on top of their base stats, and sometimes stats that are increased by level-ups may not be affected by the corresponding player stat.
