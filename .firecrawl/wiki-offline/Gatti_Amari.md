**Gatti Amari** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Giovanna Grana](Giovanna_Grana.md). It is unlocked by surviving 15 minutes as Giovanna Grana.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Vicious Hunger](Vicious_Hunger.md), which requires  [Stone Mask](Stone_Mask.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Gatti Amari summons cats from a hexagram on the edge of the screen. These cats mostly walk around in a straight line and may change direction after a while, and have a chance to attack with their claws when they are close to the enemies. They can also occasionally hurt the player character when they are close to it, dealing twice the usual damage. When a cat is close to another, they have a chance to start a fight, which creates a huge Area-of-Effect of cartoonish scuffle cloud that lasts for a while. If a cat walks over a [Pickup](Pickup.md) on the screen, it gets automatically consumed, affecting the cat in different ways.

 [Area](Area.md) affects both how big the claw attacks and the scuffle are.  [Duration](Duration.md) affects how long the cats remain on the screen as well as how long the scuffle lasts.

Gatti Amari\'s scratches have a 1 second [Hitbox Delay](Weapon.md#Stats) and its scuffle has a 0.03 seconds Hitbox Delay, meaning that the same enemy cannot be hit more often than every second and 0.03 seconds respectively. However, because all active delays are reset simultaneously as soon as any one elapses, some enemies may get hit more often. This does not apply to claw attacks as they do not last long enough to hit twice.

### Interactions {#Interactions}

[Pickups](Pickup.md) consumed by the cats have varying effects on them:

-  [Floor Chicken](Floor_Chicken.md) permanently increases the chance stat and [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md).
-  [Nduja Fritta Tanto](Nduja_Fritta_Tanto.md) explodes the cat that consumed it.
  - The explosion is the same as from  [Heart of Fire (XIX)](Heart_of_Fire_(XIX).md).
-  [Little Clover](Little_Clover.md) and  [Gilded Clover](Gilded_Clover.md) make the cat that consumes them run twice as fast.
-  [Orologion](Orologion.md) freezes the cat that consumed it. It can be used by the player instead.
-  [Rosary](Rosary.md) and  [Vacuum](Vacuum.md) have no additional effects. It can be used by the player instead.

The chance for most interactions of the cats is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md), multiplying the chance stat of the weapon.

- Increases the chance for the cats to attack enemies.
- Decreases the chance for the cats to attack the player.
- Increases the chance for them to start a scuffle.
- Increases the chance of Pickups that are not beneficial to the cats being used by the player instead.

### Floor Chicken {#Floor_Chicken}

Floor Chicken, when collected by the cats, permanently increases the chance stat and attack damage of all cats. The chance stat is multiplied by 1.1 every time a chicken is collected. The [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md) increase per chicken is calculated with the formula:

$bonus\ damage\ per = 1 - min(0.95,\frac{C}{108})$,

where \'C\' is the total amount of chicken collected. The minimum-function breaks even at 102 chickens, after which every chicken collected grants 0.05 Base Damage.

The total bonus damage, up to 102 chickens, can be calculated using the following equation:

$total\ bonus\ damage = \frac{C \times (215 - C)}{216}$

## Levels {#Levels}

Gatti Amari has 8 levels. At max level, Gatti Amari has +60%  [Speed](Speed.md), +3 seconds to [Duration](Duration.md), and +2 to  [Amount](Amount.md).

|---------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                                                  |
| Level 1 | Summons capricious projectiles. Might interact with pickups. |
| Level 2 | Base Speed up by 20%.                                        |
| Level 3 | Effect lasts 1.5 seconds longer.                             |
| Level 4 | Base Speed up by 20%.                                        |
| Level 5 | Fires 1 more projectile.                                     |
| Level 6 | Effect lasts 1.5 seconds longer.                             |

| Level 8 | Fires 1 more projectile.                                     |
