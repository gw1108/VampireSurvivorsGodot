**Candybox** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by discovering every standard [evolution](Evolutions.md) and union.

Its Gift is  [Super Candybox II Turbo](Super_Candybox_II_Turbo.md), obtainable only when opening a  [Treasure Chest](Treasure_Chest.md) after obtaining a Candybox.

It is the starting weapon of  [Big Trouser](Big_Trouser.md).  [Queen Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md) can also buy the Candybox from the [Merchant](Merchant.md) for 1,000, replacing the option to buy  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Obtaining Candybox opens a list of all base weapons remaining to be obtained in the run. For a weapon to appear in the Candybox, it has to be used at least once in the entire save file beforehand.

Weapons that were [banished](Banish.md) will also still appear in the list. Its rarity is multiplied by  [Luck](Luck.md). Under normal circumstances, it is only available once. If it is not taken when it is first offered, it will never be offered again with [level ups](Level_up.md).

It may also appear in treasure chests and requires a fully upgraded [Weapon](Weapons.md) and [Passive](Passive_items.md) inventory (so that Coin Bags spawn in chests). Once these conditions are met, Candybox has a small luck-based chance to appear in any treasure chest the player opens, in place of a Big Coin Bag. Five-item treasure chest guarantees spawning a Candybox with the above condition maxed.

The following weapons cannot be picked due to being redundant for Candybox:

-  [Morning Star](Morning_Star.md)
-  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md)
-  [Spectral Sword](Spectral_Sword.md)
-  [Belnades\' Spellbook](Belnades'_Spellbook.md)
-  [Ebony Diabologue](Ebony_Diabologue.md)
-  [Intuition](Intuition.md)

Unlocking and using all five *Ode to Castlevania* selectors mentioned above will unlock Candybox as an alternative starting weapon for [Alucard](Alucard.md), [Shanoa](Shanoa.md), and [Soma Cruz](Soma_Cruz.md).
