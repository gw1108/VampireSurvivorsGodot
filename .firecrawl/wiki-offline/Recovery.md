# Recovery {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

</figure>

**Recovery** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It determines the amount of health that is regenerated per second.

The base value of Recovery is 0, which means that health does not regenerate without any Recovery bonuses. Recovery also boosts healing from [pickups](Pickups.md), each 0.1 translates to an additive 10% boost. It does not always increase healing from [weapons](Weapons.md). Bonuses to Recovery are flat bonuses and thus stack additively.

There is no upper limit to how much Recovery the player can have.

Recovery also affects:

- Base damage of  [Sharp Tongue](Sharp_Tongue.md) ,  [Silver Tongue](Silver_Tongue.md) , and  [Impostongue](Impostongue.md) 
- Base damage of  [Star Flail](Star_Flail.md)  and  [Moon Rod](Moon_Rod.md) 
- Healing effect of  [Lifesign Scan](Lifesign_Scan.md)  and  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md) 
- Healing effect of  [Refectio](Refectio.md)  and  [Sanctuary](Sanctuary.md)

The Recovery [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases Recovery by 0.1 per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, it provides +0.5 Recovery. It initially costs 200.

|      |          |
|------|----------|
| Rank | Recovery |
| 1    | +0.1     |
| 2    | +0.2     |
| 3    | +0.3     |
| 4    | +0.4     |
| 5    | +0.5     |

## From items {#From_items}

Recovery\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is the  [Pummarola](Pummarola.md), which provides +0.2 Recovery per level, up to a maximum of +1 Recovery at level 5.

 [Metaglio Left](Metaglio_Left.md) also provides +0.1 Recovery per level, up to a maximum of +0.8 Recovery at level 9.

 [Centralis Custos](Centralis_Custos.md)  provides +2 Recovery when the player character takes damage from enemies.

 [Trinum Custodem](Trinum_Custodem.md)  provides a passive +2 Recovery and an additional +2 Recovery when the player character takes damage from enemies.

 [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) permanently increases Recovery by 0.1 for the character that collected it.

## From darkana {#From_darkana}

 [Crystal Cries (XII)](Crystal_Cries_(XII).md) generates crystals when killing frozen enemies that can be collected to slightly increase  [Max Health](Max_Health.md), Recovery, and  [Growth](Growth.md).
