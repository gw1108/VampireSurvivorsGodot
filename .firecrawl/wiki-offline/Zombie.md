**Zombie** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), with an additional appearance in [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md).

In Mad Forest, it appears exclusively as a normal enemy. In Eudaimonia Machine, it is summoned by [The Directer](The_Directer.md) as a special enemy during the second phase of the stage.

Zombie takes reduced [knockback](Knockback.md).
