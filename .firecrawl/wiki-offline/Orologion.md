**Orologion** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md). It can only drop after the character reaches level 4, and its drop rate is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md).

Collecting Orologion for the first time will unlock  [Clock Lancet](Clock_Lancet.md).

Collecting 20 Orologions will unlock the [Whiteout](Whiteout.md) [stage](Stages.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Picking up Orologion creates a spinning blue ring around the player, [freezing](Freeze.md) all [enemies](Enemies.md). Frozen enemies become tinted blue, do not move on their own, and do no damage to the player. The player is able to push enemies out of the way by walking into them. Enemies will continue to spawn, and new enemies will also become frozen. The effect will last for 10 seconds before the ring of blue will begin to fade. Once it has completely faded, all enemies will continue to move again. Some enemies are resistant to the freeze effect.

Orologion slows down  [Gold Fever\'s](Gilded_Clover.md) duration decay by half to counteract enemies not moving in.

 [Out of Bounds (XII)](Out_of_Bounds_(XII).md) will double the drop rate rarity of Orologions from 2 to 4.

### Starry Heavens {#Starry_Heavens}

 [Celestial Voulge](Celestial_Voulge.md) gains a special effect that turns Orologions into Starry Heavens. This effect causes frozen enemies to move towards the player while still not dealing damage, in addition to continuously dealing damage to all enemies.
