The **Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane** are a [relic](Relics.md) located in [The Bone Zone](The_Bone_Zone.md). Once collected, it permanently unlocks the [secret menu](Secrets.md), which offers hints to find secret [characters](Characters.md), and allows the player to cast [spells](Spells.md).

## Obtaining {#Obtaining}

The Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane are obtained from defeating the  [Sketamari](Sketamari.md) in [The Bone Zone](The_Bone_Zone.md), which appears after the player has obtained the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md), at with point it will be marked with a question mark on the  [pause menu map](Milky_Way_Map.md). Once the Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane have been collected, it can no longer be dropped by the Sketamari, though the Sketamari will still spawn thereafter.

It should also be noted that as long as the player is not within viewing distance or has not gotten close enough to the Sketamari, it will not have absorbed any enemies, and can be faced at a much later level, instead of being taken down right away, making obtaining the scroll much easier and doable with any character.

- For a more extensive guide to unlocking this relic, see [Guide:Defeating the Sketamari](/w/Guide:Defeating_the_Sketamari "Guide:Defeating the Sketamari").

Alternatively, the Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane can have its features accessed by tapping the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md) in the [Collection](Collection.md) seven times.
