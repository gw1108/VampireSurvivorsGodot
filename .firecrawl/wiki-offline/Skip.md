**Skip** is one of the [stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects the amount of times the player can skip the rewards when [leveling up](Level_up.md) and gain [experience](Experience_Gem.md) instead.

There is no upper limit to how many Skips the player may have.

The Skip [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases Skip by 2 per rank, with a maximum rank of 5 after unlocking all Skip [achievements](Achievements.md). With one rank unlocked it initially costs 100.

|      |      |
|------|------|
| Rank | Skip |
| 1    | +2   |
| 2    | +4   |
| 3    | +6   |
| 4    | +8   |
| 5    | +10  |

Each rank of PowerUp is unlocked by these achievements:

- Surviving 15 minutes in [Il Molise](Il_Molise.md).
- Surviving 30 minutes in [Green Acres](Green_Acres.md).
- Surviving 30 minutes in [The Bone Zone](The_Bone_Zone.md).
- Surviving 15 minutes in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md).
- Surviving 15 minutes in [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md).

## From items {#From_items}

Skip does not have any associated [passive item](Passive_item.md).

 [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) permanently increases Skip by 0.2 for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

 [Silent Old Sanctuary (XX)](Silent_Old_Sanctuary_(XX).md) provides 3 Skip.
