# Preserve {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Preserve** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It provides a chance to retain uses of  [Reroll](Reroll.md),  [Skip](Skip.md), and  [Banish](Banish.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Preserve has a chance to keep a Reroll, Skip, and Banish action after being used, allowing more uses of them in a run overall.

The Preserve [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases the chance of preserving Reroll, Skip, and Banish by 10% per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, there is a 50% chance to preserve them. It initially costs 500, and is unlocked by collecting  [Wax Fetish](Wax_Fetish.md) in [Mazerella](Mazerella.md).

| 2     | 20%      |
| 3     | 30%      |
| 4     | 40%      |
| 5     | 50%      |
