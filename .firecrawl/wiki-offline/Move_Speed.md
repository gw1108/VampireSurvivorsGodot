# Move Speed {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

This article is about movement speed. For projectile speed, see [Speed](Speed.md).

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Move Speed** (also known as **Movement Speed**, originally known as Speed) is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects how fast the [character](Characters.md) moves in relation to the default speed.

The player has 100% base MoveSpeed before modifications. The MoveSpeed in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base MoveSpeed, meaning that MoveSpeed stat of +30% equals to 130% total MoveSpeed, for example. MoveSpeed modifications from [characters](Characters.md), [PowerUps](PowerUps.md), and [passive items](Passive_items.md) stack additively. [Stage bonuses](Stage.md#Stage_modifiers) stack multiplicatively with the collective bonus from the aforementioned.

There is no upper limit to how much MoveSpeed the player may have.

MoveSpeed also affects:

- [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) of  [Shadow Pinion](Shadow_Pinion.md) and  [Valkyrie Turner](Valkyrie_Turner.md)
- Cooldown of  [Valmanway](Valmanway.md) while moving
-  [Tragic Princess (III)](Tragic_Princess_(III).md)\'s effectiveness
-  [Babi-Onna](Babi-Onna.md)\'s effectiveness on triggering weapons

 [Ariana](Ariana.md)  gains +1  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md) per +10% MoveSpeed.

 [Sheena Etranzi](Sheena_Etranzi.md)  gains -1%  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) per +2% MoveSpeed.

The Move Speed [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases MoveSpeed by 5% per rank, with a maximum rank of 2. At rank 2, it provides +10% MoveSpeed. It initially costs 600.

|------|------------|
| Rank | Move Speed |
| 1    | +5%        |
| 2    | +10%       |

## From items {#From_items}

Move Speed\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is  [Wings](Wings.md), which provide +10% Move Speed per level, up to a maximum of +50% Move Speed at level 5.

-  [Sonic Dash](Sonic_Dash.md)  increases Move Speed by +10% per level per activation stack, up to a total of +80% MoveSpeed per stack at level 8. The MoveSpeed bonus is capped at +500%.
-  [Rapidus Fio](Rapidus_Fio.md)  increases Move Speed by +80% per activation stack. The MoveSpeed bonus is capped at +500%.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting a Move Speed stat bonus of +8% upon activation.
-  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Move Speed by +1% for the character that collected it.
