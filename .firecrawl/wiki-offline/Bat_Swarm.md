**Bat Swarm** is a [swarm](Swarm.md)-type [map event](Map_event.md) that occurs in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), and [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md).

Bat Swarm summons 50  [Pipeestrellos](Pipeestrello.md#Pipeestrello_(swarm)) that move in a straight line across the map instead of targeting the player. These units push other enemies along their path as they travel. Although they resemble  [Little Pipeestrello](Pipeestrello.md#Pipeestrello_1) and  [Red-eyed Pipeestrello](Pipeestrello.md#Pipeestrello_2) in appearance, they are a unique variant of Pipeestrello and have distinct properties.

Bat Swarm units are immune to [defang](Defang.md).
