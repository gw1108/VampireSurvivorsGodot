**Ghost Swarm** is a [swarm](Swarm.md)-type [map event](Event.md) that occurs in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md).

Ghost Swarm summons 20  [Swarm Ghost](Ghost.md#Ghost_(swarm)) that move in a straight line across the map instead of targeting the player. These units push other enemies along their path as they travel.
