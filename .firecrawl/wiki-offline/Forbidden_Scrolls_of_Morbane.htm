The Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane are a relic located in The Bone Zone. Once collected, it permanently unlocks the secret menu, which offers hints to find secret characters, and allows the player to cast spells.

Obtaining

[edit | edit source]

The Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane are obtained from defeating the  Sketamari in The Bone Zone, which appears after the player has obtained the Yellow Sign, at with point it will be marked with a question mark on the  pause menu map. Once the Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane have been collected, it can no longer be dropped by the Sketamari, though the Sketamari will still spawn thereafter.

It should also be noted that as long as the player is not within viewing distance or has not gotten close enough to the Sketamari, it will not have absorbed any enemies, and can be faced at a much later level, instead of being taken down right away, making obtaining the scroll much easier and doable with any character.

  • For a more extensive guide to unlocking this relic, see Guide:Defeating the Sketamari.

Alternatively, the Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane can have its features accessed by tapping the Yellow Sign in the Collection seven times.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • The Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane are a reference to Morbane's Book of Monsters & Magic; a collection of stories or tales about various legendary and or mythical monsters and spells, collected on cards, and published in installments in a magazine in early 1992. The magazine was "The Ancestral Trail," a valuable collector's item in its own right, if a player can find or put together a full collected set.
