Characters are what the player can control to move around in a stage. Each character starts with its own unique weapon(s) and passive bonus.

There are currently 207 official playable characters. The base game has 54 characters, 22 can be obtained by discovering secrets. The DLCs have a combined roster of 153 characters, 56 of them being secret ones. All non-secret characters need to be purchased using gold, increased by 10% additively per characters purchased, with the exception of Antonio, being the first basic character a new player uses; Queen Sigma, who requires an achievement but costs 0 gold to purchase; and Dracula, who is immediately unlocked upon obtaining the Black Disk and does not require a purchase. Some of these characters require an achievement to unlock for purchase. Certain characters have alternate unlocking method unlisted.

Certain characters are duo, which means a secondary character will follow the main character. Both characters get their leveling up by turns, and shares the same upgrade pool (which means if both of them takes the same weapon or passive item, both of them will not be able to evolve it). Passive items can be shared between both of them for evolution. Stage items will only be added to the main character's inventory. Secondary character will not take damage normally.

The unlock conditions for each character can be found in the Unlocks menu. It should be noted that if a new player unlocks at least one new character, the character selection menu will start to show silhouettes of other locked characters, instead of just showing the character select icons of  Antonio Belpaese,  Imelda Belpaese,  Pasqualina Belpaese, and  Gennaro Belpaese.

  • Characters that are unlocked and bought will have a gray selection icon shown with their character and starting weapon, with their name marked in white.
  • Characters that are unlocked but not bought will also have a gray selection icon and their starting weapon shown, but the characters themselves will have a black silhouette and their name is marked in gray.
  • Characters that are locked have their selection icon entirely colored blue-gray, with the character silhouette, character name, and starting weapon being translucent.

Non-player characters

[edit | edit source]

The following is a list of non-player characters that appear in Vampire Survivors (not counting all of the merchants that are also player characters, any enemy, or any of the unused characters):

  •  Eggman: a merchant in Moongolow who only sells  Golden Eggs.
  •  Healer: a merchant in Moongolow who can remove  Golden Eggs.
  •  Xanthia: a merchant who appears in Planar Bazaar where they sell the  Victory Sword and the  Phas3r. They also sell the  Emerald Disk and the  Joker Disk in Moongolow.
  •  Giocare: a merchant in Westwoods who asks for the player to spin the wheel in exchange for some  Gold Coins. Winning the "Spin the Wheel!" minigame would allow for the player to gain access to more parts of this stage.
