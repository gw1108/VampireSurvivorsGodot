Speed

From Vampire Survivors Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
This article is about projectile speed. For movement speed, see Move Speed.

Speed (also known as Projectile Speed) is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. It affects how fast the projectiles of most weapons move.

The player has 100% base Speed before modifications, such as the characters bonuses or PowerUps. The Speed in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Speed, meaning that Speed stat of +30% equals to 130% total Speed, for example. Speed modifications from characters, PowerUps, and items stack additively. Other modifiers, such as Arcanas, stack multiplicatively with the collective bonuses from the aforementioned.

The upper limit of total Speed after character, PowerUp and item modifications is 500%. Bonuses from Arcanas are unaffected by the limit. If total Speed is negative, projectiles move backwards. At 0 Speed, they do not move at all.

Speed also affects:

  • Base Damage of SpellString ,  SpellStrom ,  Confodere ,  Vol Confodere ,  Sonic Dash ,  Rapidus Fio ,  Hydro Storm 
  • Base Damage and Cooldown of  Melio Confodere 
  • Singularity damage of  SpellStrom 
  • Critical Damage of  Rune Sword 
  • Further increases the projectile speed of  Thunderbolt Spear 's special attack.
  •  Jonathan Morris 's charged ability
  •  Newt Plissken  gains +1% Might per +1% Speed.

From items

[edit | edit source]

Speed's associated passive item is the  Bracer, which provides +10% Speed per level, up to a maximum of +50% Speed at level 5.

  •  Torrona's Box provides +4%, then +3% per level, up to a maximum of +25% at level 8.
  •  Rapid Fire  provides +30% Speed for 10 seconds, this stacks with no cap.
  •  Paranormal Scan  has a chance of granting a Speed stat bonus of +4% upon activation.
  •  Golden Egg can permanently increase Speed by +1% for the character that collected it.

From arcana

[edit | edit source]
  •  Awake (IV) provides +5% Speed when chosen per  Revival consumed.
  •  Chaos in the Dark Night (V) provides a multiplier cycling between 0.5 to 1.5 every 10 seconds, and gives +1% Speed every level.

From characters

[edit | edit source]

Base Game

[edit | edit source]
  •  Pasqualina starts with +10% Speed and gains +10% Speed every 5 levels, up to +30% at level 15. In total, she can have +40% Speed.
  •  Dommario starts with +40% Speed bonus.
  •  Giovanna gains +1% Speed every level, with no cap.
  •  Zi'Assunta gains +0.5% Speed every level, with no cap.
  •  Exdash and  Toastie start with -50% Speed.
  •  Smith starts with -50% Speed and gains +0.7% Speed every level, with no cap.
  •  Marrabbio starts with -110% Speed.
  •  Minnah's Speed cycles between multiplied by 2 and divided by 2 every minute.

Legacy of the Moonspell

[edit | edit source]
  •  Syuuto starts with -30% Speed. He gains +30% Speed after he morphs when his  starting weapon evolves.

Tides of the Foscari

[edit | edit source]
  •  Keitha starts with +20% Speed.

Emergency Meeting

[edit | edit source]
  •  Scientist can select to gain +1% Speed per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Speed per level.

Operation Guns

[edit | edit source]
  •  Bill and  Lance start with +20% Speed and gain 2.5% Speed at specific kill thresholds up to 50%.
  •  Ariana starts with +20% Speed.
  •  Lucia gains +4% Speed after reviving, with no cap.
  •  Brad gains up to +10% Speed based on missing percentage of  Max Health scaling linearly.

Ode to Castlevania

[edit | edit source]
  •  Eric and  Isaac start with +20% Speed.
  •  Yoko starts with +40% Speed.
  •  Joachim can select to gain 1% Speed per level.
  •  Blackmore can select to gain +1% Speed per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Speed per level.
  •  Maxim gains +1% Speed per 24 health of overhealing.
  •  Leon gains +10% Speed upon obtaining the  Vampire Killer.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Projectile Speed stat is additive. The highest possible Projectile Speed is possible while playing as Giovanna, with the rank 2 Speed PowerUp (+20%), rank 5 Omni PowerUp (+10%), level 5 Bracer (+50%), and level 8  Torrona's Box (+25%) for a total of +(player level+105)% Projectile Speed.
  • Minnah's stat cycling cannot stack with  Chaos in the Dark Night (V).
