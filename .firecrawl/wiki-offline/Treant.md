> Trees filled with evil by an evil god of pollution whose toxic love makes wastelands of woodlands. Their hunger for desolation makes them effective lobbyists, ensuring deforestation goes unchallenged around the world.
>
> **--- [Ars Gouda](Ars_Gouda.md), p. 321**

The **Treant** is a boss [enemy](Enemies.md) that appears in [Ode to Castlevania](Ode_to_Castlevania_(stage).md). Upon defeating the Treant for the first time, it will drop the  [Wood Carving Score](Wood_Carving_Score.md). On subsequent defeats, it will drop a  [Treasure Chest](Treasure_Chest.md).
