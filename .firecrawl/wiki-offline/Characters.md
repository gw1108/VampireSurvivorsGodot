**Characters** are what the player can control to move around in a [stage](Stage.md). Each character starts with its own unique [weapon(s)](Weapon.md) and passive bonus.

There are currently 207 official playable characters. The base game has 54 characters, 22 can be obtained by discovering [secrets](Secrets.md). The DLCs have a combined roster of 153 characters, 56 of them being secret ones. All non-secret characters need to be purchased using [gold](Gold_Coin_(currency).md), increased by 10% additively per characters purchased, with the exception of [Antonio](Antonio.md), being the first basic character a new player uses; [Queen Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md), who requires an achievement but costs 0 [gold](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) to purchase; and [Dracula](Dracula.md), who is immediately unlocked upon obtaining the [Black Disk](Black_Disk.md) and does not require a purchase. Some of these characters require an achievement to unlock for purchase. Certain characters have alternate unlocking method unlisted.

Certain characters are duo, which means a secondary character will follow the main character. Both characters get their leveling up by turns, and shares the same **upgrade pool** (which means if both of them takes the same weapon or passive item, both of them will not be able to [evolve](Evolution.md) it). Passive items can be shared between both of them for evolution. [Stage items](Stage_item.md) will only be added to the main character\'s inventory. Secondary character will not take damage normally.

<figure class="mw-default-size" typeof="mw:File/Thumb">

<figcaption>Here,  <a href="Antonio_Belpaese.md">Antonio</a> is bought,  <a href="Arca_Ladonna.md">Arca</a> is locked, and  <a href="Suor_Clerici.md">Clerici</a> is unlocked but not bought.</figcaption>
</figure>

The unlock conditions for each character can be found in the [Unlocks](Unlocks.md) menu. It should be noted that if a new player unlocks at least one new character, the character selection menu will start to show silhouettes of other locked characters, instead of just showing the character select icons of  [Antonio Belpaese](Antonio_Belpaese.md),  [Imelda Belpaese](Imelda_Belpaese.md),  [Pasqualina Belpaese](Pasqualina_Belpaese.md), and  [Gennaro Belpaese](Gennaro_Belpaese.md).

- Characters that are unlocked and bought will have a gray selection icon shown with their character and starting weapon, with their name marked in white.
- Characters that are unlocked but not bought will also have a gray selection icon and their starting weapon shown, but the characters themselves will have a black silhouette and their name is marked in gray.
- Characters that are locked have their selection icon entirely colored blue-gray, with the character silhouette, character name, and starting weapon being translucent.

## Non-player characters {#Non-player_characters}

The following is a list of non-player characters that appear in *Vampire Survivors* (not counting all of the merchants that are also player characters, any [enemy](Enemy.md), or any of the [unused characters](Characters/Unused.md)):

- ** [Eggman](Eggman.md):** a merchant in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md) who only sells  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md).
- ** [Healer](Healer.md):** a merchant in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md) who can remove  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md).
- ** [Xanthia](Xanthia.md):** a merchant who appears in [Planar Bazaar](Planar_Bazaar.md) where they sell the  [Victory Sword](Victory_Sword.md) and the  [Phas3r](Phas3r.md). They also sell the  [Emerald Disk](Emerald_Disk.md) and the  [Joker Disk](Joker_Disk.md) in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md).
- ** [Giocare](Giocare.md):** a merchant in [Westwoods](Westwoods.md) who asks for the player to spin the wheel in exchange for some  [Gold Coins](Gold_Coin_(currency).md). Winning the \"Spin the Wheel!\" minigame would allow for the player to gain access to more parts of this stage.
