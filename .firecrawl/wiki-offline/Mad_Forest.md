**Mad Forest** is the first [stage](Stage.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*, unlocked from the very start of the game. It is the only stage that is available in the demo version of the game on [itch.io](https://itch.io), the others being completely inaccessible, rather than being locked like in the full version of the game.

Reaching level 80 in Mad Forest with **inverse** mode enabled unlocks [Bat Country](Bat_Country.md) as playable stage.

[Boon Marrabbio (enemy)](Boon_Marrabbio_(enemy).md), which is a special [enemy](Enemy.md), will spawn under [certain conditions](Boon_Marrabbio.md#Unlocking) in Mad Forest after obtaining [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md).

## Description {#Description}

> *Once a thriving haven, now a dumping ground for evil. A vampire is said to be the root of this evil, but we can find only mayhem and roast chicken.*

## Overview {#Overview}

Mad Forest features an open map with a large area and very few obstacles, giving players plenty of space to move around. The only destructible that spawn in this stage are [light sources](Light_source.md) in the form of braziers, which will drop random [pickups](Pickups.md) once broken.

### Hyper mode {#Hyper_mode}

The [hyper mode](Hyper_mode.md) for this stage can be unlocked by defeating the [Giant Blue Venus](Giant_Blue_Venus.md) (25:00) for the first time in normal mode. It can also be unlocked in the [Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane](Forbidden_Scrolls_of_Morbane.md) by casting the [spell](Spells.md) \"everywhere\".

Hyper mode in Mad Forest has a +50% [Gold](Greed.md) bonus.

## Items {#Items}

The following items can be found in this stage:

### Stage items {#Stage_items}

On this stage, 5 unique [passive items](Passive_items.md) can be found.

-  [Skull O\'Maniac](Skull_O'Maniac.md): 6 tilesets, northwest of starting area.
-  [Hollow Heart](Hollow_Heart.md): Between 4 and 5 tilesets, north of starting area.
-  [Spinach](Spinach.md): Up to four may appear with 30%, 20%, and 10% chance for each additional Spinach to spawn.
  - 2 tilesets northeast and 3 tilesets, east of the starting area (guaranteed to spawn).
  - 2 tilesets northeast and 5 tilesets, east of the starting area.
  - 2 tilesets southeast and 4 tilesets, east of the starting area.
  - 2 tilesets southeast and 6 tilesets, east of the starting area.
-  [Pummarola](Pummarola.md): 5 tilesets, south of starting area.
-  [Clover](Clover.md): Up to four may appear with 30%, 20%, and 10% chance for each additional Clover to spawn.
  - 3 tilesets southwest and 4 tilesets, west of the starting area (guaranteed to spawn).
  - 4 tilesets southwest and 2 tilesets, west of the starting area.
  - 4 tilesets southwest and 4 tilesets, west of the starting area.
  - 3 tilesets southwest and 5 tilesets, west of the starting area.

The 4 hidden stage items are also present, if the player has obtained the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md).

-  [Silver Ring](Silver_Ring.md): 7 tilesets, north of the starting area.
-  [Metaglio Right](Metaglio_Right.md): 7 tilesets, east of the starting area.
-  [Gold Ring](Gold_Ring.md): 7 tilesets, south of the starting area.
-  [Metaglio Left](Metaglio_Left.md): 7 tilesets, west of the starting area.

### Coffin {#Coffin}

One  [Coffin](Coffin.md) is located in Mad Forest.

-  [Pugnala Provola](Pugnala_Provola.md): 6 tilesets, northeast of the starting area.
