The **Eggman** appears directly north in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md) near one of the [secret items](Silver_Ring.md) if the player has at least 5,000 [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) total, at least 1,000,000 [gold](Gold_Coin_(currency).md), and has acquired the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md). He acts (and appears) similar to the [Merchant](Merchant.md), but only sells Golden Eggs.

When the player touches the Eggman, a shop will be opened with 2 options: 1 Golden Egg for 10,000 gold, or \"MAX EGGS\", which purchases as many Golden Eggs as the player can afford.

To remove Golden Eggs, see [Healer](Healer.md).
