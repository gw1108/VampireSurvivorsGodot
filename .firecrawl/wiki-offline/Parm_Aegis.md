**Parm Aegis** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by collecting it in any [stage](Stages.md).

Parm Aegis increases [invulnerability time](Invulnerability_Time.md) after taking damage by 50ms per level.

Parm Aegis can be found as a stage item on [The Coop](The_Coop.md), [Ode to Castlevania](Ode_to_Castlevania_(stage).md), and [Emerald Diorama](Emerald_Diorama_(stage).md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Parm Aegis has 5 levels, and the stacking of the duration is additive, therefore making the max duration of invulnerability +250ms.

|         |                                                               |
|---------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                                                   |
| Level 1 | Increases invulnerability time after getting damaged by 50ms. |
| Level 2 | Increases invulnerability time after getting damaged by 50ms. |
| Level 3 | Increases invulnerability time after getting damaged by 50ms. |
| Level 4 | Increases invulnerability time after getting damaged by 50ms. |
| Level 5 | Increases invulnerability time after getting damaged by 50ms. |
