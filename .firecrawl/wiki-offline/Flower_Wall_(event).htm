Flower Wall is a circle-type map event that occurs in Mad Forest.

The Flower Wall event summons a ring of durable  Flower Wall units around the player, which slowly creeps inward. The Flower Walls trap both the player and enemies inside until they are defeated or expire after their set duration.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Flower Walls have high health but deal relatively little damage, so it's often better to break through their encirclement rather than stay trapped and risk taking more damage from other enemies in the wave.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • An event similar to the Flower Wall also appears in Mt.Moonspell and Lake Foscari. However, these stages use the generic Circle map event template, which can spawn any enemy type in a ring formation rather than exclusively Flower Walls.
