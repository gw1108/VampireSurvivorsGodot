**Phiera Der Tuphello** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is one of the starting weapons of  [Pugnala Provola](Pugnala_Provola.md). It is unlocked by surviving 10 minutes as Pugnala Provola.

Its [union](Union.md) is  [Phieraggi](Phieraggi.md), which requires both  [Eight The Sparrow](Eight_The_Sparrow.md) at max level and  [Tirajisú](Tirajisú.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Phiera Der Tuphello shoots a circular red energy bullet in the four ordinal directions of the character. Each bullet can initially hit one enemy before dissipating. The bullets cannot pass through walls.

[Amount](Amount.md) affects all four bullet streams, granting four bullets total per point in Amount.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Gemini (I)](Gemini_(I).md) - Creates counterpart  [Red Muscle](Red_Muscle.md). It levels up as Phiera Der Tuphello does and the angles it shoots at is slightly more vertical, but does not benefit from [Limit Breaking](Limit_Break.md).
-  [Iron Blue Will (VII)](Iron_Blue_Will_(VII).md) - Allows Phiera Der Tuphello\'s projectiles to bounce off of enemies once, occasionally have infinite pierce, and pass through walls. Upon bouncing, the bullet completely reverses its horizontal and vertical speed returning to the direction it came from.
-  [Heart of Fire (XIX)](Heart_of_Fire_(XIX).md) - Allows Phiera Der Tuphello\'s projectiles to explode upon contact with an enemy, dealing half its base damage in an area.

## Levels {#Levels}

Phiera Der Tuphello has 8 levels. At max level, Phiera Der Tuphello has +10 [Base Damage](Base_damage.md), +50% [Projectile Speed](Speed.md), +2 [Amount](Amount.md), and +6 [Pierce](Pierce.md).

|---------|----------------------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                                  |
| Level 1 | *Fires quickly in four fixed directions.*    |
| Level 2 | *Fires 1 more projectile.*                   |
| Level 3 | *Passes through 2 more enemies.*             |
| Level 4 | *Fires 1 more projectile.*                   |
| Level 5 | *Base Damage up by 5.*                       |
| Level 6 | *Passes through 2 more enemies.*             |
| Level 7 | *Base Damage up by 5. Base Speed up by 50%.* |
| Level 8 | *Passes through 2 more enemies.*             |
