**Flower Wall** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), with additional appearances in [Mt.Moonspell](Mt.Moonspell.md) and [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md) .

In Mad Forest, it first appears during the [**Flower Wall**](Flower_Wall_(event).md) [map event](Map_event.md) and later reappears as a normal enemy in later waves. In Mt.Moonspell and Lake Foscari, it appears during **[Circle](Circle.md)** map events and as a special enemy that spawns at specific, fixed locations on the map.

Flower Wall\'s [health scales with the player\'s level](HP_x_Level.md), except for the *Foscari* variant. Movement speed varies by type: the base variant moves very slowly, the *Foscari* variant is as fast as most enemies, and both *Moonspell* variants are immobile. Knockback resistance also differs: the base and *Foscari* variants take reduced knockback, *Moonspell 1* is semi-resistant, and *Moonspell 2* is fully resistant.
