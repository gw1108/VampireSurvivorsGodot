Collection

From Vampire Survivors Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Collection is a menu in the game that lists all of the items that the player has used in their game so far. The items listed in the Collection include weapons, passive items, pickups, relics, and arcanas that are used by the player at least once in their save file. This menu can be accessed by clicking the "COLLECTION" button on the left side of the game's main menu.

By default, most items that haven't been used yet are marked with a question mark, and clicking on a locked item slot will indicate that the item is unknown and not yet found. Furthermore, the background of the question mark slots is marked differently, depending on the type of the item (for example, pickups are highlighted with a grassy background, relics are highlighted with a pink background, etc). However, there are some items that won't have their dedicated slot in the Collection shown until they are used, which is why the total indicated number of used and unused items in the Collection menu would increase.

If the player buys the Seal powerup, they can select which items they could disable in their runs, depending on the number of Seals that have bought. Though, some items, such as relics and arcanas, cannot be disabled.

The following is a listing of items as displayed in the game's "Collection" menu, with the Filter option set to "By Version". To date, the total number of items to be collected across the game, with all of its DLCs, is 486 items.

Note: You can click or tap on any of the table cells below in this wiki page to keep track of what you have collected.
