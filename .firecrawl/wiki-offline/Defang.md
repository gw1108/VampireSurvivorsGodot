**Defang** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It renders certain [enemies](Enemies.md) unable to harm the player.

## Effects {#Effects}

Defang turns a percentage of enemies from each spawn interval unable to deal damage to the player. Enemies affected by Defang are visually identified by a green appearance, which the player can disable in the settings.

Some enemies cannot be Defanged. This includes, but is not limited to: wave bosses, enemies from wave events, and special enemies such as [Atlanteans](Atlantean.md).

The Defang [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases the quantity of enemies unable to deal damage by 3% per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, 15% of enemies are defanged. It initially costs 10, and is unlocked by collecting  [Antidote](Antidote.md) in [Whiteout](Whiteout.md).

|       |        |
|-------|--------|
| Level | Defang |
| 1     | +3%    |
| 2     | +6%    |
| 3     | +9%    |
| 4     | +12%   |
| 5     | +15%   |

## From items {#From_items}

The weapons  [Time Warp](Time_Warp.md),  [Gaze of Gaea](Gaze_of_Gaea.md), and  [Embrace of Gaea](Embrace_of_Gaea.md) have a chance to Defang enemies. The chance of it happening is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md).
