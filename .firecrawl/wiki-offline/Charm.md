**Charm** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It buffs the spawn quantity of [enemies](Enemies.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Charm increases the quantity of enemies in each [wave](Enemies.md#Waves) by a flat amount; the amount of which corresponds to the current value of the player\'s Charm stat.

It is added last after other bonuses that affect enemy spawn quantity, such as  [Curse](Curse.md). This results in waves always spawning a fixed value of additional enemies which may cause the 500 maximum enemy quantity limit to be reached and constantly filled.

The Charm [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases the quantity of enemies spawned by 20 per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, it increases enemy quantity by 100. It initially costs 10,000, and is unlocked by collecting  [Apoplexy](Apoplexy.md) in [Bat Country](Bat_Country.md).

| 3     | +60   |
| 4     | +80   |
| 5     | +100  |

## From items {#From_items}

Charm\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is  [Karoma\'s Mana](Karoma's_Mana.md), which provides +10 Charm per level, up to a total of +50 Charm at level 5.

 [Gold Finger](Gold_Finger.md) sets the player\'s Charm at +500, overriding the player\'s pre-existing Charm stat throughout its duration.
