# Growth {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

**Growth** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects how much experience  [Experience Gems](Experience_Gem.md) are worth.

The player has 100% base Growth before modifications. The Growth in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Growth, meaning that Growth stat of +30% equals to 130% total Growth, for example. Growth modifications from [character](Character.md) bonuses, [PowerUps](PowerUps.md) and [items](Item.md) stack additively.

At level 20 and 40, all characters gain +100% Growth to offset a formula change that makes leveling up harder by making the increased experience requirement for each level higher, which applied retroactively and makes it especially hard at the first level it changes. This bonus is reverted at level 21 and 41, applying a -100% Growth penalty.

There is no upper limit to how much Growth the player may have.

Growth also affects:

- Base Damage of  [Wind Whip](Wind_Whip.md) 
- Base Damage, Speed, Area and Duration of  [Vol Luminatio](Vol_Luminatio.md) 
- Charge ability of  [Charlotte Aulin](Charlotte_Aulin.md)

The Growth [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases Growth by 3% per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, it provides +15% Growth. It initially costs 900.

|      |        |
|------|--------|
| Rank | Growth |
| 1    | +3%    |
| 2    | +6%    |
| 3    | +9%    |
| 4    | +12%   |
| 5    | +15%   |

## From items {#From_items}

Growth\'s associated [passive item](Passive_item.md) is  [Crown](Crown.md), which provides +8% Growth per level, up to a maximum of +40% Growth at level 5.

 [Academy Badge](Academy_Badge.md)  has 6 levels and provides -3% Growth every level after 1, for a maximum of -15% Growth.

 [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) permanently increases Growth by 1% for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

 [Game Killer (0)](Game_Killer_(0).md) multiplies experience gain by 0, effectively halting level ups. Growth is not directly affected and can be used for other calculations like the experience gem damage dealt with this Arcana.

 [Wicked Season (XIII)](Wicked_Season_(XIII).md) doubles Growth in certain 10 second intervals, and gives +0.5% Growth every level.

## From darkana {#From_darkana}

 [Crystal Cries (XII)](Crystal_Cries_(XII).md) generates  [Crystallized Souls](Crystallized_Soul.md) when killing frozen enemies that can be collected to slightly increase  [Max Health](Max_Health.md),  [Recovery](Recovery.md) and Growth.

## From characters {#From_characters}

### Base Game {#Base_Game}

-  [Imelda](Imelda_Belpaese.md) gains +10% Growth every 5 levels, up to +30% at level 15.
-  [Gallo](Iguana_Gallo_Valletto.md) gains +10% Growth every 5 levels, up to +50% at level 25.
-  [Gains](Gains_Boros.md) gains +2% Growth every level, with no cap.
-  [Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md) gains +1% Growth every level, with no cap.
-  [Rose](Rose_De_Infernas.md) starts with -80% Growth. She gains +10% Growth every 12 levels from level 36 to 84 and +15% Growth on levels 96 and 108.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell}

-  [McCoy-Oni](McCoy-Oni.md) starts with +20% Growth.

### Tides of the Foscari {#Tides_of_the_Foscari}

-  [Eleanor](Eleanor_Uziron.md) starts with 25% Growth.

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Crewmate](Crewmate_Dino.md) has a chance to gain a random amount of Growth every time a weapon or passive item is upgraded to its maximum level, with no cap.
-  [Scientist](Scientist_Mina.md) can select to gain +1% Growth per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Growth per level.

### Operation Guns {#Operation_Guns}

-  [Ariana](Ariana.md) starts with +10% Growth.
-  [Lucia](Lucia_Zero.md) gains +5% Growth after [reviving](Revival.md), with no cap.

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Blackmore](Blackmore.md) can select to gain +1% Growth per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Growth per level.
-  [Chaos](Chaos.md) starts with -99% Growth.
-  [Fleaman](Fleaman.md) starts with  [Game Killer (0)](Game_Killer_(0).md), making him having effectively no Growth.
-  [Hector](Hector.md) starts with +20% Growth.
-  [Joachim](Joachim_Armster.md) can select to gain +1% Growth per level.
-  [Leon](Leon_Belmont.md) gains +10% Growth upon obtaining the  [Vampire Killer](Vampire_Killer.md).
-  [Lisa](Lisa_Tepes.md) gains +1% Growth every level, with no cap.

## From stages {#From_stages}

The [hurry mode](Hurry_mode.md) of all [stages](Stage.md), except [Holy Forbidden](Holy_Forbidden.md) and [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md), give a +25% experience gain.

- [Bat Country](Bat_Country.md) has a -75% experience gain penalty.
