# Revival {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

**Revival** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. Each point of Revival a player has allows them to revive once upon death with 50% of their  [Max Health](Max_Health.md).

The player has 0 base Revivals before modifications. Bonuses to Revival are flat bonuses and thus stack additively.

There is no upper limit to how many Revivals the player may have.

## Effects {#Effects}

If the player dies and has at least 1 Revival, instead of getting the usual \"Quit\" button to return to the main menu, they will instead be greeted with a \"Revive\" button. An animation is played and the player resumes control of their character exactly where they left off, with 50% of their  [Max Health](Max_Health.md) also being restored. The player is additionally given a large amount of invulnerability frames. The health gained upon reviving counts as healing, causing it to interact with effects related to restoring health, such as  [Soul Eater](Soul_Eater.md) and  [Sarabande of Healing (VI)](Sarabande_of_Healing_(VI).md).

If Revivals are carried to the end of the game, the player will still be allowed to Revive after being killed by  [The Reaper](The_Reaper.md). Every Revival used like this will add [gold](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) to the bonus gold for completing the stage, at +100 gold per revival, increasing by 100 for every additional revival used. For example, if the player has 3 revivals and use them all on The Reaper, they will die and get the normal 500 bonus gold, then revive and die and get an extra 100, then an extra 200, then an extra 300, and end the round with 1100 bonus gold. The scaling gold increase caps at 400 and any additional unused Revivals will only give 100 bonus gold.

Revival also affects:

- Base Damage and [Amount](Amount.md) of  [Phieraggi](Phieraggi.md)
- Base Damage of  [Meal Ticket](Meal_Ticket.md)

## From items {#From_items}

- Revival\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is  [Tirajisú](Tirajisú.md), which provides 1 additional revival per level, up to a maximum of 2 at level 2.
-  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Revival by 0.1 for the character that collected it.
- [Survarot](Survarot.md) cards featuring  [Pasqualina Belpaese](Pasqualina_Belpaese.md) provide +1 Revival upon selection. The card\'s edition-exclusive skill (or any edition card as a random bonus) can grant up to +3 Revivals after reaching certain kill thresholds.
-  [Academy Badge](Academy_Badge.md)  has 6 levels and provides +1 Revival at levels 3 and 5, for a maximum of +2 Revival.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  may provide 0.2 Revival whenever it activates, requiring 5 such bonuses to give +1 Revival, with no cap.
-  [Weapon Power-Up](Weapon_Power-Up.md)  provides +1 Revival on pickup.

\

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) provides +3 Revivals when chosen and gives +10%  [Max Health](Max_Health.md), +1  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md), and +5%  [Might](Might.md),  [Area](Area.md),  [Duration](Duration.md), and  [Speed](Speed.md) per Revival consumed.
-  [Hidden Anathema (III)](Hidden_Anathema_(III).md) may provide additional Revivals when obtaining food-based [Pickups](Pickups.md), with no cap.
