**Imelda Belpaese** is one of the playable [characters](Characters.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. Her starting weapon is  [Magic Wand](Magic_Wand.md).

## Unlocking {#Unlocking}

She is unlocked by default. She needs to be purchased for 10, which scales with other characters purchased.

### Unblinded Imelda Belpaese {#Unblinded_Imelda_Belpaese}

Completing the Adventure [World of Light and Dark](Adventures.md#World_of_Light_and_Dark) for the first time unlocks an alternative [skin](Mindbender.md) named Unblinded Imelda Belpaese.

Alternatively, Unblinded Imelda can be unlocked in the  [Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane](Forbidden_Scrolls_of_Morbane.md) by casting the [spell](Spells.md) \"unblindimelda\".

## Passive bonuses {#Passive_bonuses}

Imelda gains +10%  [Growth](Growth.md) every 5 levels until level 15. The maximum Growth gained this way is +30%.

The following table shows how much Growth she gains by level:

|        |        |
|--------|--------|
| Levels | Growth |
| 1-4    | 0%     |
| 5-9    | +10%   |
| 10-14  | +20%   |
| 15+    | +30%   |

### Unblinded Imelda Belpaese {#Unblinded_Imelda_Belpaese_2}

Compared to Imelda, Unblinded Imelda starts with +40%  [Speed](Speed.md), +40%  [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md), and -10%  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md). She also starts with a level 5  [Empty Tome](Empty_Tome.md).
