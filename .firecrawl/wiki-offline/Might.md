# Might {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

**Might** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects the damage output of all [weapons](Weapons.md).

The player has 100% base Might before modifications, such as the characters bonuses or [PowerUps](PowerUps.md). The Might in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Might, meaning that Might stat of +30% equals to 130% total Might, for example. Might modifications from [characters](Characters.md), [PowerUps](PowerUps.md), [passive items](Passive_items.md), and [arcanas](Arcanas.md) stack additively.

The upper limit of total Might after character, PowerUp, and item modifications is 1000%, or +900% in the stats panel.

- Might has a further multiplier of 1.25x on  [SpellStrike](SpellStrike.md) .
- Might also affects the charge ability of  [Quincy Morris](Quincy_Morris.md) .
-  [Stanley](Stanley.md)  gets +1  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md) per +10% Might.

The Might [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases Might by 5% per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, it provides +25% Might. It initially costs 200.

|      |       |
|------|-------|
| Rank | Might |
| 1    | +5%   |
| 2    | +10%  |
| 3    | +15%  |
| 4    | +20%  |
| 5    | +25%  |

## From items {#From_items}

Might\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is  [Spinach](Spinach.md), which provides +10% Might per level, up to a maximum of +50% Might at level 5.

-  [Torrona\'s Box](Torrona's_Box.md) also provides +4%, then +3% per level, up to a maximum of +25% at level 8.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting a Might stat bonus of +4% upon activation.
-  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Might by +1% for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) provides +5% Might when chosen per  [Revival](Revival.md) consumed.
-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) provides +1 flat damage (displayed as Might in stats menu) for every 10 health less than the  [Max Health](Max_Health.md).
-  [Silent Old Sanctuary (XX)](Silent_Old_Sanctuary_(XX).md) provides +20% Might when chosen per active weapon slot left empty.
