**Vento Sacro** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Zi\'Assunta Belpaese](Zi'Assunta_Belpaese.md). It is unlocked by surviving 15 minutes as Zi\'Assunta Belpaese.

Its [union](Union.md) is  [Fuwalafuwaloo](Fuwalafuwaloo.md), which also requires  [Bloody Tear](Bloody_Tear.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Vento Sacro creates multiple slashes horizontally in front of the character, damaging all enemies in the area.

 [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md) increases its overall fire rate when moving; when the character stops, it initiates the default  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md).  [Speed](Speed.md) makes the slashes go further out horizontally.  [Duration](Duration.md) increases how long the hitbox lingers.

By default, it is capable of dealing critical hits, which deal 2x damage and have a 5% chance to occur. This chance is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md).

Vento Sacro gains bonus damage from walking nonstop. It caps at 5 flat damage multiplied by level, up to 40 flat damage at Level 8. The actual bonus comes from distance walked divided by 200,000 up to the cap. This bonus will reset once the character stops moving. The unit for distance walked is slightly arbitrary and is not as big as it seems in the game. [Limit Break](Limit_Break.md) levels do not further increase the cap. The weapon also creates a trail of light blue particle effects that arcs upward. The longer they last indicates the bonus being higher.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Slash (XVI)](Slash_(XVI).md) - Ventro Sacro\'s critical hit damage multiplier is doubled from 2x to 4x.
-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) - Allows Ventro Sacro\'s slashes to deal extra flat damage based on  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md).
-  [Pale Diamond Incursion (V)](Pale_Diamond_Incursion_(V).md) - Aims at the nearest enemy horizontally, and gains  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) bonus based on  [Speed](Speed.md).
