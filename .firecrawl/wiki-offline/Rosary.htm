The Rosary is an item pickup in Vampire Survivors that has a chance to drop from light sources. It can only drop after the character reaches level 8, and its drop rate is affected by Luck.

Collecting the Rosary for the first time unlocks the  Cross.

Picking up 33 Rosaries will unlock the Laborratory stage.

Effects

[edit | edit source]

Picking up the Rosary instantly kills all enemies on the screen.

Rosaries can attack and defeat the otherwise intangible  Stalkers and  Drowners in Dairy Plant, Gallo Tower, and Cappella Magna before the 30:00 mark, causing them to drop  Treasure Chests. However, some enemies, such as  The Reaper, are resistant or immune to the Rosary's effect.

Weird Souls Purifier

[edit | edit source]

 Seraphic Cry gains a special effect that turns Rosaries into Weird Souls Purifiers. This effect triggers the Rosary's effect four times while also turning enemies into experience gems. Seraphic Cry also multiplies the drop weight of Rosaries by 3, increasing their weight from 1 to 3.
