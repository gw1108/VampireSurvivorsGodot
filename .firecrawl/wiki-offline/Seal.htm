Seal

From Vampire Survivors Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Seal is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. It affects the amount of times the player can remove an item choice before the run in the collection menu.

Seal effectively allows the player to  Banish an item from level up choices or a pickup from light sources before entering a stage. It cannot be used on relics[1], Arcanas, the  Experience Gem,  Gold Coin,  Coin Bag,  Rich Coin Bag,  Treasure Chest,  Friendship Amulet,  Golden Egg, weapons acquired from  Gemini (I), passive items acquired from the Yellow Sign, and  Academy Badge.

After buying any of the Seal PowerUps, and having installed at least one DLC, the MEGA SEAL module is also unlocked in the collection menu, allowing the player to seal all weapons in a specific version category at once, without taking up any normal Seal slots. However, it is not possible to "un-seal" a specific weapon sealed by the MEGA SEAL.

Each Seal Powerup has a maximum rank of 10. There are four Seal PowerUps: Seal I, Seal II, Seal III, and Seal All. Seal I adds 1 Seal per rank, Seal II adds 2, Seal III adds 3 and Seal All adds 4. Each one initially costs 10,000. Once all four Seal Powerups are maxed out, the usable Seal slots in the collection becomes ∞ (infinite).

Unlocking

[edit | edit source]

Each PowerUp is unlocked by these achievements:

PowerUp Unlocking
Seal I Banish 10 or more weapons in a single run.
Seal II Banish 20 or more weapons in a single run.
Seal III Banish 40 or more weapons in a single run.
Seal All Banish 80 or more weapons in a single run.

If no DLCs are owned the amount of weapons that can be banished through the level up choices will be lower than 80. To remedy this the player has to go to the collection and seal weapons that normally wouldn't show up in the level up screen like for example evolutions, around 20 seals will be needed to cover the missing weapons.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Individually sealed items contribute as Banishes for Seal unlocks. However, items sealed by MEGA SEAL do not contribute to the unlocks.
  • When an evolved or united weapon is sealed, the base weapon(s) cannot transform into them without a  Super Candybox II Turbo.
  • Sealing an item will not prevent it from appearing as a starting item, appearing on the stage or appearing in Item Selectors (e.g.  Candybox). Sealed items obtained with those methods will behave as if the player Banished the item at level 1, preventing it from being leveled up.
  • MEGA SEAL is able to seal  Gemini (I)'s counterpart weapons, but cannot seal any other type of unsealable item.
