**Knife** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Gennaro Belpaese](Gennaro_Belpaese.md), and one of the optional starting weapons of  [Zephyr](Zephyr.md) . It is unlocked by default.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Thousand Edge](Thousand_Edge.md), which requires  [Bracer](Bracer.md).

As a vampire-killing tool, it can be picked from  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md) .

## Effects {#Effects}

Knife throws projectiles in the direction the player is moving or last moved. It ignores  [Duration](Duration.md) and has limited benefit from  [Area](Area.md).

Multiple knives from  [Amount](Amount.md) are fired in short volleys in parallel. The firing [Interval](Interval.md) is decreased on levels 4, 6 and 8, grouping each volley of knives closer together. Each knife can initially hit one enemy before dissipating. They cannot pass through walls.

While playing with a keyboard, the knives can only be shot out in eight cardinal and ordinal directions. When playing with a mouse or gamepad, the knives can be fired in any direction.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Iron Blue Will (VII)](Iron_Blue_Will_(VII).md) - Allows Knife\'s projectiles to bounce off enemies up to three times, occasionally have infinite [Pierce](Pierce.md), and pass through walls.
-  [Slash (XVI)](Slash_(XVI).md) - Allows Knife to critical hit. Its critical hits deal 6x damage and have 30% chance to occur. This chance is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md).
-  [Pale Diamond Incursion (V)](Pale_Diamond_Incursion_(V).md) - Aims at the nearest enemy instead of the faced direction, and gains  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) bonus based on  [Speed](Speed.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Knife has 8 levels. At max level, Knife has +10 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +5 [Amount](Amount.md), +2 [Pierce](Pierce.md), and -0.06 seconds of [Interval](Interval.md).

|         |                                                              |
|---------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                                                  |
| Level 1 | Fires quickly in the faced direction.                        |
| Level 2 | Fires 1 more projectile.                                     |
| Level 3 | Fires 1 more projectile. Base Damage up by 5.                |
| Level 4 | Fires 1 more projectile.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-interval-1)^     |
| Level 5 | Passes through 1 more enemy.                                 |
| Level 6 | Fires 1 more projectile.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-interval-1)^     |
| Level 7 | Fires 1 more projectile. Base Damage up by 5.                |
| Level 8 | Passes through 1 more enemy.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-interval-1)^ |
