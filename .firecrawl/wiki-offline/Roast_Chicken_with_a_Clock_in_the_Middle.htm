Roast Chicken with a Clock in the Middle is a relic located in The Coop. Once collected, it permanently unlocks the ability to speed up gameplay.

After the player has obtained this relic, a speed modifier button will be added to the left of the pause button. By default, the game will play in ×1 speed. If the player clicks on the speed modifier button once, the game will play in ×1.5 speed. Doing so again will make the game play in ×2 speed. Clicking once more will return the game to ×1 speed.

  • PC players can also use the Tab key to modify their gameplay speed instead of needing to use the cursor to click on the speed modifier button, while console players can use the Left and Right Bumpers to decrease and increase the speed, respectively.

Obtaining

[edit | edit source]

Roast Chicken with a Clock in the Middle is located at the farthest eastern end of The Coop. Once collected, it will no longer appear on the map.

Alternatively, Roast Chicken with a Clock in the Middle can be unlocked in the  Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane by casting the spell "yeoldeshoppe".

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • If the player uses this relic and has Hurry Mode enabled in the stage that they are currently playing, time-based events (such as enemy spawns) will double on top of the speed that this relic provides. For example, if the player has ×2 speed enabled from this relic with Hurry Mode, the timer and all time-based events will have ×4 speed, but everything else happening in the game that is not a time-based event (such as player movement, enemy movement, and weapon projectiles) will be at ×2 speed.
  • This relic is particularly disabled in Carlo Cart, regardless if the player sealed this relic in their Collection or not.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • This is a reference to "Rubber Chicken With a Pulley in the Middle" item from the Monkey Island series.
    • The spell "yeoldeshoppe" is a reference to "Ye Olde Rubber-Chicken-With-a-Pulley-in-the-Middle Shoppe" from the first game in the series, The Secret of Monkey Island.
  • Its icon is a reference to a kitchen timer with a red bottom and a chicken on top.
