Armor (stat)

Armor is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. Each point of Armor the player has will reduce all incoming damage by 1 and increases the damage from retaliatory effects by 10%.

The player has 0 base Armor before modifications, such as the characters bonuses or PowerUps. Armor is additional and the retaliatory damage bonus stack additively with itself and multiplicatively with  Might.

There is no upper limit to how much Armor the player can have, but the retaliatory bonus damage is capped at +500% (50 Armor). Also, regardless the amount of Armor the player has, the damage taken from an enemy will always be at least 1.

Armor also affects Base Damage of  Iron Ball  and  Wrecking Ball .

 Probotector  gains 10% Might per +1 Armor.

From items

[edit | edit source]

Armor's associated passive item is  Armor (item), which increases Armor by 1 per level, up to a maximum of +5 Armor at level 5.

  •  Centralis Custos  provides +10 Armor when the player character takes damage from enemies.
  •  Trinum Custodem  provides a passive +10 Armor and an additional +10 Armor when the player character takes damage from enemies.
  •  Paranormal Scan  has a chance of granting an Armor stat bonus of +0.2 upon activation.
  •  Golden Eggs can permanently increase Armor by +0.1 for the character that collected them.

From arcana

[edit | edit source]
  •  Awake (IV) provides +1 Armor per  Revival consumed.

  •  Toastie gains +65520 Armor upon reaching level 200.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Armor bonuses reduce how much damage enemies deal to you. Even if a single point sounds small, bonuses quickly add up as each point is more valuable than previous.
  • On most characters the maximum armor attainable is 14 points, 5 from the passive item, 3 from the PowerUp and 6 from reviving with the Awake Arcana (3 revives are gained from the Arcana itself, 2 from Tiragisú and 1 from the PowerUp).
    • Leda and Divano, with their passive bonuses granting up to +5 Armor, can have a maximum of 19 points of Armor.
    • Toastie is capable of having the highest Armor in the game, gaining a massive +65520 Armor bonus upon reaching level 200.
