Fever

From Vampire Survivors Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Fever is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. It increases the duration of  Gold Fever and  Gold Finger.

Effects

[edit | edit source]

Fever multiplies the duration of Gold Fever and Gold Finger, making them last longer and potentially achieve a higher coin count or better rewards respectively.

Note that this multiplier is separate from the Gold Fever cap and duration bonus of  Big Trouser and  Sammy .

From Items

[edit | edit source]

Fever's associated passive item is  Karoma's Mana, which provides +10% Fever per level, up to a total of +50% Fever at level 5.

From Survarot

[edit | edit source]
  •  FOIL Skill: Overhealing by +16 provides +1% Fever.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • Fever,  Defang,  Charm,  Preserve, Invulnerability Time, and Thorns are the only character stats in the game that are not displayed on the stat panel.
    • Fever, Invulnerability Time, and Thorns are the only stats without a respective PowerUp.
  • Fever was accidentally leaked as a stat in all languages besides English for the erroneous description of  Brave Story, displaying the description of the relic that unlocks it as a PowerUp.
