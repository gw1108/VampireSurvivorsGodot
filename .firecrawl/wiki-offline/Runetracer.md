**Runetracer** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Pasqualina Belpaese](Pasqualina_Belpaese.md). It is unlocked by surviving 5 minutes as Pasqualina Belpaese.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [NO FUTURE](NO_FUTURE.md), which requires  [Armor](Armor_(item).md).

As a vampire-killing tool, it can be picked from  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md) .

## Effects {#Effects}

Runetracer fires slow-moving diamond-shaped runes in random directions, each has a different color trail. The runes have infinite pierce and bounce off the edges of the screen and obstacles, damaging enemies they hit. Its nature makes it scale up with all stat bonuses well, which also means it needs lots of different stat bonuses to become strong.

Runetracer has a 0.5 seconds [Hitbox Delay](Weapon.md#Stats), meaning that the same enemy cannot be hit more often than every half a second by the same rune. However, because all active delays are reset simultaneously as soon as any one elapses or bounces off a wall, some enemies may get hit more often.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Twilight Requiem (II)](Twilight_Requiem_(II).md) - Allows the Runtracer\'s projectiles to explode when they expire. Damage is based on [Curse](Curse.md).
-  [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md) - Gives Runetracer a 25% chance to [freeze](Freeze.md) enemies. This chance is affected by [Luck](Luck.md#Chance_based_weapon_effects).
-  [Out of Bounds (XII)](Out_of_Bounds_(XII).md) - When paired with [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md), enemies frozen by the Runetracer\'s projectiles will explode.
-  [Crystal Cries (XII)](Crystal_Cries_(XII).md) - When paired with [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md), enemies frozen by the Runetracer\'s projectiles will drop Crystalized Souls when killed.
-  [Pale Diamond Incursion (V)](Pale_Diamond_Incursion_(V).md) - Runetracer aims at nearest enemy instead of in random directions, and gains  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) bonus based on  [Speed](Speed.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Runetracer has 8 levels. At max level, Runetracer has +20 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +40% [Projectile Speed](Speed.md), +2 [Amount](Amount.md), and +1 second to [Duration](Duration.md).

|  |  |
|----|----|
| Level | Description |
| Level 1 | Passes through enemies, bounces around. |
| Level 2 | Base Damage up by 5. Base Speed up by 20%. |
| Level 3 | Effect lasts 0.3 seconds longer.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-duration-1)^ Base Damage up by 5. |
| Level 4 | Fires 1 more projectile. |
| Level 5 | Base Damage up by 5. Base Speed up by 20%. |
| Level 6 | Effect lasts 0.3 seconds longer.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-duration-1)^ Base Damage up by 5. |
| Level 7 | Fires 1 more projectile. |
| Level 8 | Effect lasts 0.5 seconds longer. |
