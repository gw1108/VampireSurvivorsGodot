**Antonio Belpaese** is one of the playable [characters](Characters.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. He is the character that every player begins with. His starting weapon is the  [Whip](Whip.md).

## Passive bonuses {#Passive_bonuses}

Antonio starts with +20  [Max Health](Max_Health.md) and +1  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md). He gains +10%  [Might](Might.md) every 10 levels until level 50. The maximum Might gained this way is +50%.

The following table shows how much Might he gains by level:

|        |       |
|--------|-------|
| Levels | Might |
| 1-9    | 0%    |
| 10-19  | +10%  |
| 20-29  | +20%  |
| 30-39  | +30%  |
| 40-49  | +40%  |
| 50+    | +50%  |
