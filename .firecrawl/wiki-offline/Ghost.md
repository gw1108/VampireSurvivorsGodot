> "
>
> A pure fabrication, the idle fantasy of brains gripped with fever or boredom. There's simply no such thing as ghosts, and the mere suggestion is to be mocked as ridiculous. These particular apparitions are probably just a trick of the light. Tricks of the light can have hit points too!
>
> **--- [Ars Gouda](Ars_Gouda.md), p. 11**

**Ghost** is an enemy primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md), and [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), with additional appearances in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Whiteout](Whiteout.md), [Astral Stair](Astral_Stair.md), [Tiny Bridge](Tiny_Bridge.md), and [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md).

In Inlaid Library, Gallo Tower, Moongolow, Whiteout, Astral Stair, and Tiny Bridge, it appears exclusively as normal enemies. In Mad Forest, it spawns both as a normal enemy and during [**Ghost Swarm**](Ghost_Swarm.md) [map events](Map_event.md). In Boss Rash, it appears only during Ghost Swarm events. In Eudaimonia Machine, it is summoned by [The Directer](The_Directer.md) as a special enemy during the second phase of the stage.

Ghost is semi-resistant to [knockback](Knockback.md), and the *Swarm* variant only moves in a [fixed direction](Fixed_Direction.md).
