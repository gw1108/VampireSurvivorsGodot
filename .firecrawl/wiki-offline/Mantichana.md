**Mantichana** is an enemy primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), with additional appearances in [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md) and [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md) .

In Mad Forest, it first appears as a [boss](Boss.md) and later reappears as a normal enemy in later waves. In Boss Rash, it appears exclusively as a boss. In Lake Foscari, it appears during **[Circle](Circle.md)** [map events](Map_events.md) and also as a special enemy that spawns at a specific, fixed location on the map.

Mantichana is semi-resistant to [knockback](Knockback.md). Its boss and *Foscari* variants have [health that scales with the player\'s level](HP_x_Level.md), with the boss variant also being partially resistant to [freeze](Freeze.md).
