**Candelabrador** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *Vampire Survivors*. It is unlocked by getting [Santa Water](Santa_Water.md) to Level 4.

Candelabrador increases [Area](Area.md) by 10% per level.

Candelabrador is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Axe](Axe.md), [Death Spiral](Death_Spiral.md). It is also required for the evolution of [Four Seasons](Four_Seasons.md), [Godai Shuffle](Godai_Shuffle.md), in which Candelabrador must also be fully leveled.

Candelabrador can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), and [Mt.Moonspell](Mt.Moonspell.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

| Level 2 | Base area up by 10%                  |
