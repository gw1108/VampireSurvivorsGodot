**Tirajisú** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by surviving for 20 minutes as [Krochi Freetto](Krochi_Freetto.md).

Tirajisú increases [Revival](Revival.md) by 1 per level.

Tirajisú is required for the [evolution and union](Evolution.md) of [Phiera Der Tuphello](Phiera_Der_Tuphello.md) and [Eight The Sparrow](Eight_The_Sparrow.md), [Phieraggi](Phieraggi.md).

Tirajisú can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Cappella Magna](Cappella_Magna.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md) and [Ode to Castlevania](Ode_to_Castlevania_(stage).md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Tirajisú has 2 levels. At max level, Tirajisú provides +2 [Revival](Revival.md).

|         |                                   |
|---------|-----------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                       |
| Level 1 | \"Revives once with 50% health.\" |
| Level 2 | Adds 1 Revival.                   |
