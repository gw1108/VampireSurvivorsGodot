**Roast Chicken with a Clock in the Middle** is a [relic](Relics.md) located in [The Coop](The_Coop.md). Once collected, it permanently unlocks the ability to speed up gameplay.

After the player has obtained this relic, a speed modifier button will be added to the left of the pause button. By default, the game will play in ×1 speed. If the player clicks on the speed modifier button once, the game will play in ×1.5 speed. Doing so again will make the game play in ×2 speed. Clicking once more will return the game to ×1 speed.

- PC players can also use the `Tab` key to modify their gameplay speed instead of needing to use the cursor to click on the speed modifier button, while console players can use the `Left and Right Bumpers` to decrease and increase the speed, respectively.

## Obtaining {#Obtaining}

Roast Chicken with a Clock in the Middle is located at the farthest eastern end of [The Coop](The_Coop.md). Once collected, it will no longer appear on the map.

Alternatively, Roast Chicken with a Clock in the Middle can be unlocked in the  [Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane](Forbidden_Scrolls_of_Morbane.md) by casting the [spell](Spells.md) \"yeoldeshoppe\".

## Notes {#Notes}

- If the player uses this relic and has [Hurry Mode](Hurry_Mode.md) enabled in the stage that they are currently playing, time-based events (such as enemy spawns) will double on top of the speed that this relic provides. For example, if the player has ×2 speed enabled from this relic with Hurry Mode, the timer and all time-based events will have ×4 speed, but everything else happening in the game that is not a time-based event (such as player movement, enemy movement, and weapon projectiles) will be at ×2 speed.
- This relic is particularly disabled in [Carlo Cart](Carlo_Cart.md), regardless if the player [sealed](Seal.md) this relic in their [Collection](Collection.md) or not.
