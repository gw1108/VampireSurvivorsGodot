**Eight The Sparrow** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is one of the starting weapons of  [Pugnala Provola](Pugnala_Provola.md). It is unlocked by surviving 15 minutes as Pugnala Provola.

Its [union](Union.md) is  [Phieraggi](Phieraggi.md), which requires both  [Phiera Der Tuphello](Phiera_Der_Tuphello.md) at max level and  [Tirajisú](Tirajisú.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Eight The Sparrow shoots a circular blue energy bullet toward each of the four corners of the screen. Each bullet can initially hit one enemy before dissipating. The bullets cannot pass through walls.

Each point in [Amount](Amount.md) grants an additional bullet in all four directions.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Gemini (I)](Gemini_(I).md) - Creates counterpart  [Twice Upon a Time](Twice_Upon_a_Time.md). It levels up as Eight The Sparrow does and the angles it shoots at is slightly more vertical, but does not benefit from [Limit Breaking](Limit_Break.md).
-  [Iron Blue Will (VII)](Iron_Blue_Will_(VII).md) - Allows Eight The Sparrow\'s projectiles to bounce off of enemies once, occasionally have infinite pierce, and pass through walls. Upon bouncing, the bullet completely reverses its horizontal and vertical speed returning to the direction it came from.
-  [Out of Bounds (XII)](Out_of_Bounds_(XII).md) - When paired with [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md), enemies frozen by Eight The Sparrow\'s projectiles will explode.
-  [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md) - Gives Eight The Sparrow a 25% chance to [freeze](Freeze.md) enemies. This chance is affected by [Luck](Luck.md#Chance_based_weapon_effects).

<!-- -->

-  [Crystal Cries (XII)](Crystal_Cries_(XII).md) - When paired with [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md), enemies frozen by Eight The Sparrow\'s projectiles will drop Crystalized Souls when killed.

## Levels {#Levels}

Eight The Sparrow has 8 levels. At max level, Eight The Sparrow has +10 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +50% [Projectile Speed](Speed.md), +2 [Amount](Amount.md), and +6 [Pierce](Pierce.md).

|         |                                              |
|---------|----------------------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                                  |
| Level 1 | *Fires quickly in four fixed directions.*    |
| Level 2 | *Fires 1 more projectile.*                   |
| Level 3 | *Passes through 2 more enemies.*             |
| Level 4 | *Fires 1 more projectile.*                   |
| Level 5 | *Base Damage up by 5.*                       |
| Level 6 | *Passes through 2 more enemies.*             |
| Level 7 | *Base Damage up by 5. Base Speed up by 50%.* |
| Level 8 | *Passes through 2 more enemies.*             |
