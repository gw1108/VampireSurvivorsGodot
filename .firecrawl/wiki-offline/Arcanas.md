**Arcana cards** are a modifier system in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. Obtaining the  [Randomazzo](Randomazzo.md) in [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md) unlocks the first Arcana,  [Sarabande of Healing (VI)](Sarabande_of_Healing_(VI).md).

The rest of the Arcanas in the base set are unlocked by reaching level 50 with certain [characters](Characters.md), or by reaching the 31:00 mark in certain [stages](Stages.md). Obtaining the  [Darkasso](Darkasso.md) in [Room 1665](Room_1665.md) unlocks an additional set of \"Darkanas\", allowing even more Arcanas to be unlocked by achieving specific tasks with certain other characters.

## Mechanics {#Mechanics}

Each Arcana modifies gameplay in a particular way. The player can choose to enable or disable Arcanas in the stage selection screen.

With Arcanas enabled, the player can obtain up to three Arcanas over the course of a run (although through certain means, more than three can be obtained):

1.  At the beginning of a run, the player is allowed to choose one of *any* unlocked Arcanas or Darkanas. They are also given the option to select a random one.
2.  At 11:00 and 21:00, a special boss will spawn that, upon death, who drops an  [Arcana Chest](Treasure_Chest.md#Arcana). Upon being picked up, the chest will allow the player to choose an additional Arcana. The menu offers four or more randomly chosen Arcanas from the uncollected Arcanas. The player may spend [Rerolls](Reroll.md) to get new choices until all options have been displayed, and unlike normal level ups, previous options will not be offered again.

If the player has three or more Arcanas, the Arcana bosses will instead drop regular chests. If Arcanas are disabled, the Arcana bosses will not appear in the stage.

If the player has unlocked a total of 23 or more Arcanas or Darkanas (which requires unlocking at least one Darkana), Arcana Chests will instead offer six random Arcanas as opposed to four, and each Arcana Chest will allow the player one free Reroll.

In stages where the time limit is not 30:00, the Arcana bosses may appear at different times. In [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Bat Country](Bat_Country.md), and [Tiny Bridge](Tiny_Bridge.md), the bosses instead spawn at 5:00 and 10:00.

Some characters automatically start with Arcana effects, such as  [Avatar Infernas](Avatar_Infernas.md) starting with  [Heart of Fire (XIX)](Heart_of_Fire_(XIX).md); these Arcana effects do not take up an Arcana slot, and they will still be present if Arcanas are disabled.

### Obtaining more than three Arcanas {#Obtaining_more_than_three_Arcanas}

There is no limit to the amount of Arcanas a player can have at one time, although more unique methods are required for obtaining more than three.

-  [Queen Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md) is allowed to select an additional Arcana at levels 1, 2, 3, 77, and 108. All unlocked Arcanas are available as choices.
- If [Inverse mode](Inverse_mode.md) is active, the  [Merchant](Merchant.md) will sell an Arcana for 20,000, regardless of how many Arcanas the player currently has. All unlocked Arcanas are available as choices.
  - With [Endless mode](Endless_mode.md) enabled, the Merchant will respawn with another for sale at the start of each normal game length cycle (30 minutes in most stages). This can be repeated until all unlocked Arcanas are collected.
- Treasure Chests dropped by bosses spawned by  [Moonlight Bolero (VI)](Moonlight_Bolero_(VI).md) can occasionally contain Arcanas. This chance remains until all unlocked Arcanas have been collected.

## Darkanas {#Darkanas}

Darkanas were introduced in [patch 1.11](/w/Updates/Patch_1.11:_Bug_fixes_and_improvements "Updates/Patch 1.11: Bug fixes and improvements") and are unlocked by obtaining the  [Darkasso](Darkasso.md) found due south in [Room 1665](Room_1665.md). There are currently 12 Darkanas total. Each Darkana is also represented by a Roman numeral.

While Darkanas often share similar themes to their \"counterpart\" Arcanas, whether or not they\'re meant to be the inverse or opposite of Arcana is merely speculation, and has never been confirmed. However, this is likely false, as the patch notes for the same version that introduced Darkana state \"The only difference between Arcanas and Darkanas is their theme, functionally they are exactly the same,\" ^[\[1\]](#cite_note-1)^ with no other confirmation toward this intention being given.
