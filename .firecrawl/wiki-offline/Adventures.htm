Adventures

From Vampire Survivors Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Adventures are a game mode introduced in the 1.8 update. They are unlocked after collecting the  Atlas Gate in Boss Rash for base-game adventures, being A Garlic Paradise, World of Light and Dark, and To End an Ice Age. DLC Adventures, such as Tides of the Foscari, can be unlocked much earlier, being unlocked when the player unlocks the Dairy Plant Stage.

Each adventure features a linear progression of stages and unlocks with a predetermined roster of characters. While the game's description of them says that no progress carries over, achievements can be completed and relics can be used and collected within them, even if their prerequisite cannot be accessed.

Golden Eggs cannot be activated in any adventure, however they can be gained by purchasing them or defeating certain enemies, and will apply to the character that received them outside of the adventure.

Once all unlocks of an Adventure are complete, it can be ascended to reset the progress and gain an ascension point. Ascension points can be spent on Luck, Growth, Gold, or Curse, with each point granting +25%.
