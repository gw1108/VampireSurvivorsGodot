**Rich Coin Bag** is an [item pickup](Pickup.md) that increases your [Gold Coin](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) total by 100, multiplied by the [Greed](Greed.md) stat. It can drop from [light sources](Light_source.md) after your character reaches level 5 in a run. Its drop rate depends on the [Luck](Luck.md) stat.

It can trigger a [Gold Fever](Gilded_Clover.md#Gold_Fever) when [Disco of Gold (XV)](Disco_of_Gold_(XV).md) is selected.
