The **Little Clover** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md).

Collecting the Little Clover for the first time will unlock the  [Clover](Clover.md).

Collecting 23 Little Clovers will unlock [Westwoods](Westwoods.md) as well as [Hyper](Hyper.md) [Westwoods](Westwoods.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Picking up the Little Clover increases the character\'s  [Luck](Luck.md) by +10%.
