# Max Health {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

**Max Health** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It determines how much health the player\'s character can have in total.

The base value of Max Health is 100 before modifications, such as the [characters](Characters.md)\' bonuses or [PowerUps](PowerUp.md). The characters\' Max Health modification is additive. Max Health modifications from PowerUps and [items](Items.md) are calculated after the character modification and stack multiplicatively.

There is no upper limit to how much Max Health the player can have.

Max Health also affects:

- [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md) and [Amount](Amount.md) of  [Mazo Familiar](Mazo_Familiar.md)
- Damage of explosions created by  [Vanitas Whip](Vanitas_Whip.md) 

The amount of missing Health affects [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) of  [Metal Claw](Metal_Claw.md) .

With  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) activated, all [weapons](Weapons.md) gain +0.1 flat damage per missing Health, uncapped.

The Max Health [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases Max Health exponentially, multiplying it by 1.1 each rank up to a maximum rank of 3. At rank 3, it multiplies Max Health by 1.331. It initially costs 200.

|      |            |
|------|------------|
| Rank | Max Health |
| 1    | 1.1x       |
| 2    | 1.21x      |
| 3    | 1.331x     |

The calculation of initial Max Health is done differently from other stats, similarly to that of [Magnet](Magnet.md):

$Max\ Health = (100 + character\ bonus)*PowerUp\ multiplier$

For example,  [Poe](Poe_Ratcho.md) reduces Max Health by 30, so using Poe with the PowerUp at rank 3, Max Health would be:

$(100 - 30)*1.331 = 93.17$

## From items {#From_items}

Max Health\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is  [Hollow Heart](Hollow_Heart.md), which multiplies Max Health by 1.2 each time it is leveled up. Its maximum level is 5. At level 5 it provides a 2.49x multiplier to Max Health.

-  [Metaglio Left](Metaglio_Left.md) multiplies Max Health by 1.05 each time it is leveled up. Its maximum level is 9. At level it provides a 1.477x multiplier to Max Health.
-  [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md)  has a chance of granting a Max Health stat bonus of +20 upon activation.
-  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Max Health by +1 for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) provides +10% Max Health per  [Revival](Revival.md) consumed.
-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) provides +0.5 Max Health per [enemy](Enemy.md) killed by retaliatory damage.

## From darkana {#From_darkana}

-  [Crystal Cries (XII)](Crystal_Cries_(XII).md) generates crystals when killing frozen enemies that can be collected to slightly increase Max Health,  [Recovery](Recovery.md) and  [Growth](Growth.md).

## Notes {#Notes}

- Max Health value shown in the Character Selection menu doesn\'t increase exponentially, which is incorrect.
- Max Health value shown in the stats panel, both in the Character Selection menu and in-game, is rounded to the closest whole number. The actual value of Max Health is a decimal number.
- Negative Max Health makes the character invulnerable.
-  [White Hand](White_Hand.md) kills the character by setting Max Health to 0 (even if Max Health is set to be negative).
