**The Reaper** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) that spawns once every minute after the time limit of a [stage](Stage.md) has been reached, unless [endless mode](Endless_mode.md) has been turned on. In [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md), a more vulnerable variant of the Reaper is summoned by  [The Directer](The_Directer.md).

## Behavior {#Behavior}

Once spawned, The Reaper will quickly rush to the player\'s character and deal 65,535 damage to them, ending the run. Player is rewarded 500 for surviving to the end. The player can revive if any [Revivals](Revival.md) are left, increasing the bonus every time by stacking +100 Gold (e.g. total bonus for 3 revives is 500 + 100 + 200 + 300 = 1,100 Gold). This bonus lasts up to +400 Gold, after which it will reset back to +100 Gold for any remaining Revivals.

The Reaper\'s health pool is set at 655,350, further multiplied by the player\'s [level](Level.md) when it spawns. The Reaper has -50% [knockback](Knockback.md) resistance, causing it to get dragged closer to the player whenever it takes a hit, instead of being pushed away. It is possible to [freeze](Freeze.md) The Reaper using weapons or items that creates freezing effects such as  [Clock Lancet](Clock_Lancet.md),  [Orologion](Orologion.md), or a weapon affected by  [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md).

Killing The Reaper the first time unlocks the secret character  [Mask of the Red Death](Mask_of_the_Red_Death.md) as a playable character. If The Reaper was defeated with either  [Infinite Corridor](Infinite_Corridor.md) or  [Crimson Shroud](Crimson_Shroud.md), it will drop 5  [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) and trigger the  [White Hand](White_Hand.md) to appear and end the run definitely.

## Strategies {#Strategies}

### Crimson Shroud + Infinite Corridor {#Crimson_Shroud_+_Infinite_Corridor}

As of version 0.6.1, the most accessible method of defeating The Reaper involves having the [Crimson Shroud](Crimson_Shroud.md) and the [Infinite Corridor](Infinite_Corridor.md): evolutions of [Laurel](Laurel.md) and [Clock Lancet](Clock_Lancet.md) respectively. Doing so has the following requirements:

1.  Unlocking Hyper mode on 4 normal stages unlocks [Moongolow](Moongolow.md).
2.  If the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md) has not been obtained, a special boss will spawn near the end of a Moongolow run when using a non-secret character. Defeating it grants access to [Holy Forbidden](Holy_Forbidden.md) once the 15-minute time limit is reached.
3.  Within Holy Forbidden, collect the Yellow Sign.
4.  The following items will now always spawn on every stage: [Metaglio Left](Metaglio_Left.md), [Metaglio Right](Metaglio_Right.md), [Silver Ring](Silver_Ring.md) and [Gold Ring](Gold_Ring.md).
5.  Having a level 7 Clock Lancet, level 9 Silver Ring and level 9 Gold Ring will allow evolving the Clock Lancet into Infinite Corridor.
6.  Having a level 7 Laurel, level 9 Metaglio Left and level 9 Metaglio Right will allow evolving the Laurel into Crimson Shroud.
7.  Open 2 Treasure Chests and you will receive [Crimson Shroud](Crimson_Shroud.md) and [Infinite Corridor](Infinite_Corridor.md).

Crimson Shroud caps damage to 10 at a time, making The Reaper\'s attack far less effective if it attacks you. Crimson Shroud also does amplified retaliatory damage, enough to kill The Reaper without Corridor, though this may take some time. However Infinite Corridor halves all enemies health, making it possible to defeat The Reaper in a fairly short amount of time.

The above strategy can still be done with an unevolved Laurel, though it would be considerably riskier. If skipping Crimson Shroud, be sure to have mastered your usage of Clock Lancet/Infinite Corridor\'s [freeze](Freeze.md) effect, in order to avoid being attacked by The Reaper as much as possible. Also, be aware that every minute, a new Reaper spawns, which would then need to be frozen near immediately while also likely taking a Laurel charge.

Defeating the Reaper with an Infinite Corridor and without a Laurel at all is not impossible. While normally very risky due to the high probability of dying before being able to freeze the Reaper again, [Mirage Robe](Mirage_Robe.md) or its evolution [J\'Odore](J'Odore.md) can also lock the Reaper in place and, unlike Infinite Corridor itself, help protect the player from all sides. This represents a way for freeze-focused builds to defeat the Reaper without having to waste an item slot on a Laurel. Enough Revives obtained through [Tirajisú](Tirajisú.md) and [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) may also allow the player to simply \"weather\" the Reaper\'s attacks.

This strategy is required for The Reaper to drop Golden Eggs, and is also the only way for the Reaper\'s death to summon the [White Hand](White_Hand.md).

### Level 200 Toastie {#Level_200_Toastie}

Prior to version 0.6.1, defeating The Reaper was intended to be done with the character [Toastie](Toastie.md). The following special game mechanics apply to this character:

- Toastie begins with 1 HP. At level 100, it gains an additional 9,998 HP.
- At level 200, Toastie gains an additional 65,520 [Armor](Armor_(stat).md). This reduces the damage from The Reaper\'s attack to 15.
- By using the Armor [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) and [Armor](Armor_(item).md) passive item, an additional 8 Armor can be earned, reducing the damage from The Reaper\'s attack to 7.
- By using the [Awake](Awake_(IV).md) Arcana in combination with the Revival PowerUp and [Tiragisú](Tiragisú.md), an additional 6 Armor can be earned by reviving six times. This further reduces the damage from The Reaper\'s attack to 1.

A level 200 Toastie can go toe-to-toe in combat with The Reaper. Max Armor used in conjunction with direct healing from [Bloody Tear](Bloody_Tear.md) or [Soul Eater](Soul_Eater.md) and/or passive healing from the Recovery PowerUp and [Pummarola](Pummarola.md) will allow Toastie to withstand the Reapers\' onslaught indefinitely. Over the course of several minutes, Toastie will deal enough damage to defeat The Reaper.

The challenge with this approach is not in the mechanics of the battle with The Reaper, but rather in devising and executing a strategy to get Toastie to level 200 with the desired stats, all within the time limit.

### Sanctuary {#Sanctuary}

Main article: [Sanctuary](Sanctuary.md)

The Sanctuary weapon occasionally erases all enemies on screen, including enemies immune to instant kills. Paired with a Clock Lancet or Orologion to freeze The Reaper, or a Familiar to distract it, Sanctuary can erase all Reapers present in the screen. This effect may or may not be intended thus it may get patched out in a future update.

### Other Strategies {#Other_Strategies}

Main article: [/Other Strategies](The_Reaper/Other_Strategies.md)

Before the introduction of Infinite Corridor, other strategies were used to kill The Reaper, mostly relying on certain exploits involving the game mechanics. For more information, see [here](The_Reaper/Other_Strategies.md).

In summary:

- [Suor Clerici](Suor_Clerici.md)\'s temporary [Area](Area.md) bonus can be used in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md) to get [Runetracers](Runetracer.md) perpetually stuck in the geometry. By hiding behind the projectiles for protection, Clerici can survive the full 30 minutes at level 2, thereby spawning The Reaper with relatively low HP (1.2 million). Gathering the red experience gem which contains all the XP and [Treasure Chests](Treasure_Chest.md) at the 30-minute mark will give enough levels and evolution options to assemble a build capable of defeating The Reaper.
- Prior to verison 0.6.1, [Bone](Bone.md) would get stuck if spawned inside a large enemy that has been frozen in place. This is because it attempts to bounce off of enemies, but being inside one means it has nowhere to go and it bounces in place very rapidly. With [Clock Lancet](Clock_Lancet.md), Bone could be used to deal very high damage to The Reaper in very little time.
- The character [MissingN▯](MissingN▯.md) could be used to defeat The Reaper. Its stats were all randomized every game, and if Max Health rolled a negative number, the character would be invulnerable to all damage. This made defeating The Reaper trivial, but very time-consuming.
- The character [Smith IV](Smith_IV.md) or [Avatar Infernas](Avatar_Infernas.md) could be used to defeat The Reaper. Due to both characters reducing cooldowns by -0.25% per level and there is no limit, past -87.5% cooldown, Laurel\'s shield regenerates faster than the player can take damage. This made defeating The Reaper trivial, but very time consuming.
- The above strategy can also be executed with any other character using either the [Paranormal Scan](Paranormal_Scan.md) or the scaling from [Hidden Anathema (III)](Hidden_Anathema_(III).md) to reduce cooldown instead. However, without extreme luck, it may be difficult to achieve a sufficient cooldown reduction before the Reaper spawns. [Mirage Robe](Mirage_Robe.md) with enough Amount and Cooldown can be effective at staving off the Reapers until such reduction can be achieved, as, unlike the Clock Lancet, it protects the character from all sides simultaneously. Because Reapers do not give any EXP, the character will not gain any more levels once all EXP gems have been collected, so the Reapers\' health will not increase, whereas the character will continuously raise their stats, slowly improving their ability to kill Reapers over time. [Cosmo Pavone](Cosmo_Pavone.md) with [Impostongue](Impostongue.md) can be used to defeat Reapers in a relatively small amount of time thanks to the weapon\'s insane damage output.
- By using [Game Killer (0)](Game_Killer_(0).md) to keep her level 1 cooldown bonus infinitely and acquiring [Sword Brothers](Sword_Brothers.md), [Porta Ladonna](Porta_Ladonna.md) can become permanently invincible to everything short of the [White Hand](White_Hand.md), including the Reaper\'s attack.
