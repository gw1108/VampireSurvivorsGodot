**Lightning Ring** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Porta Ladonna](Porta_Ladonna.md) and  [Wood Rod](Innocent_Devil.md#Wood_Rod) . It is unlocked by defeating a total of 5,000 enemies.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Thunder Loop](Thunder_Loop.md), which requires  [Duplicator](Duplicator.md).

As a vampire-killing tool, it can be picked from  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md) .

## Effects {#Effects}

Lightning Ring summons lightning strikes from the sky to deal damage to random enemies. When the lightning strikes the ground, it creates a hitbox that deals damage, rather than the lightning bolts themselves.

Bonuses to  [Area](Area.md) affect how large this hitbox is.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Tragic Princess (III)](Tragic_Princess_(III).md) - Reduces Lightning Ring\'s  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) when the character is moving. It is affected by  [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md).
-  [Twilight Requiem (II)](Twilight_Requiem_(II).md) - Allows Lightning Ring\'s strikes to explode one more time with a different effect. The damage of these explosions is based on  [Curse](Curse.md).
-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) - Allows Lightning Ring\'s strikes to deal extra flat damage based on  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md).
-  [Pale Diamond Incursion (V)](Pale_Diamond_Incursion_(V).md) - Aims at nearest enemy, and gains  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) bonus based on  [Speed](Speed.md).
