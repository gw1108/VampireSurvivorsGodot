# Relics {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

**Relics** are permanent items that unlock new mechanics and features once collected. They are very similar to [stage items](Passive_item.md#Stage_items) as they are simply spawned on the map and are collected upon touching them. Once collected, they are permanently added to the [collection](Collection.md) menu and do not appear again. Until picked up, their locations are indicated by green arrows once the player enters a [stage](Stage.md) that contains them.
