**Cross** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Krochi Freetto](Krochi_Freetto.md). It is unlocked by picking up a  [Rosary](Rosary.md).

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Heaven Sword](Heaven_Sword.md), which requires  [Clover](Clover.md).

As a vampire-killing tool, it can be picked from  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md) .

## Effects {#Effects}

Cross shoots a spinning cross at the closest enemy. After a short while, it travels in the opposite direction it was thrown like a boomerang until it leaves the screen. Crosses pierce and damage all enemies they come in contact with.

 [Speed](Speed.md) determines how far the crosses can initially travel before they return.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) - Allows Cross to deal extra flat damage based on  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md).
-  [Waltz of Pearls (XI)](Waltz_of_Pearls_(XI).md) - Allows Cross\'s projectiles to bounce off the edges of the screen twice. Upon bouncing, Cross completely reverses its horizontal and vertical speed instead of just one of them.
