**Fire Wand** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Arca Ladonna](Arca_Ladonna.md) and  [Wood Rod](Innocent_Devil.md#Wood_Rod) . It is unlocked by destroying a total of 20 [light sources](Light_source.md).

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Hellfire](Hellfire.md), which requires  [Spinach](Spinach.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Fire Wand shoots an arc of fireballs in the direction of a random enemy.

Fireballs are fired near simultaneously and additional ones gained through  [Amount](Amount.md) bonuses are added to the ends of the arc of fireballs. The fireballs cannot pass through walls. Each fireball can hit one enemy before dissipating.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Waltz of Pearls (XI)](Waltz_of_Pearls_(XI).md) - Allows Fire Wand\'s projectiles to bounce off enemies up to three times.
-  [Heart of Fire (XIX)](Heart_of_Fire_(XIX).md) - Allows Fire Wand\'s projectiles to explode upon contact with an enemy, dealing half its base damage in an area.
-  [Pale Diamond Incursion (V)](Pale_Diamond_Incursion_(V).md) - Fire Wand aims at nearest enemy, and gains  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) bonus based on  [Speed](Speed.md).
-  [Heir of Fate (XIX)](Heir_of_Fate_(XIX).md) - Enemies defeated by Fire Wand will drop  [Scorched Souls](Scorched_Soul.md), giving bonus [MaxHealth](Max_Health.md) and [Might](Might.md).
