> "
>
> Wreathed in shadow, eyes glowing red, such oversized terrors that flap in the night must surely be the sign of a vampire's approach, yes? Well, no. They're bats, and nothing more. I'll admit they're really bloody big, though.
>
> **--- [Ars Gouda](Ars_Gouda.md), p. 34**

**Giant Bat** is a normal enemy primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), with additional appearances in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Tiny Bridge](Tiny_Bridge.md), and [Bat Country](Bat_Country.md).

In Mad Forest, it first appears as a boss and later reappears as a normal enemy in later waves. In Inlaid Library, Bat Country, and Tiny Bridge, it appears only as a normal enemy. In Boss Rash, it appears exclusively as a boss.

Giant Bat is semi-resistant to [knockback](Knockback.md) and features a partial hitbox: the upper portion of its wings lacks collision, allowing it to partially pass through walls and other enemies without obstruction.
