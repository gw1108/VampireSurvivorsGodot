Might

Might is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. It affects the damage output of all weapons.

The player has 100% base Might before modifications, such as the characters bonuses or PowerUps. The Might in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Might, meaning that Might stat of +30% equals to 130% total Might, for example. Might modifications from characters, PowerUps, passive items, and arcanas stack additively.

The upper limit of total Might after character, PowerUp, and item modifications is 1000%, or +900% in the stats panel.

  • Might has a further multiplier of 1.25x on  SpellStrike .
  • Might also affects the charge ability of  Quincy Morris .
  •  Stanley  gets +1  Armor per +10% Might.

From items

[edit | edit source]

Might's associated passive item is  Spinach, which provides +10% Might per level, up to a maximum of +50% Might at level 5.

  •  Torrona's Box also provides +4%, then +3% per level, up to a maximum of +25% at level 8.
  •  Paranormal Scan  has a chance of granting a Might stat bonus of +4% upon activation.
  •  Golden Eggs can permanently increase Might by +1% for the character that collected it.

From arcana

[edit | edit source]
  •  Awake (IV) provides +5% Might when chosen per  Revival consumed.
  •  Divine Bloodline (IX) provides +1 flat damage (displayed as Might in stats menu) for every 10 health less than the  Max Health.
  •  Silent Old Sanctuary (XX) provides +20% Might when chosen per active weapon slot left empty.

  •  Sammy starts -60% Might. They gains an additional +10% Might on levels 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, and 21.

Emergency Meeting

[edit | edit source]

  •  Ghost starts with -100% Might and cannot be increased by any means.
  •  Impostor starts with +20% Might and gains +200% Might while in their boosted state.
  •  Scientist can select to gain +1% Might per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Might per level.
  •  Megalo Impostor starts with +80% Might.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Since Might is percentage based, it is more effective with weapons that have high Base Damage, such as the Death Spiral and the Hellfire.
