**Clock Lancet** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Iguana Gallo Valletto](Iguana_Gallo_Valletto.md). It is unlocked by picking up an  [Orologion](Orologion.md).

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Infinite Corridor](Infinite_Corridor.md), which requires  [Silver Ring](Silver_Ring.md) and  [Gold Ring](Gold_Ring.md) at maximum level.

## Effects {#Effects}

Clock Lancet fires a thin, freezing beam around the character in twelve directions clockwise one at a time. The freezing beam does no damage, but will [freeze](Freeze.md) any enemy it hits.

Bonuses to  [Duration](Duration.md) will add to the time enemies are frozen. Bonuses to  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) will increase the fire rate of the weapon.

## Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Out of Bounds (XII)](Out_of_Bounds_(XII).md) - Enemies frozen by Clock Lancet will explode.
-  [Blood Astronomia (XXI)](Blood_Astronomia_(XXI).md) - Allows Clock Lancet to place a damaging zone on the ground that gradually make enemies move slower permanently.
-  [Crystal Cries (XII)](Crystal_Cries_(XII).md) - Enemies frozen by Clock Lancet will drop Crystalized Souls when killed.

## Levels {#Levels}
