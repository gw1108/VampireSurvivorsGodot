# Adventures {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

<figure typeof="mw:File/Thumb">

<figcaption>The Adventures menu with only base game adventures listed.</figcaption>
</figure>

**Adventures** are a game mode introduced in the [1.8 update](/w/Updates/Adventures_%2B_Vampire_Survivors:_Emergency_Meeting_DLC_on_Steam_Dec_18th "Updates/Adventures + Vampire Survivors: Emergency Meeting DLC on Steam Dec 18th"). They are unlocked after collecting the  [Atlas Gate](Atlas_Gate.md) in [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md) for base-game adventures, being [A Garlic Paradise](#A_Garlic_Paradise), [World of Light and Dark](#World_of_Light_and_Dark), and [To End an Ice Age](#To_End_an_Ice_Age). DLC Adventures, such as [Tides of the Foscari](#Tides_of_the_Foscari), can be unlocked much earlier, being unlocked when the player unlocks the [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md) [Stage](Stages.md).

Each adventure features a linear progression of stages and unlocks with a predetermined roster of characters. While the game\'s description of them says that no progress carries over, [achievements](Achievements.md) can be completed and [relics](Relics.md) can be used and collected within them, even if their prerequisite cannot be accessed.

[Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) cannot be activated in any adventure, however they can be gained by purchasing them or defeating certain enemies, and will apply to the character that received them outside of the adventure.

Once all unlocks of an Adventure are complete, it can be ascended to reset the progress and gain an ascension point. Ascension points can be spent on Luck, Growth, Gold, or Curse, with each point granting +25%.
