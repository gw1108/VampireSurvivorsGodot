Amount

Amount is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. It determines how many extra projectiles most weapons have.

The player has a base Amount of 0 before modifications. Bonuses to Amount are flat bonuses and thus stack additively.

The upper limit of Amount from characters, PowerUps and passive item or weapon bonuses is 10.

For certain weapons, Amount may work differently. It affects:

  • Base Damage of  Four Seasons ,  Godai Shuffle ,  Flash Arrow ,  Millionaire ,  Endo Gears ,  Peri Pendulum ,  Myo Lift , and  Epi Head 
  • Duration of  Globus  and  Nitesco 
  • Pierce of  Just Vent ,  Mace , and  Stamazza 
  • Healing effect of  Lifesign Scan  and  Paranormal Scan 
  • Number of bounces of  Carréllo

From items

[edit | edit source]

Amount's associated passive item is the  Duplicator, which provides +1 Amount per level, up to a maximum of +2 Amount at level 2.

  •  Academy Badge  has 6 levels and provides +1 Amount every even level, up to a maximum of +3 Amount.
  •  Paranormal Scan  has a chance of granting an Amount stat bonus of +0.2 upon activation.
  •  Golden Egg can permanently increase Amount by +0.1 for the character that collected it.

From arcana

[edit | edit source]
  •  Beginning (X) provides +3 Amount for the character's starting weapon and its evolution, and +1 Amount for  Bone,  Cherry Bomb,  Carréllo,  Celestial Dusting, and  La Robba.

From characters

[edit | edit source]

Base Game

[edit | edit source]
  •  Gennaro and  Sigma start with +1 Amount.
  •  Mortaccio,  Cavallo,  Ramba, and  O'Sole gain +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60.
    • Mortaccio, Cavallo, Ramba, and O'Sole gain +1 additional Amount when they morph at level 80 following the collection of their respective Chaos relic.
  •  Ambrojoe starts with +10 Amount, every level decreases by -2 until it returns to normal by level 6. He gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60.
  •  Bats gains +1 amount per revival.

Legacy of the Moonspell

[edit | edit source]
  •  Gav'Et-Oni gains+1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

Tides of the Foscari

[edit | edit source]
  •  Rottin'Ghoul gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

Emergency Meeting

[edit | edit source]
  •  Crewmate has a chance to gain a random amount of Amount every time a weapon or passive item is upgraded to its maximum level, with no cap.
  •  Horse gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

Operation Guns

[edit | edit source]
  •  Lucia gains +0.2 Amount after reviving, with no cap.
  •  Simondo gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.

Ode to Castlevania

[edit | edit source]
  •  Carrie and  Cornell start with +1 Amount.
  •  Blue Crescent Moon Cornell starts with +3 Amount.
  •  Trevor gains +1 Amount every 10 levels, up to +3 at level 30.
  •  Familiar and  Innocent Devil gain +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60.
  •  Cave Troll gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +4 at level 80.
  •  Hammer starts with +1 Amount and gains +1 Amount every 20 levels, up to +3 at level 60. His Amount is also doubled while invulerable.
  •  Leon gains +1 Amount upon obtaining the  Vampire Killer.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Amount determines not how many projectiles a weapon has, but how many extra projectiles a weapon has. A Level 2  Whip with +1 Amount will produce 3 slashes: 2 from the weapon itself, and 1 from your Amount stat.
  • Amount bonuses for some weapons work better than others. Weapons such as the  Axe and  Fire Wand do great when paired with the Duplicator, but weapons such as the  Magic Wand benefit less due to the already high amount of low damage projectiles.
