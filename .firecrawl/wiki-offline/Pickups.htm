Pickups

From Vampire Survivors Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Pickups are collectibles in Vampire Survivors dropped by destroying light sources or by defeating enemies.

The Magnet stat affects the radius pickups can be gathered from.

Most pickups' drop rate increases with Luck.

List of Pickups

[edit | edit source]

Note that DLC-exclusive pickups only appear when playing on specific maps or as specific characters.

Rarity is the relative weight of the item in the light source loot pool. When rarity is 0, this means it has no chance of dropping.

Unlock level is the level at which the item becomes available as a light source drop. The unlock level is ignored when rarity is 0, even if the player were to hit the item's given unlock level.

    Trivia

    [edit | edit source]
    • As per the nature of this game, a lot of items reference the Castlevania series:
      • Gold and Coin Bag is inspired by the Gold.
      • Rosary is inspired by the Rosary item.
      • Floor Chicken is inspired by the Food from Castlevania: Dracula X.
      • Orologion is inspired by the Stopwatch sub-weapon.
    • Picking up Floor Chicken and its variants provide a brief period of invulnerability. This can be chained when farming Sammys to provide minutes of invulnerability.
    • The Big Coin Bag, just like the Coin Bag and Rich Coin Bag, had an old sprite.
    • Despite having an "Unlock Level", items with rarity 0 do not drop from light sources even if said level is reached.
