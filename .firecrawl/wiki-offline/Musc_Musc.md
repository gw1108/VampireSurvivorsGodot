> Cursed by vengeful librarians for the crime of sneezing too loudly, they are perhaps the most pitiable of all wretches. Congealed for all eternity, to slay these victims of disproportionate retribution is a mercy. You'd probably get good karma for doing it if there were such thing as a morality system.

**Musc Musc** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) that appears in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md), [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md) and [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md). In Inlaid Library, it appears both as a normal enemy and a boss. In Dairy Plant, it only appears as an [event enemy](Map_event.md) from a trap. In Boss Rash it only appears as a boss.

Musc Musc\'s boss variant\'s health is based on the player\'s [level](Level.md) and it takes reduced [knockback](Knockback.md).
