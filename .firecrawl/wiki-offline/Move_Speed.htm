Move Speed

This article is about movement speed. For projectile speed, see Speed.

Move Speed (also known as Movement Speed, originally known as Speed) is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. It affects how fast the character moves in relation to the default speed.

The player has 100% base MoveSpeed before modifications. The MoveSpeed in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base MoveSpeed, meaning that MoveSpeed stat of +30% equals to 130% total MoveSpeed, for example. MoveSpeed modifications from characters, PowerUps, and passive items stack additively. Stage bonuses stack multiplicatively with the collective bonus from the aforementioned.

There is no upper limit to how much MoveSpeed the player may have.

MoveSpeed also affects:

  • Cooldown of  Shadow Pinion and  Valkyrie Turner
  • Cooldown of  Valmanway while moving
  •  Tragic Princess (III)'s effectiveness
  •  Babi-Onna's effectiveness on triggering weapons

 Ariana  gains +1  Armor per +10% MoveSpeed.

 Sheena Etranzi  gains -1%  Cooldown per +2% MoveSpeed.

From items

[edit | edit source]

Move Speed's associated passive item is  Wings, which provide +10% Move Speed per level, up to a maximum of +50% Move Speed at level 5.

  •  Sonic Dash  increases Move Speed by +10% per level per activation stack, up to a total of +80% MoveSpeed per stack at level 8. The MoveSpeed bonus is capped at +500%.
  •  Rapidus Fio  increases Move Speed by +80% per activation stack. The MoveSpeed bonus is capped at +500%.
  •  Paranormal Scan  has a chance of granting a Move Speed stat bonus of +8% upon activation.
  •  Golden Egg can permanently increase Move Speed by +1% for the character that collected it.

From stages

[edit | edit source]
  • Mad Forest multiply MoveSpeed by 1.1.
    • Hyper Mad Forest multiply MoveSpeed by 2.
  • Inlaid Library, Dairy Plant, Gallo Tower, Holy Forbidden, Il Molise, Green Acres, The Bone Zone, Boss Rash, Bat Country, and Tiny Bridge multiply MoveSpeed by 1.25.
    • Hyper mode of these stages provides multiply MoveSpeed by 1.9, except Holy Forbidden and Il Molise.
    • Holy Forbidden does not have hyper mode.
    • Hyper Il Molise multiply MoveSpeed by 2.
  • Cappella Magna multiply MoveSpeed by 1.4.
    • Hyper mode multiply MoveSpeed by 2.
  • Moongolow multiply MoveSpeed by 1.35.
    • Hyper mode Moongolow multiply MoveSpeed by 2.
  • Eudaimonia Machine multiply MoveSpeed by 1.5.
    • Eudaimonia Machine does not have Hyper mode.

Legacy of the Moonspell

[edit | edit source]
  • Mt.Moonspell multiply MoveSpeed by 1.4.
    • Hyper mode Mt.Moonspell multiply MoveSpeed by 2.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Movement Speed is additive.
  • If MoveSpeed modifier is below -100%, i.e. the total MoveSpeed is negative, the controls become inverted.
