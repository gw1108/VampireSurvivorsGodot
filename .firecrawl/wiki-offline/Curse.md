# Curse {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Curse** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It buffs the speed, health, spawn frequency, and quantity of [enemies](Enemies.md).

The player has 100% base Curse before modifications. The Curse in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Curse, meaning that Curse stat of +30% equals to 130% total Curse, for example. Curse modifications from [character](Character.md) bonuses, [PowerUps](PowerUps.md) and [items](Item.md) stack additively.

There is no upper limit to how much Curse the player may have.

## Effects {#Effects}

Curse increases the frequency and quantity of enemy [waves](Enemy.md#Waves), as well as their speed and health, by the percentage of Curse. The multipliers to health, speed, and enemy minimum stack multiplicatively with other effects of the same kind.

The new spawn interval with any amount of Curse can be calculated as follows:

$effectiveSpawnInterval = \frac{spawnInterval}{totalCurse}$

When Curse is gained during a run, the health multiplier effect is immediately applied to all newly spawned enemies. All other effects are applied at the start of the next minute.

Total enemy quantity allowed on screen is capped at 500, regardless of Curse and  [Charm](Charm.md) value.

Curse also affects:

- Base Damage of  [Hex](Hex.md) 
- Instant-killing chance of  [Sonic Whip](Sonic_Whip.md) 
- Base Damage of  [108 Bocce](108_Bocce.md)  if Curse is higher than  [Luck](Luck.md)
- Damage of explosion summoned by  [Twilight Requiem (II)](Twilight_Requiem_(II).md)

 [Colonel Bahamut](Colonel_Bahamut.md)  and  [Vlad Tepes Dracula](Vlad_Tepes_Dracula.md)  gain +1%  [Might](Might.md) per +1% Curse.

The Curse [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases Curse by 10% per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, it provides +50% Curse. It initially costs 1,666.

|-------|-------|
| Level | Curse |
| 1     | +10%  |
| 2     | +20%  |
| 3     | +30%  |
| 4     | +40%  |
| 5     | +50%  |

## From items {#From_items}

Curse\'s associated [passive item](Passive_item.md) is  [Skull O\'Maniac](Skull_O'Maniac.md), which provides +10% Curse per level, up to a maximum of +50% Curse at level 5.

 [Gold Ring](Gold_Ring.md) and  [Metaglio Right](Metaglio_Right.md) both provide +5% per level, up to +40% each at level 9.

 [Torrona\'s Box](Torrona's_Box.md) also provides +100% at level 9.

 [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increases Curse by 1% for the character that collected it.

 [Nightmare](Nightmare.md)  creates green plague balls that increases Curse by 100% when stepping in (which can stack up from each plague balls).

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

 [Wicked Season (XIII)](Wicked_Season_(XIII).md) doubles Curse in certain 10 second intervals, and gives +0.5% Curse every level.

## From characters {#From_characters}

### Base Game {#Base_Game}

-  [Lama](Lama_Ladonna.md) starts with +10% Curse and gains +5% Curse every 10 levels, up to +20% at level 40. In total, she can have +30% Curse.
-  [Concetta](Concetta_Caciotta.md),  [Zi\'Assunta](Zi'Assunta_Belpaese.md),  [Marrabbio](Boon_Marrabbio.md), and  [Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md) start with +10% Curse.
-  [Gyorunton](Gyorunton.md) and  [Gyoruntin](Gyoruntin.md) gain +1% Curse every level, with no cap.
-  [Avatar](Avatar_Infernas.md) gains 0.5% Curse every level, with no cap.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell}

-  [Menya](Menya_Moonspell.md) gains +50% Curse when she is invincible.
-  [Syuuto](Syuuto_Moonspell.md) and  [Megalo Syuuto](Megalo_Syuuto_Moonspell.md) start with +50% Curse.

### Tides of the Foscari {#Tides_of_the_Foscari}

-  [Maruto](Maruto_Cuts.md) and  [Keitha](Keitha_Muort.md) start with +10% Curse.
-  [Je-Ne-Viv](Je-Ne-Viv.md) gains +0.5% Curse every level, with no cap.

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Scientist](Scientist_Mina.md) can select to gain +1% Curse per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Curse per level.

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Axe Armor](Axe_Armor.md) starts with +10% Curse and gains +5% Curse every 10 levels, up to +20% at level 40. In total, it can have +30% Curse.
-  [Shaft](Shaft.md) gains +1% Curse every level, with no cap. He also gains +50% Curse during his HP-Critical ability.
-  [Joachim](Joachim_Armster.md) can select to gain +1% Curse per level.
-  [Blackmore](Blackmore.md) can select to gain +1% Curse per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Curse per level.
-  [Barlowe](Barlowe.md) gains +1% Curse per 24 health of overhealing, with no cap.
