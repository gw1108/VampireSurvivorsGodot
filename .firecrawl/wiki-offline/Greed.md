# Greed {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Greed** is one of the [stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects the amount of  [Gold Coins](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) that are gained from [pickups](Pickups.md) and  [Treasure Chests](Treasure_Chest.md).

The player has 100% base Greed before modifications. The Greed in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Greed, meaning that Greed stat of +30% equals to 130% total Greed, for example. Greed modifications from [character](Character.md) bonuses, [PowerUps](PowerUps.md) and [items](Item.md) stack additively. [Stage bonuses](Stage.md#Stage_modifiers) to gold gained do not count as Greed and stack multiplicatively with the collective bonus from the aforementioned.

There is no upper limit to how much Greed the player may have.

Greed also affects Base Damage of  [Night Sword](Night_Sword.md)  and  [Muramasa](Muramasa.md) .

 [Colonel Bahamut](Colonel_Bahamut.md)  gains +1%  [Might](Might.md) per +1% Greed.

The Greed [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases Greed by 10% per rank, with a maximum rank of 5. At rank 5, it provides +50% Greed. It initially costs 200.

|      |       |
|------|-------|
| Rank | Greed |
| 1    | +10%  |
| 2    | +20%  |
| 3    | +30%  |
| 4    | +40%  |
| 5    | +50%  |

## From items {#From_items}

Greed\'s associated [passive item](Passive_item.md) is the  [Stone Mask](Stone_Mask.md), which provides +10% Greed per level, up to a maximum of +50% Greed at level 5.

 [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) permanently increases Greed by 1% for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

 [Wicked Season (XIII)](Wicked_Season_(XIII).md) doubles Greed in certain 10 second intervals, and gives +0.5% Greed every level.

## From characters {#From_characters}

### Base Game {#Base_Game}

-  [Ambrojoe](Sir_Ambrojoe.md) starts with +20% Greed.
-  [Gallo](Iguana_Gallo_Valletto.md) and  [Divano](Divano_Thelma.md) reduce Greed by -50%.
-  [Leda](Leda.md) and  [Marrabbio](Boon_Marrabbio.md) reduce Greed by -80%.
-  [Trouser](Big_Trouser.md) starts with +20% Greed and gains +1% Greed every level, with no cap.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell}

-  [Megalo Syuuto](Megalo_Syuuto_Moonspell.md) starts with +50% Greed.

### Tides of the Foscari {#Tides_of_the_Foscari}

-  [Genevieve](Genevieve_Gruyère.md) and  [Je-Ne-Viv](Je-Ne-Viv.md) start with +50% Greed.

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Scientist](Scientist_Mina.md) can select to gain +1% Greed per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Greed per level.

### Operation Guns {#Operation_Guns}

-  [Lucia](Lucia_Zero.md) gains +1% Greed after [reviving](Revival.md), with no cap.

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Soma](Soma_Cruz.md) starts with +50% Greed.
-  [Dracula](Vlad_Tepes_Dracula.md),  [Elizabeth](Elizabeth_Bartley.md),  [Stella & Loretta](Stella_&_Loretta_Lecarde.md),  [Loretta & Stella](Loretta_&_Stella_Lecarde.md),  [Stella](Stella_Lecarde.md),  [Loretta](Loretta_Lecarde.md),  [Brauner](Brauner.md),  [Graham](Graham_Jones.md),  [Walter](Walter_Bernhard.md),  [Carmilla](Carmilla.md),  [Olrox](Count_Olrox.md),  [Succubus](Succubus.md),  [Galamoth](Galamoth.md),  [Megalo Elizabeth](Megalo_Elizabeth_Bartley.md),  [Megalo Olrox](Megalo_Olrox.md),  [Megalo Death](Megalo_Death.md) and  [Megalo Dracula](Megalo_Dracula.md) start with +100% Greed.
-  [Master Librarian](Master_Librarian.md) starts with +1000% Greed.
-  [Vincent](Vincent_Dorin.md) starts with +20% Greed and gains Greed when overhealing, with diminishing returns. His Greed is also doubled while invulnerable.
-  [Joachim](Joachim_Armster.md) starts with +100% Greed and can select to gain +1% Greed per level.
-  [Blackmore](Blackmore.md) starts with +100% Greed and can select to gain +1% Greed per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Greed per level.

## From stages {#From_stages}

### Base Game {#Base_Game_2}

- [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md) multiply gold gain by 1.2.
- [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), [Bat Country](Bat_Country.md), and [Tiny Bridge](Tiny_Bridge.md) multiply gold gain by 1.3.
- [Cappella Magna](Cappella_Magna.md) multiply gold gain by 1.4.
- [The Bone Zone](The_Bone_Zone.md) multiply gold gain by 1.5.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell_2}

- [Mt.Moonspell](Mt.Moonspell.md) multiply gold gain by 1.5.

The [hyper modes](Hyper_mode.md) of all [stages](Stage.md), except [Holy Forbidden](Holy_Forbidden.md) and [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md), give a +50% gold gain bonus additively.

The [inverse modes](Inverse_mode.md) of all stages, except Holy Forbidden and Eudaimonia Machine, give a +200% gold gain bonus additively.
