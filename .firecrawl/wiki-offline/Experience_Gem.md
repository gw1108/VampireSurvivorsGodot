**Experience Gems** are [pickups](Pickup.md) that can be dropped by killed [enemies](Enemy.md). Collecting enough experience allows the player to [level up](Level_up.md). The amount of experience gained is affected by the [Growth](Growth.md) stat.

## Mechanics {#Mechanics}

Experience Gems are pulled in by the player when they get in the player\'s [Magnet](Magnet.md) radius.

 [Vacuum](Vacuum.md) pickup, and  [Gorgeous Moon](Gorgeous_Moon.md) can be used to collect all Experience Gems from the ground.  [Summon Spirit Tornado](Summon_Spirit_Tornado.md) can also collect Experience Gems.

 [Luminaire](Luminaire.md)  can increase the value of the Experience Gems.

Experience Gems have varying value depending on their color. Blue Experience Gems grant up to 2 XP, green Experience Gems up to 9 XP and red gems contain anything beyond. If there are more than 400 Experience Gems on the ground at a time, no more gems can be dropped and any experience gained is instead accumulated into a single red Experience Gem. During the pulsing and chiming phase of Gorgeous Moon, it produces a total of 20 moon beams which erase enemies and generate uniquely colored Experience Gems (orange, yellow, magenta) that yield 1 experience next to their usual gem drop. Luminaire can randomize the color of the gems.

Enemies killed by the  [Emerald Wave](Emerald_Wave.md)  will always drop red gems.

### Level up {#Level_up}

Main article: [Level up](Level_up.md)

When the player collects enough experience, they will gain a level up. Each successive level up requires more experience than the previous. Upon level up the game is paused and the player is given 3 or 4 options consisting of [weapons](Weapon.md) and [passive item](Passive_item.md) to choose from.

## Notes {#Notes}

- Players can pick up a green Experience Gem, containing 5 experience, near the starting areas in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md) and [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md).
- A total of 15 blue Experience Gems can be found surrounding 3  [Little Clovers](Little_Clover.md) in [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md).
