Max Health

Max Health is one of the player stats in Vampire Survivors. It determines how much health the player's character can have in total.

The base value of Max Health is 100 before modifications, such as the characters' bonuses or PowerUps. The characters' Max Health modification is additive. Max Health modifications from PowerUps and items are calculated after the character modification and stack multiplicatively.

There is no upper limit to how much Max Health the player can have.

Max Health also affects:

  • Base Damage and Amount of  Mazo Familiar
  • Damage of explosions created by  Vanitas Whip 

The amount of missing Health affects Cooldown of  Metal Claw .

With  Divine Bloodline (IX) activated, all weapons gain +0.1 flat damage per missing Health, uncapped.

The Max Health PowerUp increases Max Health exponentially, multiplying it by 1.1 each rank up to a maximum rank of 3. At rank 3, it multiplies Max Health by 1.331. It initially costs 200.

Rank Max Health
1 1.1x
2 1.21x
3 1.331x

The calculation of initial Max Health is done differently from other stats, similarly to that of Magnet:

Max Health=(100+character bonus)*PowerUp multiplier

For example,  Poe reduces Max Health by 30, so using Poe with the PowerUp at rank 3, Max Health would be:

(100−30)*1.331=93.17

From items

[edit | edit source]

Max Health's associated passive item is  Hollow Heart, which multiplies Max Health by 1.2 each time it is leveled up. Its maximum level is 5. At level 5 it provides a 2.49x multiplier to Max Health.

  •  Metaglio Left multiplies Max Health by 1.05 each time it is leveled up. Its maximum level is 9. At level it provides a 1.477x multiplier to Max Health.
  •  Paranormal Scan  has a chance of granting a Max Health stat bonus of +20 upon activation.
  •  Golden Eggs can permanently increase Max Health by +1 for the character that collected it.

From arcana

[edit | edit source]
  •  Awake (IV) provides +10% Max Health per  Revival consumed.
  •  Divine Bloodline (IX) provides +0.5 Max Health per enemy killed by retaliatory damage.

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • Max Health value shown in the Character Selection menu doesn't increase exponentially, which is incorrect.
  • Max Health value shown in the stats panel, both in the Character Selection menu and in-game, is rounded to the closest whole number. The actual value of Max Health is a decimal number.
  • Negative Max Health makes the character invulnerable.
  •  White Hand kills the character by setting Max Health to 0 (even if Max Health is set to be negative).

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Max Health bonuses are simultaneously more useful and less useful for characters that have less Max Health: The Hollow Heart provides them with especially-important survivability, but it will not scale as fast or as high as other characters. On the opposite side, Max Health Bonuses are especially potent while playing as  Clerici.
