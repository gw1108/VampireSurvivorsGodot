> Said to have evolved from verminous creatures that flocked from a world trapped in time. Their lupine nature has been called into question, but if you ask for their opinion, they'll tear you to shreds with a most uncommon brutality. They must be sensitive about it.
>
> **--- [Ars Gouda](Ars_Gouda.md), p. 12**

**Werewolf** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), with additional appearances in [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md) and [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md).

In Mad Forest, it appears as both a normal enemy and a [boss](Boss.md). In Boss Rash, it appears exclusively as a boss. In Dairy Plant, it spawns as a special enemy after collecting the [Cheese](Minnah_Mannarah.md#Unlocking) [pickup](Pickups.md) on the stage.

Werewolf takes reduced [knockback](Knockback.md). It has two distinct boss variants: *Boss 1*, also known as \"Giant Werewolf\", whose [health scales with the player\'s level](HP_x_Level.md), and *Boss 2*, also known as \"Colossal Werewolf\", which does not.
