The **Merchant** is a mechanic in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*, which allows the purchasing of rare [Weapons](Weapon.md) and [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md) using [Gold Coins](Gold_Coin_(currency).md). They initially only appear in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md). Then later in all other stages after the purchase of the [Glass Vizard](Glass_Vizard.md).

## Other Merchants {#Other_Merchants}

The Merchant is not the only vendor in the game.

- The  [Master Librarian](Master_Librarian_(Merchant).md) from [Ode to Castlevania](Ode_to_Castlevania.md), who allows purchasing of various Ode to Castlevania weapons.
- Adventure Merchants, accessible inside of [Adventures](Adventures.md), especially inside the [Planar Bazaar](Planar_Bazaar.md) stage. The Adventure Merchants sell a plethora of items and unlock them for the Adventure the player is currently in.
  - All of the Merchants in Planar Bazaar also happen to be player characters as well, except for  [Xanthia](Xanthia.md), who can also be seen selling the  [Emerald Disk](Emerald_Disk.md) and the  [Joker Disk](Joker_Disk.md) in Moongolow.
- The  [Eggman](Eggman.md) in [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), who allows for bulk purchasing of [Golden Eggs](Golden_Egg.md).
- The  [Healer](Healer.md) in Moongolow, who allows for the removal of Golden Eggs.
-  [Giocare](Giocare.md), who appears in [Westwoods](Westwoods.md), sells spins for the [Spin the Wheel!](Spin_the_Wheel!.md) minigame.
-  [Big Troubler](Big_Troubler_(Merchant).md), who appears at the top of [The Lycaeum](The_Lycaeum.md) and sells the  [Green Eyes Vizard](Green_Eyes_Vizard.md),  [Survarocchi](Survarocchi.md), and  [Enemahs](Enemahs.md).

## Notes {#Notes}

- In order to access the shop, the player needs to touch the merchant.
- [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) applies to the current character being played but its effects will not apply until after the current game ends.
- All rare weapons ([Bone](Bone.md), [Cherry Bomb](Cherry_Bomb.md), [Carréllo](Carréllo.md), [Celestial Dusting](Celestial_Dusting.md), [La Robba](La_Robba.md)) bought lasts for the current game and cannot be bought if the max weapon limit is already reached.
- The player is unable to purchase weapons if the player\'s weapon slots are full already.
- The merchant can only be accessed once per run. Once the player exits the shop, the merchant disappears from the map.
  - The merchant can be accessed infinite times in [Endless Mode](Endless_mode.md), spawning every cycle.
- The merchant does not disappear after a certain time. They will remain like this until the player interacts with them.
