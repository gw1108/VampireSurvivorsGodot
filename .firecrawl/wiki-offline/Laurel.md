**Laurel** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Divano Thelma](Divano_Thelma.md). It is unlocked by default.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Crimson Shroud](Crimson_Shroud.md), which requires  [Metaglio Left](Metaglio_Left.md) and  [Metaglio Right](Metaglio_Right.md) at maximum level.

## Effects {#Effects}

Upon triggering, Laurel creates a shield. The next time the player takes damage, the shield loses a charge instead and briefly makes the player [invulnerable](Invulnerable.md). When the shield is out of charge, it breaks. The shield\'s color changes based on the remaining charges: one is blue, two is green, and three is yellow. Taking a hit during Laurel\'s invulnerability window does not trigger the shield.

Laurel only benefits from bonuses to  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md). Unlike most weapons, Laurel does not benefit from [Limit Break](Limit_Break.md).

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Blood Astronomia (XXI)](Blood_Astronomia_(XXI).md) - Allows Laurel to place a damaging zone on the ground. The zone deals heavy damage.

## Levels {#Levels}

Laurel has 7 levels. At level 7, Laurel has -2 seconds of [Cooldown](Cooldown.md), +0.8 seconds of [invulnerability](Invulnerable.md), and 2 additional charges.

| Level 2 | Cooldown reduced by 0.5 seconds. Shield invulnerability increased by 0.2 seconds. |
| Level 3 | Cooldown reduced by 0.5 seconds. Shield invulnerability increased by 0.2 seconds. |
| Level 4 | Gains 1 additional charge. |
