**Bracer** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by getting [King Bible](King_Bible.md) to Level 4.

Bracer increases [Projectile Speed](Speed.md) by 10% per level.

Bracer is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Knife](Knife.md) into [Thousand Edge](Thousand_Edge.md). It is also required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Flash Arrow](Flash_Arrow.md) into [Millionaire](Millionaire.md), in which Bracer must be fully leveled.

Bracer can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), and [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md).
