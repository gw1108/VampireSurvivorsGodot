# Passive items {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

**Passive Items** (also referred to as **Passive Weapons**) are non-weapon items that give the player bonuses to their [stats](Player_stats.md), similar to [PowerUps](PowerUp.md). They are offered to the player alongside [weapons](Weapon.md) whenever the player levels up.

The player can select up to 6 different passive items from level ups, but it is possible to carry more by picking up passive items that [generate with the stage](Passive_item.md#Stage_items). Most passive items must be unlocked before they can be offered to the player. The [Survarot](Survarot.md) of Zi\'Assunta Belpaese will provide +1 passive weapon slot after gaining a set amount of gold.

Most passive items are unique, which means a character can only get them once per run, and there is no way to get the same passive item multiple times (even if it is consumed, like [Tirajisú](Tirajisú.md)). Exceptions include  [Weapon Power-Up](Weapon_Power-Up.md)  and Familiars from  [Familiar Forge](Familiar_Forge.md) .

Most passive items can be used to [evolve](Evolution.md) a particular normal weapon into a stronger one.

See [Combos](Weapons/Combos.md) page for a list of which [Weapon](Weapon.md) and [Arcana](Arcana.md) is affected by [stats](Player_stats.md) and their corresponding passive items, [PowerUps](PowerUp.md), and Arcana.

## Types {#Types}

The base game has 21 passive items, 17 of which are normal passive items and 4 of which are special passive items. In addition, there are 13 passive items that are only available in DLCs: 1 from the [Tides of the Foscari DLC](Tides_of_the_Foscari.md), 8 from the [Emergency Meeting DLC](Emergency_Meeting.md), 1 from the [Operation Guns DLC](Operation_Guns.md), 2 from the [Ode to Castlevania DLC](Ode_to_Castlevania.md), and 1 from the [Ante Chamber DLC](Ante_Chamber.md), bringing the total number of passive items in the game to 34.

There are certain passive items in the game referred to as \"special passive items\", which cannot appear normally from the level-up choices and must be collected from a stage or by fulfilling set conditions in order to obtain them. These special passives include the passive items unlocked after collecting the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md) and most of DLC-exclusive passive items.

On each normal [stage](Stage.md), certain passive items spawn on the map. [Milky Way Map](Milky_Way_Map.md) displays the locations of these items. Each item is guaranteed to appear at least once, but certain items have a chance of spawning more than once, depending on the starting [Luck](Luck.md).

Upon moving to an item, it can be picked up or discarded. If the player already has the item, it grants a free level if not at max level. If they already have 6 or more passive items, it becomes an additional passive item and can be thereafter upgraded from level ups. If discarded, the item disappears and can no longer be picked up.

### Hidden stage items {#Hidden_stage_items}

After obtaining the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md), four additional passive items will spawn in all normal, bonus and challenge stages. These passive items cannot be obtained through any other means than picking them up during a run. The first time entering a stage after getting Yellow Sign, the game will automatically pause to show the new hidden stage items that were added:

- [Silver Ring](Silver_Ring.md), usually found due north of the starting area.
- [Gold Ring](Gold_Ring.md), usually found due south of the starting area.
- [Metaglio Left](Metaglio_Left.md), usually found due west of the starting area.
- [Metaglio Right](Metaglio_Right.md), usually found due east of the starting area.

Each hidden stage item is guarded by an [Atlantean](Atlantean.md) that spawns when any player gets close to the item. The player does not have to defeat the guard to pick up the item, but they will continue to follow the player until defeated. If the stage item was attracted using [Mad Groove (VIII)](Mad_Groove_(VIII).md), the guard will not appear until the item is interacted with.

- By approaching near the item without picking it up (prior to obtaining Mad Groove (VIII)), If it is later picked up from Mad Groove, a second Atlantean can be spawned for each hidden stage item.
