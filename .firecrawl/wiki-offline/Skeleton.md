**Skeleton** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md), [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), and [The Bone Zone](The_Bone_Zone.md), with additional appearances in [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md) and [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md).

In Mad Forest, Gallo Tower, The Bone Zone, and Boss Rash, it appears exclusively as a normal enemy. In Dairy Plant, it spawns both as a normal enemy and during **[Swarm](Swarm.md)** [map events](Map_event.md). In Eudaimonia Machine, it is summoned by [The Directer](The_Directer.md) as a special enemy during the second phase of the stage.

Defeating a total of 3,000 Skeletons unlocks the [character](Characters.md) ** [Mortaccio](Mortaccio.md)**.
