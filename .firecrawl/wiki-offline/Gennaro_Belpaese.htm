Gennaro Belpaese is one of the playable characters in Vampire Survivors. His starting weapon is the  Knife.

Unlocking

[edit | edit source]

He is unlocked by default. He needs to be purchased for 500, which scales with other characters purchased.

Passive bonuses

[edit | edit source]

Gennaro starts with +20  Max Health and +1  Amount.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Gennaro's innate Amount bonus makes him one of the stronger character early in the game, but this initial boost falls behind in the mid and late game.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • Gennaro's family name Belpaese is derived from the phrase Bel Paese, which is Italian for "beautiful country", and also refers to a semi-soft Italian cheese with the same name.
  • Gennaro's name is the Italian version of Latin Januarius, referring to Janus, the two-faced god.
