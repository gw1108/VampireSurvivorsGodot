The **Golden Egg** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that permanently increases a random [stat](Player_stats.md) of the player\'s current character by a small amount.

## Obtaining {#Obtaining}

Golden Eggs can be purchased repeatedly from the  [Merchant](Merchant.md) or the  [Eggman](Eggman.md), who appears when the player has at least 5,000 Golden Eggs on their save file and is currently holding at least 1,000,000, for 10,000. The Eggman also has the option to buy max eggs, using all of the player\'s gold. Additionally, the following special enemies can drop a Golden Egg when killed:

-  [Moon Atlantean](Moon_Atlantean.md)
-  [Sun Atlantean](Sun_Atlantean.md)
-  [City Atlantean](City_Atlantean.md)
-  [Volcano Atlantean](Volcano_Atlantean.md)
-  [Moongolow Atlanteans](Moongolow_Atlanteans.md)
-  [The Reaper](The_Reaper.md) will drop five Golden Eggs when killed if the player is holding the  [Infinite Corridor](Infinite_Corridor.md) or  [Crimson Shroud](Crimson_Shroud.md).

The amount of Golden Eggs a single character can have is hard capped at 3.402823E+38, which is the [32-bit floating point maximum](Single-precision_floating-point_format.md).

Unlike all other characters,  [Queen Sigma](Queen_Sigma.md) is completely unaffected by Golden Eggs. When playing as her, Golden Eggs in the Merchant\'s shop are replaced with the  [Candybox](Candybox.md), and Golden Eggs dropped by Atlanteans or The Reaper will be thrown away when picked up.

## Effects {#Effects}

Each Golden Egg will provide a small permanent boost to one of the 19 stats available by the following amount:

- +1  [Max Health](Max_Health.md)
- +0.1  [Recovery](Recovery.md),  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md),  [Amount](Amount.md),  [Revival](Revival.md),  [Reroll](Reroll.md)
- +1%  [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md),  [Might](Might.md),  [Speed](Speed.md),  [Duration](Duration.md),  [Area](Area.md),  [Luck](Luck.md),  [Growth](Growth.md),  [Greed](Greed.md),  [Curse](Curse.md)
- -0.5%  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md)
- +0.3  [Magnet](Magnet.md)
- +0.2  [Skip](Skip.md),  [Banish](Banish.md)

Golden Eggs boosts the character\'s starting stats. It does not however increase the character stats while in-game. As a result, any golden eggs obtained in-game will take effect for the next game session.

There is a hard cap on certain stats, even if visually the number displayed is higher. The following are stats with hard caps:

- +50  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md) (with the exception of  [Toastie](Toastie.md))
- +900%  [Might](Might.md)
- +400%  [Speed](Speed.md)
- +400%  [Duration](Duration.md)
- +900%  [Area](Area.md)
- -90%  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md)
- +10  [Amount](Amount.md)

## Removal {#Removal}

The  [Healer](Healer.md) will appear in Moongolow when a character has at least 5,000 Golden Eggs. The Healer can remove 100 Golden Eggs at once, or, when that same character has at least 25,000 Golden Eggs, all eggs that grant Move Speed.
