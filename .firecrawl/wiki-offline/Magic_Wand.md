**Magic Wand** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Imelda Belpaese](Imelda_Belpaese.md), and one of the starting weapons of  [Wood Rod](Innocent_Devil.md#Wood_Rod) . It is unlocked by default.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Holy Wand](Holy_Wand.md), which requires  [Empty Tome](Empty_Tome.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Magic Wand shoots a blue magic missile that is fired at the closest enemy to the player.

Additional projectiles from  [Amount](Amount.md) will be fired in a sequence. Each missile can initially hit one enemy before dissipating. The missiles cannot pass through walls.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Waltz of Pearls (XI)](Waltz_of_Pearls_(XI).md) - Allows Magic Wand\'s projectiles to bounce off enemies up to three times.
-  [Out of Bounds (XII)](Out_of_Bounds_(XII).md) - When paired with [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md), enemies frozen by Magic Wand\'s projectiles will explode.
-  [Jail of Crystal (XIV)](Jail_of_Crystal_(XIV).md) - Gives Magic Wand a 25% chance to [freeze](Freeze.md) enemies. This chance is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md).
-  [Crystal Cries (XII)](Crystal_Cries_(XII).md) - When paired with Jail of Crystal (XIV), enemies frozen by Magic Wand\'s projectiles will drop  [Crystallized Souls](Crystallized_Soul.md) when killed.

## Levels {#Levels}

Magic Wand has 8 levels. At max level, Magic Wand has +20 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +3 [Amount](Amount.md), +1 [Pierce](Pierce.md), and -0.2 seconds of [Cooldown](Cooldown.md).

|         |                                  |
|---------|----------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                      |
| Level 1 | Fires at the nearest enemy.      |
| Level 2 | Fires 1 more projectile.         |
| Level 3 | Cooldown reduced by 0.2 seconds. |
| Level 4 | Fires 1 more projectile.         |
| Level 5 | Base Damage up by 10.            |
| Level 6 | Fires 1 more projectile.         |
| Level 7 | Passes through 1 more enemy.     |
| Level 8 | Base Damage up by 10.            |
