**Stone Mask** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by finding it as a stage item for the first time.

Stone Mask increases [Greed](Greed.md) by 10% per level.

Stone Mask is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Gatti Amari](Gatti_Amari.md), [Vicious Hunger](Vicious_Hunger.md). It is also required for the evolution of [Night Sword](Night_Sword.md), [Muramasa](Muramasa.md), in which Stone Mask must also be fully leveled.

Stone Mask can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Mt.Moonspell](Mt.Moonspell.md), and [Abyss Foscari](Abyss_Foscari.md).

## Levels {#Levels}
