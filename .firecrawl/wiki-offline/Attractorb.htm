Attractorb is a passive item in Vampire Survivors. It is unlocked upon finding a Vacuum dropped by a light source.

Attractorb gives multiplicative bonuses of varying size to the player's Magnet stat.

Attractorb is required for the evolution of Santa Water, La Borra. It is also required for the evolution of Mirage Robe, J'Odore, in which Attractorb must also be fully leveled.

Attractorb can be found as a stage item in Dairy Plant, Moongolow, Boss Rash, Tiny Bridge, Mt.Moonspell, and Abyss Foscari.

Tips

[edit | edit source]
  • Attractorb's bonuses to Magnet apply multiplicatively. Each level will increase your Magnet by a percent of the current total, not the original total. This means that the Attractorb gives you more powerful bonuses as you level it up.
    • Each level up approximate to a round multiplier of 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, and 4x respectively.
  • Attractorb is usually better at increasing experience gain than Crown. While Crown provides small bonuses to the actual value of experience, Attractorb makes experience much easier to collect. Combining the two is the best way to gain experience in the entire game.
  • Attractorb also increases the pickup range of other items. This is useful for picking up Floor Chicken and other consumables at large distances, as to avoid putting yourself in a dangerous scenario. Treasure Chests must be walked over no matter how much Magnet one has.
