Gold Finger is an item pickup in Vampire Survivors. It starts out exclusive to Astral Stair. After  Trisection is obtained, Gold Fingers become available in all stages. It is also the rarest drop from destroyed light sources.

Picking up 5 Gold Fingers will unlock the Space 54 stage.

Effects

[edit | edit source]

Picking up Gold Finger grants the character a 14 second period of invulnerability and 10 seconds of set enemies spawned to 500 and -100%  Cooldown, and offers variable rewards after said period ends.

 Karoma's Mana increases Gold Finger duration by 10% per level, up to +50% at max, making a total duration of 21 seconds.

The enemies killed threshold of each tier and its reward being:

  • Bronze: 0 + Player Level; Healing (Pet command) + bouncy weapon ( Bone,  Cherry Bomb,  Carréllo,  Celestial Dusting).
  • Silver: 500 + Player Level x 2; 32 Gold Coins + healing + Little Clover + random pickup between  Gilded Clover,  Nduja Fritta Tanto, or  Rosary.
  • Gold: 1000 + Player Level x 3; 64 Gold Coins +  Floor Chicken +  Treasure Chest (cannot evolve, 3% chance to have 5 items, 33% to have 3)
  • Demon: 1500 + Player Level x 4; 32 Gold Coins + Treasure Chest (all five slots can evolve, 3% chance to have 5 items, 33% to have 3)
  • Cosmic: 2500 + Player Level x 5; 64 Gold Coins + Treasure Chest (same as above) + random weapon:
    •  Greatest Jubilee,  Torrona's Box, or  Victory Sword; including  Candybox if the player has less than 6 weapons; including anima weapon ( Anima of Mortaccio,  Yatta Daikarin,  Carozza!,  Profusione D'Amore) if the player has less than 7 weapons and does not already have an anima weapon.

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • If a Gold Finger is a stage item (like in Astral Stair or in Space 54), it cannot be attracted by the  Mad Groove (VIII).

[edit | edit source]
  • According to the trailer for the 1.5.0 update, the "Demon Finger" tier was originally supposed to be called "Devil Finger".[1]
