> Sorcerous experimentation sought to discover if a skeleton, once rendered animate, could toil more efficiently were its head and body split into two separate entities. The answer is no. Of course not. These former farmers, unable to lift the rakes and hoes they so loved, can now only gnash their teeth in eternally silent frustration.
>
> **--- [Ars Gouda](Ars_Gouda.md), p. 3**

**Skullino** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) that appears in [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), [The Bone Zone](The_Bone_Zone.md), [Tiny Bridge](Tiny_Bridge.md), and [Eudaimonia Machine](Eudaimonia_Machine.md). In Gallo Tower and The Bone Zone, it only appears as a normal enemy. In Eudaimonia Machine, it is summoned by [The Directer](The_Directer.md).
