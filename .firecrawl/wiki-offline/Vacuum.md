The **Vacuum** is a [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md). It can only drop after the character reaches level 12, and its drop rate is affected by [Luck](Luck.md).

Collecting the Vacuum for the first time will unlock the  [Attractorb](Attractorb.md).

Picking up 50 Vacuums will unlock the [The Lycaeum](The_Lycaeum.md) stage.

## Effects {#Effects}

Picking up the Vacuum pulls all the  [Experience Gems](Experience_Gem.md) lying on the ground towards the player\'s character and collects them.

### Lavatrix Machina {#Lavatrix_Machina}

 [Penshin Fatcha](Penshin_Fatcha.md) that has been evolved once gains a special effect that turns Vacuums into Lavatrix Machinas. This effect spins enemies around and can collect [Treasure Chests](Treasure_Chest.md), [light sources](Light_source.md), [stage items](Stage_item.md), and [pickups](Pickup.md).
