**Clover** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by finding a [Little Clover](Little_Clover.md) from a [light source](Light_source.md).

Clover increases [Luck](Luck.md) by 10% per level.

Clover is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Cross](Cross.md), [Heaven Sword](Heaven_Sword.md). It is also required for the evolution of [Santa Javelin](Santa_Javelin.md), [Seraphic Cry](Seraphic_Cry.md) in which it must also be fully leveled.

Clover can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), and [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Clover has 5 levels. At max level, Clover provides +50% [Luck](Luck.md).

|---------|---------------------------------|

| Level 1 | \"Character gets 10% luckier.\" |
| Level 2 | Chance to get lucky up by 10%   |
| Level 3 | Chance to get lucky up by 10%   |
| Level 4 | Chance to get lucky up by 10%   |
| Level 5 | Chance to get lucky up by 10%   |
