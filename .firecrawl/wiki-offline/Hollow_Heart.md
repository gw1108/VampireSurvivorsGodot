**Hollow Heart** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by surviving 1 minute as any [character](Character.md).

It multiplies  [Max Health](Max_Health.md) by 1.2 per level and can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), and [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Hollow Heart has 5 levels. Each of them multiplies the current value of  [Max Health](Max_Health.md) by 1.2.

|       |                                |                     |
|-------|--------------------------------|---------------------|
| Level | Description                    | Effect total        |
| 1     | \"Augment max health by 20%.\" | 1.2x Max Health     |
| 2     | Max health increases by 20%    | 1.44x Max Health    |
| 3     | Max health increases by 20%    | 1.728x Max Health   |
| 4     | Max health increases by 20%    | 2.0736x Max Health  |
| 5     | Max health increases by 20%    | 2.48832x Max Health |
