**Garlic** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Poe Ratcho](Poe_Ratcho.md) and  [Bitterfly](Innocent_Devil.md#Bitterfly) . It is unlocked by picking up five  [Floor Chicken](Floor_Chicken.md).

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Soul Eater](Soul_Eater.md), which requires  [Pummarola](Pummarola.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Garlic places an aura of three rotating concentric circles around the player, dealing damage to enemies inside it.

 [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) acts similarly to [Hitbox Delay](Weapons.md#Stats) for Garlic. Damage will be dealt instantly to an enemy if they have never been in the circle before, after which they cannot be hit by it again until the Garlic\'s Cooldown is over, even if they temporarily leave its area.

[Enemies](Enemies.md) hit by Garlic will take more [knockback](Knockback.md) and become more vulnerable to [freeze](Freeze.md). If the enemy is not immune to debuffs, each hit applies +0.3 knockback to the enemy until the maximum knockback they can take and -0.1 to freeze resistance until it reaches 0.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Tragic Princess (III)](Tragic_Princess_(III).md) - Reduces Garlic\'s  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) when the character is moving. It is affected by  [Move Speed](Move_Speed.md).
-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) - Allows Garlic to deal extra flat damage based on  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md).
-  [Blood Astronomia (XXI)](Blood_Astronomia_(XXI).md) - Allows Garlic to place multiple damage zones on the ground.

## Levels {#Levels}

Garlic has 8 levels. At max level, Garlic has +10 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +100% [Area](Area.md), and -0.3 seconds of [Cooldown](Cooldown.md).

|----|----|
| Level | Description |

| Level 2 | Base Area up by 40%. Base Damage up by 2. |
| Level 3 | Cooldown reduced by 0.1 seconds. Base Damage up by 1. |
| Level 4 | Base Area up by 20%. Base Damage up by 1. |
| Level 5 | Cooldown reduced by 0.1 seconds. Base Damage up by 2. |
| Level 6 | Base Area up by 20%. Base Damage up by 1. |
| Level 7 | Cooldown reduced by 0.1 seconds. Base Damage up by 1. |
