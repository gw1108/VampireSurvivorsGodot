**Song of Mana** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Poppea Pecorina](Poppea_Pecorina.md). It is unlocked by surviving 15 minutes as Poppea Pecorina.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Mannajja](Mannajja.md), which requires  [Skull O\'Maniac](Skull_O'Maniac.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Song of Mana pulses a wave of sparkling particles outwards in the shape of a vertical ellipse, bouncing along the edges of a vertical column centered around the character. The particles are positioned relative to the character, and follow the character\'s movement.

Song of Mana has a 1 second [Hitbox Delay](Weapon.md#Stats), meaning that the same enemy cannot be hit more often than every second by the same pulse. However, because all active delays are reset simultaneously as soon as any one elapses, some enemies may get hit more often.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) - Allows the Song of Mana to deal extra flat damage based on  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md).
-  [Blood Astronomia (XXI)](Blood_Astronomia_(XXI).md) - Allows the Song of Mana to emit special damaging zones. These zones take the form of several planets orbiting around a point.

## Levels {#Levels}

Song of Mana has 8 levels. At max level, Song of Mana has +30 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +175% [Area](Area.md), +1.5 seconds to [Duration](Duration.md), and +2.25 seconds to [Cooldown](Cooldown.md).

|  |  |
|----|----|
| Level | Description |
| Level 1 | Attacks vertically, passes through enemies. |
| Level 2 | Base Area up by 100%. |
| Level 3 | Effect lasts 0.5 seconds longer. Cooldown increased by 0.8 seconds.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-cooldown-1)^ |
| Level 4 | Base Area up by 25%. Base Damage up by 10. |
| Level 5 | Effect lasts 0.5 seconds longer. Cooldown increased by 0.8 seconds.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-cooldown-1)^ |
| Level 6 | Base Area up by 25%. Base Damage up by 10. |
| Level 7 | Effect lasts 0.5 seconds longer. Cooldown increased by 0.8 seconds.^[\[1\]](#cite_note-cooldown-1)^ |
| Level 8 | Base Area up by 25%. Base Damage up by 10. |
