**Karoma\'s Mana** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by collecting it in any [stage](Stages.md).

Karoma\'s Mana increases  [Charm](Charm.md) by 10 and  [Fever](Fever.md) by 10% per level.

Karoma\'s Mana can be found as a stage item on [Westwoods](Westwoods.md), [Ode to Castlevania](Ode_to_Castlevania_(stage).md), and [Emerald Diorama](Emerald_Diorama_(stage).md).
