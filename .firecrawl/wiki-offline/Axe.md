**Axe** is a [weapon](Weapon.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Lama Ladonna](Lama_Ladonna.md) and  [Axe Armor](Axe_Armor.md) , and one of the starting weapons of  [missingN▯](MissingN▯.md). It is unlocked by default.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Death Spiral](Death_Spiral.md), which requires  [Candelabrador](Candelabrador.md).

As a vampire-killing tool, it can be picked from  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md) .

## Effects {#Effects}

Axe throws projectiles above the player, which arc downward. The first axe is thrown directly above the character and subsequent axes from  [Amount](Amount.md) are thrown in quick succession towards the direction the player is facing in progressively wider arcs.  [Speed](Speed.md) determines how far up axes can be thrown before they start falling. Each axe can initially hit up to three enemies before dissipating.

Axe scales with the character\'s  [Area](Area.md) stat multiplied by 1.3.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Iron Blue Will (VII)](Iron_Blue_Will_(VII).md) - Allows Axe\'s projectiles to bounce off enemies up to three times, occasionally have infinite [Pierce](Pierce.md), and pass through walls. Whenever an axe bounces off an enemy, it gains upwards momentum like it is being thrown again, but in the opposite direction in the horizontal axis.
-  [Slash (XVI)](Slash_(XVI).md) - Allows Axe to critical hit. Its critical hits deal 4x damage and have 30% chance to occur. This chance is affected by  [Luck](Luck.md).
-  [Pale Diamond Incursion (V)](Pale_Diamond_Incursion_(V).md) - Axe now aims at nearest enemy instead of thrown upward, and gains  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) bonus based on  [Speed](Speed.md).
