**King Bible** is a [weapon](Weapons.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is the starting weapon of  [Dommario](Dommario.md). It is unlocked by default.

Its [evolution](Evolution.md) is  [Unholy Vespers](Unholy_Vespers.md), which requires  [Spellbinder](Spellbinder.md).

As a vampire-killing tool, it can be picked from  [Coat of Arms](Coat_of_Arms.md) .

## Effects {#Effects}

King Bible spawns a ring of blue bibles around the player that occasionally has pages fall out of it. The bibles spin clockwise around the player and deal damage to any enemy it touches.

Additional bibles from  [Amount](Amount.md) are added to the circle and spaced evenly.  [Speed](Speed.md) affects how fast the books rotate,  [Duration](Duration.md) affects how long they rotate before cooling down, and  [Area](Area.md) affects how far away from the player they spin which has a cap in size and increases the size of the bibles.

The  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) of King Bible depends on the weapon\'s Duration, meaning that its Cooldown is extended by its Duration of 3 seconds to 6 seconds total. The extension will increase with level-ups as they change the base weapon\'s stats, but it is not affected by other Duration bonuses.

King Bible has a 1.7 seconds [Hitbox Delay](Weapon.md#Stats), meaning that the same enemy cannot be hit more often than every 1.7 seconds by the same bible. However, because all active delays are reset simultaneously as soon as any one elapses, some enemies may get hit more often.

### Arcanas {#Arcanas}

-  [Twilight Requiem (II)](Twilight_Requiem_(II).md) - Allows King Bible\'s projectiles to explode when they expire. Damage is based on  [Curse](Curse.md).
-  [Divine Bloodline (IX)](Divine_Bloodline_(IX).md) - Allows King Bible to deal extra flat damage based on  [Armor](Armor_(stat).md).

## Levels {#Levels}

King Bible has 8 levels. At max level, it has +20 [Base Damage](Base_Damage.md), +50% [Area](Area.md), +60% [Speed](Speed.md), +3 [Amount](Amount.md), and +1 second to [Duration](Duration.md).

|         |                                                        |
|---------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| Level   | Description                                            |
| Level 1 | Orbits around the character.                           |
| Level 2 | Fires 1 more projectile.                               |
| Level 3 | Base Area up by 25%. Base Speed up by 30%.             |
| Level 4 | Effect lasts 0.5 seconds longer. Base Damage up by 10. |
| Level 5 | Fires 1 more projectile.                               |
| Level 6 | Base Area up by 25%. Base Speed up by 30%.             |
| Level 7 | Effect lasts 0.5 seconds longer. Base Damage up by 10. |
| Level 8 | Fires 1 more projectile.                               |
