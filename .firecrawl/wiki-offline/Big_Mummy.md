**Big Mummy** is an [enemy](Enemy.md) primarily encountered in [Mad Forest](Mad_Forest.md), with additional appearances in [Inlaid Library](Inlaid_Library.md) and [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md).

In Mad Forest, it appears as both a normal enemy and a boss. In Inlaid Library and Boss Rash, it appears exclusively as a boss.

Big Mummy is semi-resistant to [knockback](Knockback.md). Its boss variant has [health that scales with the player\'s level](HP_x_Level.md) and is partially resistant to [freeze](Freeze.md).
