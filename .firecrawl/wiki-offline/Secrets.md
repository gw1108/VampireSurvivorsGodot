The **Secrets** menu is unlocked after obtaining the  [Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane](Forbidden_Scrolls_of_Morbane.md). It shows hints to unlocking secret [characters](Character.md), and gives the player the ability to cast [spells](Spells.md).

Players can temporarily unlock the Secrets menu by clicking on the ninth [relic](Relics.md) icon with the pink background (the [Yellow Sign](Yellow_Sign.md)) in the [collection](Collection.md) seven times. A message saying \"You are now a magician!\" will appear on the tooltip, and the secret menu will now be unlocked for the current session. This allows the player to cast spells right away, instead of having to slay the  [Sketamari](Sketamari.md). It must be triggered again each time the player opens the game until the relic is collected.

The following is a list of hints for finding secret characters. All secrets can be unlocked without the Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane, or using their respective spell. Once a spell for a character is used successfully in the Secrets menu, it cannot be input again, as fully typing out the spell again causes it to disappear with no effect.

## List of secrets {#List_of_secrets}

The codes listed here are not [Spells](Spells.md), and will not work when typed into the Secrets menu.

Although many are similar to spells, these are the game\'s internal code names/IDs for the secrets.

To see the full unlock method for any listed secret, visit the page for its unlocked character.
