# Reroll {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File">

</figure>

**Reroll** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It affects the amount of times the player can reroll the rewards when [leveling up](Level_up.md) or selecting an [Arcana](Arcana.md).

The items that were discarded by a reroll cannot appear in the new set of items. However, this does not completely remove them from the pool, and they can be offered again in the same level up if more rerolls are used. Arcanas that are rerolled, on the other hand, are removed from the pool and do not appear again in the same chest.

There is no upper limit to how many Rerolls the player may have.

The Reroll [PowerUp](PowerUp.md) increases Reroll by 2 per rank, with a maximum rank of 5 after unlocking all Reroll [achievements](Achievements.md). With one rank unlocked it initially costs 1,000.

|      |        |
|------|--------|
| Rank | Reroll |
| 1    | +2     |
| 2    | +4     |
| 3    | +6     |
| 4    | +8     |
| 5    | +10    |

Each rank of PowerUp is unlocked by these achievements:

- Reaching level 80 with  [Mortaccio](Mortaccio.md)
- Reaching level 80 with  [Yatta Cavallo](Yatta_Cavallo.md)
- Reaching level 80 with  [Bianca Ramba](Bianca_Ramba.md)
- Reaching level 80 with  [O\'Sole Meeo](O'Sole_Meeo.md)
- Reaching level 80 with  [Sir Ambrojoe](Sir_Ambrojoe.md)

## From items {#From_items}

Reroll does not have any associated [passive item](Passive_item.md).

The  [Rerollo](Rerollo.md) grants 1 Reroll for the character that collected it.

The  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) permanently increases Reroll by 0.1 for the character that collected it.
