The **Rosary** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md). It can only drop after the character reaches level 8, and its drop rate is affected by [Luck](Luck.md).

Collecting the Rosary for the first time unlocks the  [Cross](Cross.md).

Picking up 33 Rosaries will unlock the [Laborratory](Laborratory.md) [stage](Stages.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Picking up the Rosary instantly kills all [enemies](Enemies.md) on the screen.

Rosaries can attack and defeat the otherwise intangible  [Stalkers](The_Stalker.md) and  [Drowners](The_Drowner.md) in [Dairy Plant](Dairy_Plant.md), [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), and [Cappella Magna](Cappella_Magna.md) before the 30:00 mark, causing them to drop  [Treasure Chests](Treasure_Chest.md). However, some [enemies](Enemies.md), such as  [The Reaper](The_Reaper.md), are resistant or immune to the Rosary\'s effect.

### Weird Souls Purifier {#Weird_Souls_Purifier}

 [Seraphic Cry](Seraphic_Cry.md) gains a special effect that turns Rosaries into [Weird Souls Purifiers](Weird_Souls_Purifier.md). This effect triggers the Rosary\'s effect four times while also turning enemies into experience gems. Seraphic Cry also multiplies the drop weight of Rosaries by 3, increasing their weight from 1 to 3.
