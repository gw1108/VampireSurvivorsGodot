**Spellbinder** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by getting the  [Runetracer](Runetracer.md) to Level 7.

Spellbinder increases [Duration](Duration.md) by 10% per level.

Spellbinder is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of  [King Bible](King_Bible.md) and  [Unholy Vespers](Unholy_Vespers.md). It is also required for the evolution of  [Mille Bolle Blu](Mille_Bolle_Blu.md),  [Boo Roo Boolle](Boo_Roo_Boolle.md), in which Spellbinder must also be fully leveled.

Spellbinder can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Gallo Tower](Gallo_Tower.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), and [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md).
