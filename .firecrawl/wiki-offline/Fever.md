**Fever** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It increases the duration of  [Gold Fever](Gold_Fever.md) and  [Gold Finger](Gold_Finger.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Fever multiplies the duration of Gold Fever and Gold Finger, making them last longer and potentially achieve a higher coin count or better rewards respectively.

Note that this multiplier is separate from the Gold Fever cap and duration bonus of  [Big Trouser](Big_Trouser.md) and  [Sammy](Sammy.md) .

## From Items {#From_Items}

Fever\'s associated passive item is  [Karoma\'s Mana](Karoma's_Mana.md), which provides +10% Fever per level, up to a total of +50% Fever at level 5.

## From Survarot {#From_Survarot}

-  [FOIL Skill](Survarot.md#List_of_FOIL_Skills): Overhealing by +16 provides +1% Fever.
