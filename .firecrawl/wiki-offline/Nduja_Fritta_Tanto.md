**Nduja Fritta Tanto** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)* that has a chance to drop from [light sources](Light_source.md). Its drop rate is affected by [Luck](Luck.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Upon picking up Nduja Fritta Tanto, the player\'s character begins to breathe fire in the direction they are facing, similar to  [Knife](Knife.md) for 10 seconds. The flame will grow in size as they travel. It is coded to be a temporary weapon, so it can be affected by different [stat](Player_stats.md) increases.
