**Crown** is a [passive item](Passive_item.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It is unlocked by reaching Level 10 as any [character](Character.md).

Crown boosts [experience gain](Growth.md) by +8% per level.

Crown is required for the [evolution](Evolution.md) of [Pentagram](Pentagram.md), [Gorgeous Moon](Gorgeous_Moon.md). It is also required for the evolution of [Prismatic Missile](Prismatic_Missile.md), [Luminaire](Luminaire.md), in which Crown must also be fully leveled.

Crown can be found as a [stage item](Stage_item.md) in [Cappella Magna](Cappella_Magna.md), [Moongolow](Moongolow.md), [Boss Rash](Boss_Rash.md), [Lake Foscari](Lake_Foscari.md), and [Abyss Foscari](Abyss_Foscari.md).

## Levels {#Levels}

Crown has 5 levels. At max level, Crown provides +40% [Growth](Growth.md).

|---------|-----------------------------------------|

| Level 1 | \"Character gains 8% more experience.\" |
| Level 2 | XP gain increases by 8%.                |
| Level 3 | XP gain increases by 8%.                |
| Level 4 | XP gain increases by 8%.                |
| Level 5 | XP gain increases by 8%.                |
