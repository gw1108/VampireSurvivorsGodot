**Gold Coin** is an [pickup](Pickup.md) that increases your Gold Coin total by 1, multiplied by the  [Greed](Greed.md) stat. It is the most common drop from [light sources](Light_source.md).

Gold Coins are also the form of [currency](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) in the game.
