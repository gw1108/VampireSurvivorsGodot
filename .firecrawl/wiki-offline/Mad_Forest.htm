Mad Forest is the first stage in Vampire Survivors, unlocked from the very start of the game. It is the only stage that is available in the demo version of the game on itch.io, the others being completely inaccessible, rather than being locked like in the full version of the game.

Reaching level 80 in Mad Forest with inverse mode enabled unlocks Bat Country as playable stage.

Boon Marrabbio (enemy), which is a special enemy, will spawn under certain conditions in Mad Forest after obtaining Yellow Sign.

Description

[edit | edit source]

Once a thriving haven, now a dumping ground for evil. A vampire is said to be the root of this evil, but we can find only mayhem and roast chicken.

Overview

[edit | edit source]

Mad Forest features an open map with a large area and very few obstacles, giving players plenty of space to move around. The only destructible that spawn in this stage are light sources in the form of braziers, which will drop random pickups once broken.

Hyper mode

[edit | edit source]

The hyper mode for this stage can be unlocked by defeating the Giant Blue Venus (25:00) for the first time in normal mode. It can also be unlocked in the Forbidden Scrolls of Morbane by casting the spell "everywhere".

Hyper mode in Mad Forest has a +50% Gold bonus.

Stage items

[edit | edit source]

On this stage, 5 unique passive items can be found.

  •  Skull O'Maniac: 6 tilesets, northwest of starting area.
  •  Hollow Heart: Between 4 and 5 tilesets, north of starting area.
  •  Spinach: Up to four may appear with 30%, 20%, and 10% chance for each additional Spinach to spawn.
    • 2 tilesets northeast and 3 tilesets, east of the starting area (guaranteed to spawn).
    • 2 tilesets northeast and 5 tilesets, east of the starting area.
    • 2 tilesets southeast and 4 tilesets, east of the starting area.
    • 2 tilesets southeast and 6 tilesets, east of the starting area.
  •  Pummarola: 5 tilesets, south of starting area.
  •  Clover: Up to four may appear with 30%, 20%, and 10% chance for each additional Clover to spawn.
    • 3 tilesets southwest and 4 tilesets, west of the starting area (guaranteed to spawn).
    • 4 tilesets southwest and 2 tilesets, west of the starting area.
    • 4 tilesets southwest and 4 tilesets, west of the starting area.
    • 3 tilesets southwest and 5 tilesets, west of the starting area.

The 4 hidden stage items are also present, if the player has obtained the Yellow Sign.

  •  Silver Ring: 7 tilesets, north of the starting area.
  •  Metaglio Right: 7 tilesets, east of the starting area.
  •  Gold Ring: 7 tilesets, south of the starting area.
  •  Metaglio Left: 7 tilesets, west of the starting area.

Coffin

[edit | edit source]

One  Coffin is located in Mad Forest.

  •  Pugnala Provola: 6 tilesets, northeast of the starting area.

Waves

[edit | edit source]

Waves in Mad Forest have the Standard spawn type, meaning enemies appear from all four directions of the player.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • Mad Forest has the second lowest Move Speed bonuses compared to every other stage in the game, even including Adventure maps, with only Eudaimonia Machine having a lower Move Speed.
    • On the other hand, it has higher bonuses to Speed and Move Speed in its hyper mode.
    • Mad Forest is also the only stage that does not grant a Luck bonus in its hyper mode.
  • The name of this stage is a reference to the song "Mad Forest," which is the background theme of the Forest of Darkness in Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse.
  • Before version 1.0, the stage's description was as follows: "The castle is a lie, but there's still free roast chicken here, so it's all good."
