# Area {#firstHeading .firstHeading .mw-first-heading}

[Jump to navigation](#mw-head) [Jump to search](#searchInput)

</figure>

**Area** is one of the [player stats](Player_stats.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It increases the area of effect of most [weapons](Weapons.md).

The player has 100% base Area before modifications. The Area in the stat panel is given as the difference to the base Area, meaning that Area stat of +30% equals to 130% total Area, for example. Area modifications from [characters](Characters.md), [PowerUps](PowerUps.md), and items stack additively. Other modifiers, such as [Arcanas](Arcanas.md), stack multiplicatively with the collective bonuses from the aforementioned.

The upper limit of total Area after character, PowerUp and item modifications is 1000%. Bonuses from Arcanas are unaffected by the limit.

Area also affects:

- Base Damage of  [SpellStream](SpellStream.md) ,  [SpellStrom](SpellStrom.md) ,  [Sonic Dash](Sonic_Dash.md) ,  [Rapidus Fio](Rapidus_Fio.md) 
- Singularity damage of  [SpellStrom](SpellStrom.md) 
- Critical damage of  [Rune Sword](Rune_Sword.md)

The Area [PowerUp](PowerUps.md) increases Area by +5% per rank, with a maximum rank of 2. At rank 2, it provides +10% Area. It initially costs 300.

|       |      |
|-------|------|
| Level | Area |
| 1     | +5%  |
| 2     | +10% |

## From items {#From_items}

Area\'s associated [passive item](Passive_items.md) is the  [Candelabrador](Candelabrador.md), which provides +10% Area per level, up to a maximum of +50% Area at level 5.

-  [Silver Ring](Silver_Ring.md) also provide +5% per level, up to a maximum of +40% at level 9.
-  [Torrona\'s Box](Torrona's_Box.md) provides +4%, then +3% per level, up to a maximum of +25% at level 8.
-  [Golden Egg](Golden_Egg.md) can permanently increase Area by +1% for the character that collected it.

## From arcana {#From_arcana}

-  [Awake (IV)](Awake_(IV).md) provides +5% Area when chosen per  [Revival](Revival.md) consumed.
-  [Boogaloo of Illusions (XVIII)](Boogaloo_of_Illusions_(XVIII).md) provides a multiplier cycling between 0.75 to 1.25 every 10 seconds, and gives +1% Area every level.

## From characters {#From_characters}

-  [Porta](Porta_Ladonna.md) starts with +30% Area.
-  [Clerici](Suor_Clerici.md) starts with +400% Area, every level decreases by -100% until it returns to normal by level 5.
-  [Concetta](Concetta_Caciotta.md) gains +1% Area every level, with no cap.
-  [Zi\'Assunta](Zi'Assunta_Belpaese.md) gains +0.5% Area every level, with no cap.
-  [Exdash](Exdash_Exiviiq.md) and  [Toastie](Toastie.md) start with -10% Area.
-  [Smith IV](Smith_IV.md) starts with -10% Area and gains +0.7% Area every level, with no cap.
-  [Minnah](Minnah_Mannarah.md)\'s Area cycles between multiplied by 2 and divided by 2 every minute.
-  [Leda](Leda.md) starts with +10% Area.
-  [Peppino](Peppino.md) starts with -60% Area and gains +10% Area at level 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, and 21.
-  [Scorej-Oni](Scorej-Oni.md) starts with +20% Area.

### Legacy of the Moonspell {#Legacy_of_the_Moonspell}

-  [Syuuto](Syuuto_Moonspell.md) starts with -30% Area. He gains +30% Area after he morphs when his  [starting weapon](Summon_Night.md) evolves.
-  [McCoy-Oni](McCoy-Oni.md)\'s Area cycles between normal and multiplied by 2.5 every second.

### Emergency Meeting {#Emergency_Meeting}

-  [Scientist](Scientist_Mina.md) can select to gain +1% Area per level every 10 levels until level 50, for a maximum of up to +5% Area per level.

### Operation Guns {#Operation_Guns}

-  [Lucia](Lucia_Zero.md) gains +4% Area after [reviving](Revival.md), with no cap.
-  [Browny](Browny.md) gains 2% Area per -1%  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md) up to a maximum of +100% Area.

### Ode to Castlevania {#Ode_to_Castlevania}

-  [Charlotte](Charlotte_Aulin.md),  [Eric](Eric_Lecarde.md),  [Isaac](Isaac.md),  [Barlowe](Barlowe.md), start with +20% Area.
-  [Charlotte & Jonathan](Charlotte_&_Jonathan.md) starts with +40% Area.
-  [Joachim](Joachim_Armster.md) can select to gain +1% Area per level.
-  [Blackmore](Blackmore.md) can select to gain +1% Area per level after defeating 1000, 5000, 9000, 13000 and 17000 enemies, for a maximum of up to +5% Area per level.
-  [Leon](Leon_Belmont.md) gains +10% Area upon obtaining the  [Vampire Killer](Vampire_Killer.md).
-  [Henry](Henry.md) gains up to +100% area depending on the number of enemies in sight.
