**Gold Finger** is an [item pickup](Pickups.md) in *[Vampire Survivors](Vampire_Survivors.md)*. It starts out exclusive to [Astral Stair](Astral_Stair.md). After  [Trisection](Trisection.md) is obtained, Gold Fingers become available in all stages. It is also the rarest drop from destroyed [light sources](Light_source.md).

Picking up 5 Gold Fingers will unlock the [Space 54](Space_54.md) [stage](Stages.md).

## Effects {#Effects}

Picking up Gold Finger grants the character a 14 second period of invulnerability and 10 seconds of set enemies spawned to 500 and -100%  [Cooldown](Cooldown.md), and offers variable rewards after said period ends.

 [Karoma\'s Mana](Karoma's_Mana.md) increases Gold Finger duration by 10% per level, up to +50% at max, making a total duration of 21 seconds.

The enemies killed threshold of each tier and its reward being:

- Bronze: 0 + Player Level; Healing ([Pet command](Spells.md#Main_Menu_and_others)) + bouncy weapon ( [Bone](Bone.md),  [Cherry Bomb](Cherry_Bomb.md),  [Carréllo](Carréllo.md),  [Celestial Dusting](Celestial_Dusting.md)).
- Silver: 500 + Player Level x 2; 32 [Gold Coins](Gold_Coin_(currency).md) + healing + [Little Clover](Little_Clover.md) + random pickup between  [Gilded Clover](Gilded_Clover.md),  [Nduja Fritta Tanto](Nduja_Fritta_Tanto.md), or  [Rosary](Rosary.md).
- Gold: 1000 + Player Level x 3; 64 Gold Coins +  [Floor Chicken](Floor_Chicken.md) +  [Treasure Chest](Treasure_Chest.md) (cannot evolve, 3% chance to have 5 items, 33% to have 3)
- Demon: 1500 + Player Level x 4; 32 Gold Coins + Treasure Chest (all five slots can evolve, 3% chance to have 5 items, 33% to have 3)
- Cosmic: 2500 + Player Level x 5; 64 Gold Coins + Treasure Chest (same as above) + random weapon:
  -  [Greatest Jubilee](Greatest_Jubilee.md),  [Torrona\'s Box](Torrona's_Box.md), or  [Victory Sword](Victory_Sword.md); including  [Candybox](Candybox.md) if the player has less than 6 weapons; including anima weapon ( [Anima of Mortaccio](Anima_of_Mortaccio.md),  [Yatta Daikarin](Yatta_Daikarin.md),  [Carozza!](Carozza!.md),  [Profusione D\'Amore](Profusione_D'Amore.md)) if the player has less than 7 weapons and does not already have an anima weapon.

## Notes {#Notes}

- If a Gold Finger is a stage item (like in [Astral Stair](Astral_Stair.md) or in [Space 54](Space_54.md)), it cannot be attracted by the  [Mad Groove (VIII)](Mad_Groove_(VIII).md).
